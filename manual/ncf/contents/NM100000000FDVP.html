1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL SYSTEM  CONTROL  COMMON-RAIL SYSTEM  


SYSTEM CONTROL


a.

In this system, the high-pressurized fuel that is supplied by the supply pump is stored in the common-rail, and the ECM sends signals to the injectors via the injector driver in order to control the injection timing and injection volume.


b.

Fuel pressure is controlled in accordance with the fuel pumping volume that is controlled by the Suction Control Valve (SCV) in the supply pump assembly. The SCV is controlled by the ECM via the injector driver.

R151F MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE  MANUAL TRANSMISSION SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A R151F 5-speed manual transmission is used on models with a 1HZ engine.


b.

All shift mechanisms and each gear have been installed to the transmission intermediate plate, thereby achieving improved serviceability.


c.

The layout of the gears and bearings has been optimized. Due to this, load reduction and sufficient capacity for the bearing have been ensured.


d.

A reverse synchromesh mechanism is used for the reverse gear to enhance the ease of operation.

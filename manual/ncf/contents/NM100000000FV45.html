EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  BODY STRUCTURE  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

Safety Features

i.

A door impact beam, with a structure that suppresses cabin deformation during collisions, is used.



b.

Lightweight and Highly Rigid Body

i.

High-strength steel is used in order to achieve excellent body rigidity and a lightweight body.



c.

Rust-resistant Body

i.

Rust-resistant performance is enhanced by the extensive use of anti-corrosion sheet steel, as well as by an anti-corrosion treatment that includes the application of anti-rust wax and sealer to the easily corroded parts such as the hood and fender.



d.

Low Vibration and Low Noise Body

i.

Effective application of vibration damping and noise suppressant materials reduces engine, wind and road noise.




MAIN FEATURES


a.

Exterior Design Concept

i.

A very distinctive look and fascinating impression is conveyed through a dynamic and traditional design.



b.

Exterior Design

i.

Front View

1.

The front view is larger-than-life and continues the LAND CRUISER series tradition.

2.

The wide and heavy design expresses strength. Furthermore, an over fender is provided depending on the specification.

3.

A front bumper which protrudes forward is provided depending on the specification. Also, a winch is provided as an option for the front bumper.

4.

A large hood bulge is used in models with an intercooler to express high performance.

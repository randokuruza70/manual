OUTLINE


a.

The Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) driver*1 and front passenger*2 airbags are used as supplements to the seat belts to help to reduce the shocks to the head and chest of the driver or front passenger in the event of a frontal collision.

PRECAUTION


a.

Event Data Recorder

i.

There is a system to record data in a crash or near crash event. This is called an Event Data Recorder (EDR). The airbag sensor assembly contains the EDR.


ii.

In a crash or near crash event, this device may record some or all of the following information:

·

SRS airbag deployment data

·

SRS airbag system diagnostic data



iii.

The information above is intended to be used for the purpose of improving vehicle safety performance. Unlike general data recorders, the EDR does not record sound data such as conversation between passengers.


iv.

Toyota will not disclose the data recorded in an EDR to a third party except when:

·

An agreement from the vehicle's owner (or the leasing company for a leased vehicle) is obtained

·

Officially requested by the police or other authorities

·

Used as a defense for Toyota in a lawsuit

·

Ordered by the court



v.

However, if necessary, Toyota will:

·

Use the data for research on Toyota vehicle safety performance

·

Disclose the data to a third party for research purposes without disclosing details of the vehicle owner, and only when it is deemed necessary

·

Disclose summarized data cleared of vehicle identification information to a non-Toyota organization for research purposes

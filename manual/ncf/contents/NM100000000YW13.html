1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL SYSTEM  DETAILS  EXHAUST FUEL ADDITION INJECTOR  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

On the models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations, an exhaust fuel addition injector assembly is installed on cylinder head sub-assembly LH. This injector supplies additional fuel into the exhaust port and maintains the proper catalyst temperature for the purpose of PM recovery.


b.

An exhaust fuel addition injector assembly consists of a needle valve body, a needle valve, and a solenoid valve.

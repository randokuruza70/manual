CLUTCH  CLUTCH SYSTEM  DETAILS  CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A conventional type clutch master cylinder assembly is used to improve clutch disengagement performance.


b.

On RHD models with a 1GR-FE or 1HZ engine, a diaphragm type master cylinder accumulator assembly with an integrated type clutch master cylinder assembly is used. The master cylinder accumulator assembly absorbs pulses in the hydraulic pressure system to reduce pedal vibration and noise.


c.

A clevis damper is used to reduce pedal vibration and noise.

CONSTRUCTION


a.

Gear Layout

i.

The gear layout is a 4th gear direct coupling and 5th gear overdrive type, as in the constant mesh type.


ii.

The 5th gear has been positioned near the center between the manual transmission case support and transmission intermediate plate support. Due to this, the load placed on the output shaft and counter gear when using 5th gear is distributed to the left and right bearings, reducing the load for each bearing.

Bearing

i.

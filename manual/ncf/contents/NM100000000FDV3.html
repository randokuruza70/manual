a.

The ECM calculates the target injection pressure (25 - 157 MPa) based on the engine conditions that are the signals from the accelerator pedal sensor assembly and the crank position sensor. To control fuel pressure, signals sent to the Suction Control Valve (SCV) of the supply pump assembly regulate the pumping volume, so that the pressure detected by the fuel pressure sensor matches the target injection pressure.

1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  SFI SYSTEM  DETAILS  FUEL PUMP CONTROL  


OPERATION


a.

Fuel pump speed is controlled by the ECM, using the fuel pump relay assembly and the fuel pump resistor.


b.

When the ECM detects the airbag deployment signal from the airbag sensor assembly, the ECM will turn the circuit opening relay off. After the fuel cut control has been activated, turning the ignition switch from off to ON cancels the fuel cut control, and the engine can be restarted.

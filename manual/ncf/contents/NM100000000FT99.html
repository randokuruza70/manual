HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM  CONTROL  AIR CONDITIONING CONTROL  


FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS


Component Function
Heater Blower Switch Assembly Allows operation and adjustment of the air conditioning system*1 or heater system*2 via switches and levers.
Cooler Stabilizer Amplifier Assembly*1 Transmits and receives signals to and from the switches and sensors.
Cooler Compressor Assembly*1 A compact and lightweight 10-cylinder swash plate type cooler compressor is used.
Cooler Condenser Assembly*1 A multi-flow sub-cool condenser is used to improve heat exchange efficiency.
Heater Radiator Unit Sub-assembly*1, *2 A multi-flow type heater radiator is used for compactness and high performance.
Blower with Motor Sub-assembly The blower with motor sub-assembly is controlled by the blower switch on the heater blower switch assembly.
No. 1 Cooler Evaporator Sub-assembly*1 A multi-flow type evaporator is used for compactness.
No. 1 Cooler Thermistor*1 Detects the temperature of the cool air past the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly and transmits the data to the cooler stabilizer amplifier assembly.
Air Filter Prevents dust, etc., from entering the cabin.
ECM*3 Transmits the air conditioning off request signal to the cooler stabilizer amplifier assembly.

*1: Models with manual air conditioning system

*2: Models with manual heater system

*3: Models with 1GR-FE engine or 1VD-FTV engine

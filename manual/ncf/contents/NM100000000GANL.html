CONSTRUCTION


a.

Front Differential Lock Mechanism

i.

The right side differential case boss has been provided with splines which mesh with the sleeve, which has both inner and outer splines.


ii.

The sleeve slides the boss by way of the No. 2 differential lock shift actuator attached to the outside of the differential carrier. Also, the outer tip area of the side gear boss has been provided with splines which mesh with the inner splines of the sleeve.

b.

Rear Differential Lock Mechanism

i.

The splined area of the right side axle shaft has a sleeve.


ii.

The sleeve and the differential case have been provided with teeth to function as a dog clutch.


iii.

The sleeve meshes with the axle shaft by way of the splines, and slides over the axle shaft by way of the differential lock shift actuator attached to the differential carrier.

1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  SFI SYSTEM  CONTROL  FUEL PUMP CONTROL  


FUNCTION


a.

The fuel pump is controlled by the ECM, using the circuit opening relay, fuel pump relay assembly and the fuel pump resister.


b.

The fuel pump control has a fuel cut control. The fuel cut control stops the fuel pump when any of the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) airbags have deployed.*

*: Models with airbag system

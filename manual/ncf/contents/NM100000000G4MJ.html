FRONT SUSPENSION  FRONT SUSPENSION SYSTEM  DETAILS  SHOCK ABSORBER  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A low-pressure (N2) gas sealed type shock absorber is used to achieve both driving stability and riding comfort.


b.

A shock absorber is positioned between the front axle housing and the frame behind the front axle, and a stud type installation method is used at the both ends.

HINT:

To prevent hazardous conditions, make sure to empty the gas from the shock absorber before discarding the low-pressure (N2) gas sealed in the shock absorber. For details, refer to the Repair Manual.

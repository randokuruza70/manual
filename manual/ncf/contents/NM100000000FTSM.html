1GR-FE INTAKE / EXHAUST  INTAKE SYSTEM  DETAILS  THROTTLE BODY  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A linkless-type throttle body, in which the throttle position sensor and the throttle control motor are integrated, is used. It achieves excellent throttle control.


b.

A DC motor with excellent response and minimal power consumption is used for the throttle control motor. The ECM performs the duty cycle control of the direction and the amperage of the current that flows to the throttle control motor in order to regulate the opening angle of the throttle valve.

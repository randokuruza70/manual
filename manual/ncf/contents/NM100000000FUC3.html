b.

Rheostat Function

i.

A rheostat function is provided.


ii.

The brightness is changed via the rheostat adjustment display on the LCD. The rheostat adjustment display can be displayed when the TAIL relay is on.


iii.

When the ODO/TRIP switch is pressed and held while the rheostat adjustment display is shown on the LCD, the brightness changes one step every second. Releasing the ODO/TRIP switch when the desired brightness is reached sets the brightness at that level.

c.

Driver Seat Belt Warning Function

·

When the ignition switch is ON, this system detects the condition of the seat belts based on the signals from the driver seat belt buckle switch.

·

On models except models for G.C.C. countries, the buzzer sounds when the vehicle speed exceeds 20 km/h (12.4 mph) to emphasize the fact that the seat belt is unbuckled. After 30 seconds, the sound changes for emphasis. The sound continues for 90 seconds. Once a buzzer begins to sound, it will continue until the seat belt is buckled or the ignition switch is turned off.

·

On models for G.C.C. countries, when the seat belt is unbuckled, the buzzer sounds 5 times 1.8 seconds after the ignition switch is turned ON. The buzzer sounds when the vehicle speed exceeds 20 km/h (12.4 mph) to emphasize the fact that the seat belt is unbuckled. After 9.6 seconds, the sound changes for emphasis. The sound continues for 20 seconds. Once a buzzer begins to sound, it will continue until the seat belt is buckled or the ignition switch is turned off.

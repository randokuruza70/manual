Engine Side

Shift Lever Housing Movement Direction



ii.

When the shift lever is moved towards 5th and R, the shift lever housing rotates and pushes down the No. 2 reverse restrict pin.



iii.

When the shift lever is moved completely to 5th, the shift lever housing slides backward. This backward movement prevents the No. 2 reverse restrict pin from contacting the shift lever housing and allows the pin to move back to its initial position using the force of the spring.



iv.

When trying to move the shift lever from 5th directly to R, the shift lever housing contacts the No. 2 reverse restrict pin, preventing an erroneous shift operation into reverse.

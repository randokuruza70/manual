NETWORKING  CAN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

The Controller Area Network (CAN) is a serial data communication network for real time application. It is a multiplex communication network to be equipped in a vehicle, and has a communication speed of 500 kbps (HS-CAN) and the ability to detect malfunctions.


b.

The communication wire used is a twisted-pair wire. The bus line has a high line (2.5 V to 3.5 V of voltage is applied) and a low line (1.5 V to 2.5 V of voltage is applied).

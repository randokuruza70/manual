a.

A yawrate sensor and deceleration sensor are built into the airbag sensor assembly.


b.

The yawrate sensor detects capacitance changes caused by the vehicle's vertical axis rotation as a rotation angle speed.


c.

The deceleration sensor detects capacitance changes between the movable electrode and fixed electrode as the vehicle's deceleration.

CONSTRUCTION


a.

Models Compliant with EURO 4 Emission Regulations

i.

2 linear solenoid type EGR valve assemblies are used. Also, a dual-valve mechanism is used to cancel the effects of the exhaust pressure during actuation in order to achieve quick response.


ii.

An EGR valve position sensor is provided in the EGR valve assembly. This sensor enables EGR valve control at a higher level of precision by detecting the amount of lift of the EGR valve.

b.

Models Compliant with EURO 5 Emission Regulations

i.

A DC motor type electric EGR control valve assembly is used. The placement of the electric EGR control valve assembly at the intersection between the intake passage and EGR bypass facilitates the uniform distribution of EGR gas and intake air.


ii.

An EGR valve position sensor is provided in the electric EGR control valve assembly. This sensor enables EGR valve control at a higher level of precision by detecting the opening angle of the EGR valve.

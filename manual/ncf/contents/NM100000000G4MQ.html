REAR SUSPENSION  REAR SUSPENSION SYSTEM  DETAILS  STABILIZER BAR  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A rear stabilizer bar is provided depending on the model to achieve both rolling rigidity and operational stability.


b.

A 20 mm or 25 mm diameter solid type rear stabilizer bar with an optimized layout is used depending on the model.


c.

A rear stabilizer bush is positioned between the rear stabilizer bar and rear axle housing.


d.

A rubber bush is used between the rear stabilizer bar and stabilizer link, and a stud type installation method is used for the stabilizer link and frame.

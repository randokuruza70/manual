CONSTRUCTION


a.

Timing chain sub-assemblies use bush chains with a pitch of 9.525 mm (0.375 in.).


b.

The timing chain sub-assembly is lubricated by an oil jet.


c.

No. 1 chain tensioner assemblies use a spring and oil pressure to maintain proper chain tension at all times. They suppress noise generated by the timing chain sub-assemblies.


d.

The No. 1 chain tensioner assembly is the ratchet type with a non-return mechanism.

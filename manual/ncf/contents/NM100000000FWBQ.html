H150F MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE  MANUAL TRANSMISSION SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

An H150F 5-speed manual transmission is used on models with a 1GR-FE or 1VD-FTV engine.


b.

The layout of the gears and bearings has been optimized. Due to this, load reduction and sufficient capacity for the bearing has been ensured.


c.

An oil pump that can perform forced lubrication is used. Due to this, a sufficient amount of lubrication is ensured from low to high speeds and strength and durability have been ensured.


d.

A closed gear ratio has been used to improve acceleration performance and usability.


e.

Along with a constant mesh type synchromesh mechanism, a helical gear is used for the reverse gear. Due to this, gear noise while driving in reverse has been reduced.


f.

All shift mechanisms and each gear have been installed to the transmission intermediate plate, thereby achieving improved serviceability.

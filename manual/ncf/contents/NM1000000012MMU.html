1VD-FTV INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

Because a single turbocharger sub-assembly is used on a V type engine, a crossover pipe is used to merge the exhaust gas from the right bank to the left bank.


b.

A stainless steel exhaust pipe is used for weight reduction and improved rust resistance.


c.

An EGR control is used. This control is designed to reduce and control NOx formation through a slight reduction of peak temperature in the engine combustion chamber, which is accomplished by introducing a small amount of inert gas into the intake manifold.*1

For details of EGR control, see the EMISSION CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.


d.

3 oxidation catalysts are used.*2


e.

An oxidation catalyst and a Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) catalyst are used.*3


f.

A catalyst support control is used. The ECM controls the exhaust fuel addition injector assembly to purify the Particulate Matter (PM) that is captured in the filter with catalyst.*3

For details of EGR control, see the EMISSION CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.

*1: Models compliant with EURO 4 emission regulations and models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations

*2: Models compliant with EURO 4 emission regulations

*3: Models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations

CONSTRUCTION


a.

The transfer case is made of aluminum to reduce weight. Also, the ribs are optimally positioned to reduce noise.


b.

A liquid gasket is used on each contact surface of the case to improve seal performance.


c.

A sealed ball bearing is used for the transfer input shaft bearing to ensure durability.


d.

A taper roller bearing is used for the transfer rear output shaft bearing to ensure support rigidity and durability.


e.

A high-tooth gear is used for each gear. Gear noise has been reduced by ensuring a maximum contact ratio for each gear.

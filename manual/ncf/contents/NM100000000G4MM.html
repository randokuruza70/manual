b.

A rubber hollow spring is positioned above the coil spring on the same axis to achieve non-linear spring characteristics when bottoming.


c.

The hollow spring deforms when first struck to absorb impacts, and then hardens by using rubber which compresses in the middle of a stroke. These spring characteristics ensure the suspension stroke volume and reduce the feeling of upward thrusting.

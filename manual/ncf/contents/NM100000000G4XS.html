POWER ASSIST SYSTEMS  POWER STEERING SYSTEM  DETAILS  VANE UP  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A compact and lightweight vane pump with built-in flow control valve, which uses die-cast aluminum as the material for its housing, is used. By optimizing its internal construction, this vane pump has achieved a low-friction operation, which leads to low fuel consumption.


b.

A belt drive type vane pump is used on models with 1GR-FE engine, and a gear drive type vane pump is used on models with 1HZ or 1VD-FTV engine.


c.

For the vane pump assembly and separate vane pump oil reservoir assembly, a cap has been integrated with the gauge to check the oil level in consideration of serviceability. Also, on models with 1GR-FE or 1VD-FTV engines, a partially transparent resin vane pump oil reservoir assembly is used to achieve weight reduction and improve fluid level visibility.

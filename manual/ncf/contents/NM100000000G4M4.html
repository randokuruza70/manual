FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS


Component Function
Brake Actuator Assembly Solenoid Valves Change the fluid path based on the signals from the skid control ECU during the operation of the ABS in order to control the fluid pressure applied to the wheel cylinders.
Pump Motor Drives the pumps inside the brake actuator.
Solenoid Relay (Built into Skid Control ECU) Supplies power to the solenoid valves.
Motor Relay (Built into Skid Control ECU) Supplies power to the pump motor.
Skid Control ECU Judges the vehicle driving condition based on the signals from each sensor and switch, and sends the brake control signals to the brake actuator.
Speed Sensors Detect the wheel speed of each of the 4 wheels.
Deceleration Sensor Detects the vehicle's longitudinal acceleration and deceleration.
Stop Light Switch Assembly Detects the brake pedal depression condition.
Parking Brake Switch Assembly Detects the parking brake lever status.
Brake Fluid Level Warning Switch Detects the brake fluid level.
Vacuum Warning Switch Assembly*1 Detects the negative pressure inside the vacuum reservoir assembly.
L4 Detection Switch (No. 1 Transfer Indicator Switch) Detects the L4 position condition.
4WD Detection Switch (No. 1 Transfer Indicator Switch) Detects the 4WD position condition.
Rear Differential Lock Position Switch (No. 4 Transfer Indicator Switch)*2 Detects the rear differential lock actuator operating condition.
Combination Meter Assembly Brake Warning Light
·

Illuminates to alert the driver when the brake fluid level is low.

·

Illuminates to inform the driver when the parking brake lever is pulled up

·

Illuminates to alert the driver when the vacuum drops inside the vacuum tank.*1


ABS Warning Light
·

Illuminates to alert the driver when the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the ABS.

·

Illuminates to alert the driver when ABS function is not operating while the rear differential lock system is switching to lock.*2


Buzzer Emits a warning sound to alert the driver when the parking brake lever is pulled up.

*1: Models with diesel engine

*2: Models with differential lock system



SYSTEM CONTROL


Electronic Control of Brake Control System
Control Outline
Anti-lock Brake System (ABS) The ABS helps prevent the wheels from locking when the brakes are applied firmly or when braking on a slippery surface.

a.

The skid control ECU operates the pump motor and the solenoid valves inside the brake actuator assembly and controls the hydraulic pressure of each wheel based on signals from the sensors to ensure vehicle stability when braking.


b.

The system includes initial check, fail-safe and diagnosis functions in case of a malfunction.



FAIL-SAFE


a.

If a malfunction occurs in the signal system of the ABS system, the skid control ECU disables the ABS control.



DIAGNOSIS


a.

If the skid control ECU detects a malfunction in the brake control system, the ABS warning light illuminates to alert the driver.


b.

At the same time, the Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs) are stored in memory.


c.

This system has a sensor signal check (test mode) function.


d.

For details of DTCs and check function, refer to the Repair Manual.

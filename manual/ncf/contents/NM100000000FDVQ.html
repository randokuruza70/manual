1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL SYSTEM  CONTROL  SUB FUEL TANK SYSTEM  


SYSTEM CONTROL


a.

On the models with a sub fuel tank (fuel tank assembly), a solenoid valve switches the fuel passage between the main fuel tank (fuel tank assembly) and the sub fuel tank (fuel tank assembly).


b.

Turning the fuel tank changeover switch (fuel control switch assembly) on operates the fuel tank solenoid main valve assembly and the fuel tank solenoid return valve assembly and switches the passage to the sub fuel tank (fuel tank assembly).

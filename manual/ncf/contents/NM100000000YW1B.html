1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM  DETAILS  EGR VALVE AND EGR COOLER  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

Models Compliant with EURO 5 Emission Regulations

i.

A water-cooled type EGR cooler assembly is used. In the water-cooled type EGR cooler, engine coolant flows through the 6-layered gas passage to cool down the EGR gas.


ii.

An EGR cooler bypass switching valve (No. 2 EGR valve assembly) is used. If EGR gas is cooled down in the EGR cooler with light engine load and low engine coolant temperature, compression air temperature decreases. To prevent this, the EGR gas passage is switched by the EGR cooler bypass switching valve (No. 2 EGR valve assembly). The EGR passage is switched in 2 stages.

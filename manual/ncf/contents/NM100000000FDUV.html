CONSTRUCTION


a.

The throttle position sensor is mounted on the throttle body assembly to detect the opening angle of the throttle valve. The throttle position sensor converts the magnetic flux density that changes when the magnetic yoke (located on the same axis as that of the throttle valve shaft) rotates around the Hall IC into electric signals to operate the throttle control motor.

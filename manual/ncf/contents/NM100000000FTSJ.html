1GR-FE INTAKE / EXHAUST  INTAKE SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

The linkless-type throttle body is used to achieve excellent throttle control.


b.

A cyclone pre-cleaner has been provided in the air cleaner inlet.*

*: Models without snorkel



c.

A plastic intake air surge tank is used.


d.

The Electronic Throttle Control System-intelligent (ETCS-i) is used to ensure excellent throttle control in all operating ranges. For details, see 1GR-FE Engine Control.


e.

The Acoustic Control Induction System (ACIS) is used to improve the engine performance in all speed ranges.

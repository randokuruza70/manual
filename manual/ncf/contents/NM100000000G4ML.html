FRONT SUSPENSION  FRONT SUSPENSION SYSTEM  DETAILS  STABILIZER BAR  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A front stabilizer bar is used to achieve both rolling rigidity and operational stability.


b.

A No. 1 front stabilizer bar bush is positioned at the front stabilizer link installation area.


c.

A ball joint is used between the stabilizer link and the frame. As a result, the ball joint performs effectively even for slight rolling and maintains a stable roll feeling.


d.

A rubber bush is used between the ends of the front stabilizer bar and front axle housing.

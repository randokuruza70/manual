1VD-FTV LUBRICATION  LUBRICATION SYSTEM  DETAILS  SCAVENGE OIL PUMP  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A scavenging pump assembly is used to suppress the accumulation of oil in the turbocharger while driving on a slope.


b.

The scavenging pump assembly, which is located at the back end of the cylinder block, is driven by the crankshaft via gears.


c.

After the turbocharger sub-assembly discharges the oil, the scavenging pump assembly returns the oil from the oil catch tank through forced suction and discharges it to the oil pan.

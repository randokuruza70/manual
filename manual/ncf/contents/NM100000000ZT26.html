BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  BRAKE CONTROL SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

The following brake control functions are provided depending on the model: Anti-lock Brake System (ABS), Electronic Brake force Distribution (EBD), brake assist, Traction Control (TRC), Active Traction Control (A-TRC), Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) and hill-start assist control.


b.

A VSC OFF switch is provided for models with VSC.


c.

An emergency brake signal system is provided depending on the models. This system that the hazard warning lights flash automatically in the case of emergency braking to alert following drivers, helping to reduce rear-end accidents.



PRECAUTION


a.

When the brake control system is activated, the brake pedal may shudder, which is a normal occurrence of the system in operation and should not be considered a malfunction.


b.

The TRC and VSC functions control brake hydraulic pressure and engine output to ensure vehicle stability. Therefore, operate the VSC OFF switch (stop the operation of the TRC and VSC functions) only when it is necessary.


c.

When the differential lock is in operation, the skid control ECU stops the operation of the ABS, TRC and VSC.

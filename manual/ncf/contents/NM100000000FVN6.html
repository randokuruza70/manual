OUTLINE


a.

A pressure feeding lubrication system is used to supply oil to the moving parts of the engine.


b.

A trochoid type oil pump is used.


c.

A water-cooled type oil cooler is used.


d.

Piston oil jets that lubricate and cool the pistons are provided.

1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A common-rail system is used in the fuel injection system.


b.

An HP4 type supply pump assembly, which is compact and lightweight, is used.


c.

An injector compensation value and a QR code are printed on the injector assembly to achieve high-precision control.


d.

A fuel filter warning switch, which turns ON/OFF when the internal vacuum of the fuel filter increases, is provided in the fuel filter.


e.

2 fuel coolers are used in the fuel return path.*1


f.

A fuel pressure discharge valve is used.*2


g.

8 solenoid type injector assemblies are used.*1


h.

8 piezo type injector assemblies are used.*2


i.

A exhaust fuel addition injector assembly is used.*2


j.

A fuel feeding pipe for the exhaust fuel addition injector assembly is provided on the supply pump assembly.*2

*1: Except models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations

*2: Models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations

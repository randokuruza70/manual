1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  DETAILS  MASS AIR FLOW METER  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The intake mass air flow meter sub-assembly of the diesel engine is used for the precise fuel injection volume control and EGR control to achieve clean emissions.


b.

The slotted drop-in type air flow meter is used, and this allows a portion of the intake air to flow through the detection area. By directly measuring the mass and the flow rate of the intake air, the detection precision is ensured and the intake air resistance is reduced.


c.

The intake mass air flow meter sub-assembly has a built-in atmospheric temperature sensor.

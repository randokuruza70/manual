1GR-FE ENGINE MECHANICAL  ENGINE UNIT  DETAILS  CYLINDER BLOCK  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The cylinder block is made of aluminum alloy.


b.

The cylinder block has a bank angle of 60°, a bank offset of 36.6 mm (1.441 in.) and a bore pitch of 105.5 mm (4.15 in.), resulting in a compact block in its length and width even for its displacement.


c.

Spiny-type liners are used.


d.

The installation bosses of the two knock control sensors are located on the inner side of the left and right banks.

1GR-FE INTAKE / EXHAUST  INTAKE SYSTEM  DETAILS  AIR CLEANER  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A nonwoven, full-fabric type air cleaner element is used.


b.

A resonator has been provided in the air cleaner hose to reduce the amount of intake air sound.


c.

A cyclone pre-cleaner has been provided in the air cleaner case*1 or the snorkel*2. This cyclone pre-cleaner has a screw shape and gathers large-sized dust particles in a dust cup by swirling the inlet air. The dust cup is removable, so the dust gathered can be disposed of.

HINT:

*1: Models without snorkel

*2: Models with snorkel

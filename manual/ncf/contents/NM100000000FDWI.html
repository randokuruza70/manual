1VD-FTV LUBRICATION  LUBRICATION SYSTEM  DETAILS  PISTON OIL JET  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

Piston oil jets (No. 1 oil nozzle sub-assemblies) are provided at the bottom of the cylinder block to spray oil to the piston's cooling channel, thus further cooling and lubricating the pistons.


b.

These oil jets (No. 1 oil nozzle sub-assemblies) contain a check valve to prevent oil from being fed when the oil pressure is low. This prevents the overall oil pressure in the engine from dropping.

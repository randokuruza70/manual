LIGHTING (EXT)  LIGHTING SYSTEM  CONTROL  MANUAL HEADLIGHT BEAM LEVEL CONTROL  


FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS


a.

Manual Headlight Beam Level Control System

Component Function
Headlight Leveling Switch The level of the headlight can be adjusted between 0 and 5, and a switch level status signal is sent to the actuator.
Headlight Assembly Headlight Leveling Motor Motors in accordance with signals from the headlight leveling switch and moves the reflector up and down.

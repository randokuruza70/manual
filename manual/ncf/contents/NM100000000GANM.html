OPERATION


a.

No. 2 Differential Lock Shift Actuator

i.

The front differential lock shift motor rotates by signals from the No. 2 4WD control ECU. This rotation is transferred in order to operate the drive gear, driven gear, spiral spring for the wait mechanism, final gear and differential lock shift fork. Thus the differential lock shift fork slides, changing the operation of the front differential between locked and free.

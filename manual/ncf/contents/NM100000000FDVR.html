OPERATION


a.

Fuel Filter Warning Switch

i.

A fuel filter warning switch, which turns ON/OFF when the internal vacuum of the filter increases, is provided in the fuel filter. This switch, which turns OFF when the internal vacuum of the fuel filter increases to a predetermined level, is connected by wire to the ECM.


ii.

When the ECM detects that the internal vacuum of the fuel filter has increased (by way of the fuel filter warning switch OFF signal), it determines that the fuel filter has become clogged. Then, it illuminates the fuel system warning light on the combination meter to urge the driver to replace the fuel filter.

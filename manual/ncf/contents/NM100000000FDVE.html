CONSTRUCTION


a.

Lightweight compact graphite iron is used for the cylinder block sub-assembly.


b.

The cylinder block sub-assembly has a bank angle of 90°, a bank offset of 22 mm (0.866 in.) and a bore pitch of 97 mm (3.82 in.), resulting in a compact block in its length and width even for its displacement.

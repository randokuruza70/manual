1VD-FTV LUBRICATION  LUBRICATION SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

The lubrication circuit is fully pressurized and all oil passes through an oil cooler and oil filter.


b.

A trochoid type oil pump driven by a gear engaged with the crankshaft is used.


c.

A water-cooled type oil cooler is installed on the oil pan sub-assembly.


d.

Piston oil jets (No. 1 Oil Nozzle Sub-assemblies) that lubricate and cool the pistons are provided.

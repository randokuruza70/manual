REAR SUSPENSION  REAR SUSPENSION SYSTEM  DETAILS  LEAF SPRING  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

Highly responsive materials which have strong durability are used to reduce the number of springs and achieve weight reduction. In addition, spring characteristics are optimized to ensure excellent riding comfort.


b.

A berlin eye, which is manufactured to bend down once at the spring end before bending up, is used for the front eye shape of the leaf spring to create understeer for the axle steer.


c.

The thickness of the fastening part of the leaf is optimized and a spacer is used at the end of the leaf to ensure superior durability.


d.

The frame side installation width of the rear spring shackle is enlarged to ensure horizontal rigidity.

BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  BRAKE CONTROL SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A brake control system [Anti-lock Brake System (ABS)] is provided depending on the model.


b.

The skid control ECU integrated into the brake actuator assembly processes signals from the sensors and switches to control the brakes.



PRECAUTION


a.

When the system is activated, the brake pedal could shudder. This is a normal occurrence of the system in operation and should not be considered a malfunction.

REFERENCE

When the rear differential lock is in operation, the skid control ECU stops the operation of the ABS and turns on the ABS warning light.

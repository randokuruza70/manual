1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM  CONTROL  EGR CONTROL  


FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS


a.

The main components of the EGR control system are as follows:

Components Function
ECM Controls the EGR control based on signals from each sensor.
EGR Valve Assembly EGR Valve Actuated by the ECM, this valve controls the flow rate of EGR gas.
EGR Valve Position Sensor Detects the EGR valve position.
EGR Cooler Assembly Cools EGR gas.
Accelerator Pedal Sensor Assembly Detects the amount of pedal effort applied to the accelerator pedal.
For details, see the ENGINE CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.(201408,999999,_51,_029314,_0133904,DE,NM100000000FDUW)
Crank Position Sensor Detects the engine speed.
For details, see the ENGINE CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.(201408,999999,_51,_029314,_0133904,DE,NM100000000FDUU)
Intake Mass Air Flow Meter Sub-assembly Air Flow Meter Detects the intake air mass and flow rate.
For details, see the ENGINE CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.(201408,201608,_51,_029314,_0133904,DE,NM100000000FDUZ)
Atmospheric Temperature Sensor Detects the atmospheric temperature.
For details, see the ENGINE CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.(201408,201608,_51,_029314,_0133904,DE,NM100000000FDUZ)
Engine Coolant Temperature Sensor Detects the engine coolant temperature.
Turbo Pressure Sensor Detects the intake manifold pressure.
For details, see the ENGINE CONTROL (1VD-FTV) section.(201408,999999,_51,_029314,_0133904,DE,NM100000000FDUX)
Throttle Control Motor Actuated by the ECM, this valve controls the flow rate of EGR gas.



FUNCTION


a.

This system is designed to reduce and control NOx formation due to a slight reduction of peak temperature in the engine combustion chamber, which is accomplished by introducing a small amount of inert gas into intake manifold.


b.

By sensing the engine driving conditions, the ECM operates the EGR valve assembly and throttle control motor, and regulates the amount of exhaust gas.

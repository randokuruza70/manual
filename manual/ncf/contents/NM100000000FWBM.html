H150F MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE  MANUAL TRANSMISSION SYSTEM  DETAILS  OIL PUMP  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A trochoid gear type oil pump that forcibly lubricates the bearings and synchromesh mechanism through forced lubrication is used. Under various usage conditions, a sufficient amount of lubrication is ensured, improving durability.


b.

During lubrication, the oil slung up from each gear is transferred to inside the transfer adapter via the large-sized oil receiver installed to the manual transmission case. This oil is sucked up from the strainer via the oil pump and sent to the hole of the output shaft. The pumped oil is discharged from the inside of the output shaft to each shaft gear hole in order to lubricate the cone side for the synchromesh mechanism and bearing and oil seals.

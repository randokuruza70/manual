OUTLINE


a.

The 1VD-FTV TOYOTA Direct injection 4 stroke common-rail Diesel engine (D-4D) is a V8 4.5 liter, 32-valve DOHC, turbocharged diesel engine with an intercooler. This engine uses the direct injection system and common-rail system to achieve high performance, clean emissions, low noise and low vibration.

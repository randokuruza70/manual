POWER ASSIST SYSTEMS  POWER STEERING SYSTEM  DETAILS  STEERING GEAR  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

An integral type is used for the recirculating ball type steering gear.


b.

The power piston diameter and sector shaft diameter have been optimized to increase assist power and to improve responsiveness around the center position of the steering wheel. Also, the steering gear ratio has been optimized, improving driving straight ahead and the feeling of directness.


c.

A rotary type control valve is used to improve steering feeling.


d.

A preload auxiliary mechanism, in which the power piston is pressed using the force of the spring, is used, and the change in preload over time while driving straight ahead is decreased, maintaining the proper steering wheel play and improving steering feeling when driving at high speeds.

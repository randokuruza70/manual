WINCH  WINCH SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A winch is provided for getting the vehicle out when it is stuck (at a standstill), when ascending or descending on steep roads, or when moving a load.


b.

A winch control switch assembly is provided. The winch control switch assembly can be used by connecting its plug to the winch assembly.



SPECIFICATIONS


Item Specification
Maximum Pulling Power 35.2 KN (3520 kgf)
Normal Pulling Power 9.8 KN (980 kgf)
Length of Wire Rope 34 m
Maximum Winding Speed 16 m/min


PRECAUTION


a.

Using the winch incorrectly may result in serious injury. Therefore, make sure to operate the winch only after fully understanding the correct operation.


b.

The winch should not be used for raising objects or people, or transferring people. Using the winch in this way may cause a serious accident to occur or result in serious injury.


c.

When not operating the winch or when contacting the winch assembly or winch by hand, remove the winch control switch assembly from the winch assembly. If the winch control switch assembly is left attached and the switch is turned on suddenly, the winch assembly may involuntarily move, resulting in serious injury.


d.

While the winch is being operated or the winch control switch assembly is installed to the winch assembly, individuals other than the person operating the winch should stay clear of the winch control switch assembly, wire rope and hook. The wire rope involuntarily moving or the hook slipping out of place may result in serious injury. Also, do not place hands, etc. around the winch assembly or wire guide. There is the possibility that a person may get caught, resulting in serious injury.


e.

Even if the winch is not being operated, do not touch the wire rope if the wire rope is pulled taut. If the wire rope breaks or the hook slips out of place, this may result serious injury.


f.

Do not cause the vehicle to move or shake while operating the winch. The hook may slip out of place or a great load may be placed on the wire rope, causing it to break, which may result in serious injury.


g.

When one person is operating the winch or if securing the vehicle before use, make sure to apply the parking brake and chock the wheels. Not doing this may cause the vehicle to move, resulting in a serious accident.


h.

When using the wire rope, use thick gloves (leather gloves are recommended) in order to prevent injury. Serious injury may result if the wire rope slips while being handled by bare hands.

Shift Lever Housing Movement Direction



ii.

If the shift lever is moved towards 5th and R, the shift lever housing rotates, pushing in the spring to push the reverse restrict pin.



iii.

If the shift lever is moved completely to 5th, the shift lever housing slides backward. Due to this, the No. 1 reverse restrict pin is detached from the shift lever housing and returns to the original position using the force of the spring.



iv.

When trying to move the shift lever from 5th directly to R, the shift lever housing contacts the cutout of the No. 1 reverse restrict pin, preventing an erroneous shift operation into reverse.

BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

The brake booster, brake master cylinder and pedal ratio are optimized to improve brake feel.


b.

A Load Sensing Proportioning & Bypass Valve (LSP & BV) is used to optimize the front and rear braking force distribution according to changes in the load capacity.*

*: Models for General Countries



c.

On models with a 1HZ or 1VD-FTV engine, a vacuum pump and vacuum tank are used to ensure the necessary vacuum for brake booster operation. Also, a vacuum warning switch, which detects the vacuum, is provided for the vacuum tank.

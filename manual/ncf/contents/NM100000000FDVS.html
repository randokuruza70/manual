1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL SYSTEM  DETAILS  SUPPLY PUMP  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The supply pump assembly consists of an inner cam (eccentric cam), outer cam (polygon ring), 3 plungers, Suction Control Valve (SCV), and feed pump. Each plunger is placed outside of the outer cam.

i.

Feed pump pumps fuel to 3 plungers.


ii.

SCV controls volume of fuel drawn into plungers.


iii.

Inner cam drives outer cam.


iv.

Outer cam drives 3 plungers.


v.

Plunger pressurizes fuel.

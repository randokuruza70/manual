HF1A TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD  TRANSFER SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A part-time 2-speed HF1A transfer is used on models with a 1GR-FE or 1VD-FTV engine.


b.

Drive mode switching uses a direct shift type, which switches the drive mode according to transfer shift lever operation.



SPECIFICATION


Drive Type Part-time
Transfer Gear Ratio High 1.000
Low 2.488
Reduction Gear Type Idler Gear
Oil Capacity 1.5 Liters (1.59 US qts, 1.32 Imp. qts)
Oil Viscosity SAE 75W-90
Oil Grade API GL-4 or GL-5

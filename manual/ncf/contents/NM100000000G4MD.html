BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE SYSTEM  DETAILS  BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER AND RESERVOIR  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

A tandem Lockheed type brake master cylinder is used on models without ABS, and a portless and Lockheed type brake master cylinder is used on models with ABS.


b.

A brake master cylinder reservoir with integrated brake master cylinder is provided to save space. Also, a brake fluid level warning switch, which detects the amount of brake fluid, is provided. When the fluid level drops, the switch turns on to illuminate the brake warning light in the combination meter assembly.

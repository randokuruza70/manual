BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  BRAKE CONTROL SYSTEM  DETAILS  VSC OFF SWITCH  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The operation of the VSC and TRC functions can be stopped by the VSC OFF switch. While the vehicle is being driven off the shoulder of the road or driven on a dirt road, engine output control is stopped to maintain drive torque.

OPERATION


a.

The VSC OFF switch can select 3 modes (Normal mode, TRC OFF mode, and VSC OFF mode).


b.

Briefly pressing the VSC OFF switch in Normal mode selects TRC OFF mode.


c.

Pressing and holding the VSC OFF switch for 3 seconds or more with the vehicle stopped selects VSC OFF mode, disabling the TRC and VSC functions.


d.

Briefly pressing the VSC OFF switch in TRC OFF mode or VSC OFF mode, or turning the engine switch off, returns to Normal mode. Also, when the speed increases, TRC OFF mode automatically switches to Normal mode.

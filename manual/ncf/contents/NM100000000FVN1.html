SEAT  SEAT ASSEMBLY  DETAILS  FRONT SEAT  


OPERATION


a.

Rotational walk-in mechanism is provided for the front passenger seat of the 2-door long steel roof van models*. This mechanism is used in consideration for passengers getting into and out of the rear No. 1 seat. The seat back will collapse forward and the seat cushion will slide diagonally forward by operating the outer side lever when getting into the vehicle. Same set of movement will occur by operating the inner side lever when getting out of the vehicle.

*: Models for General Countries

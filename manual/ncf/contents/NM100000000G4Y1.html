STEERING COLUMN  STEERING COLUMN SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A steering column with a manual tilt and telescopic mechanism is used, thus enabling the steering to be set to the desired position.


b.

An energy absorbing mechanism and steering intermediate shaft contraction mechanism with a corrugated shaft section are used to ensure driver safety in the event of a collision.

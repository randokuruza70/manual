1HZ EMISSION CONTROL  EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

On the models compliant with EURO 2 emission regulations, an emission control system is used. This system is controlled by the emission control ECU.


b.

The emission control system has the following systems:

System Outline
Pre-heating Control (Glow Plug Control) Controls the length of time when the current is applied to the glow plug assemblies in accordance with the engine coolant temperature.
Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Control Controls the EGR volume via the EGR valve in accordance with the engine conditions.
Boost Altitude Compensation System (BACS) Control Controls the vacuum that is applied to the BACS in accordance with the engine conditions, thus controlling the maximum injection volume and restraining the discharge of black smoke.
Timing Control Valve (TCV) Control Controls the fuel injection timing in accordance with the engine conditions.
Diagnosis When the emission control ECU detects a malfunction, the emission control ECU diagnoses and memorizes the failed section.
Fail-safe When the emission control ECU detects a malfunction, the emission control ECU stops or controls the EGR control, fuel injection volume control, and fuel injection timing control according to the data already stored in the memory.

1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  ENGINE UNIT  DETAILS  CYLINDER HEAD  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The cylinder head sub-assembly is made of aluminum alloy.


b.

The injector assembly has been located in the center of the combustion chamber in order to improve engine performance and clean emissions.


c.

The passage for the EGR is provided in the cylinder head sub-assembly. By cooling the exhaust gas, a great amount of exhaust gas can recirculate.


d.

A vertical 2-stage construction is used for the water jacket to improve cooling performance.


e.

A glow plug assembly is placed between the intake ports of each cylinder to ensure startability.

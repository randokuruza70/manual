PRECAUTION


a.

Free Wheel Hub

i.

Before driving in 4WD (H4 and L4), always set the left and right hubs to the LOCK position. When the drive mode is switched from H2 to H4 or L4 with the hubs free, a strange noise will occur and a malfunction may occur.



b.

Dual Mode Automatic Locking Hub

i.

When switching the hub position, always switch the left and right hubs to the same position. Setting the left and right hubs to different positions may cause a malfunction to occur, which may negatively affect driving and lead to an unforeseen accident.

1HZ ENGINE MECHANICAL  ENGINE UNIT  DETAILS  VALVE MECHANISM  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

An OHC that is driven by a belt and gears is used for the valve drive mechanism.


b.

Each cylinder has an intake valve and an exhaust valve.


c.

The valves are directly opened and closed by the camshaft.


d.

The camshaft is driven by a timing belt.


e.

A hydraulic type No. 1 timing belt tensioner assembly is used to maintain proper timing belt tension at all times.

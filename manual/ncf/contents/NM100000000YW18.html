1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM  CONTROL  EGR CONTROL  


SYSTEM CONTROL


a.

Models compliant with EURO 4 emission regulations

i.

By sensing the engine driving conditions and actual amount of EGR valve opening, the ECM actuates the EGR valve assemblies. In addition, the ECM controls the diesel throttle valve opening position using the throttle control motor. As a result, the amount of recirculating exhaust gas is regulated in accordance with the engine conditions.



b.

Models compliant with EURO 5 emission regulations

i.

By sensing the engine driving conditions and actual amount of EGR valve opening, the ECM actuates the electric EGR control valve assembly. In addition, the ECM controls the diesel throttle valve opening position using the throttle control motor. As a result, the amount of recirculating exhaust gas is regulated in accordance with the engine conditions.


ii.

The ECM switches the bypass passage of the EGR cooler bypass switching valve (No. 2 EGR valve assembly) via the No. 1 vacuum switching valve assembly (for EGR cooler bypass switching valve) in 2 stages in order to optimize the temperature of the EGR gas and clean exhaust gases.

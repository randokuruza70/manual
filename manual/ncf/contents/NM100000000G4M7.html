i.

Based on the signals received from the 4 speed sensors, the skid control ECU calculates the speed of each wheel and deceleration, and checks the wheel slipping condition. In accordance with to the slipping condition, the skid control ECU controls the pressure holding valve and pressure reduction valve in order to adjust the fluid pressure of each wheel cylinder in the following 3 modes: pressure reduction, pressure holding, and pressure increase modes.

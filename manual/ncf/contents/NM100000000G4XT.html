POWER ASSIST SYSTEMS  POWER STEERING SYSTEM  DETAILS  STEERING LINK AGE  


CONSTRUCTION


a.

The steering linkage consists of the relay rod assembly and tie rod assembly and simplification of the structure has improved steering feeling. Also, the steering damper assembly has been positioned between the relay rod assembly and frame to reduce steering flutter.


b.

The tie rod assembly has been positioned behind the front axle, reducing interference with the road during driving on rough roads and protecting the tie rod assembly.


c.

An oil maintenance-free type ball joint is used on the tie rod assembly, improving serviceability.

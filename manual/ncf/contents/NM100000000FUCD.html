POWER OUTLETS (INT)  POWER OUTLET SOCKET SYSTEM  GENERAL  GENERAL  


OUTLINE


a.

A cigarette lighter assembly is provided.



SPECIFICATIONS


Cigarette Lighter Assembly
Item Specification
Rated Voltage [V] DC12*1, DC24*2

HINT:

*1: When the vehicle voltage is 12 V.

*2: When the vehicle voltage is 24 V.



PRECAUTION


a.

When the ignition switch is turned to ON or ACC, the cigarette lighter assembly can be used.


b.

Do not touch the metal part of the cigarette lighter assembly. Doing so may result in burns.


c.

Take the following precautions to prevent damage to the cigarette lighter assembly and burns to the surrounding area.

·

Do not use the cigarette lighter assembly while pressed.

·

Do not insert a cigarette lighter assembly manufactured by another company.

·

Only use a Toyota genuine power supply.

BRAKE (REAR)  REAR BRAKE FLEXIBLE HOSE(for Rear Drum Brake)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN BRAKE FLUID

NOTICE:

Wash off brake fluid immediately if it comes in contact with any painted surface.


47319F 2.REMOVE REAR FLEXIBLE HOSE LH

a.

Disconnect the brake tube and 3-way from the flexible hose with a union nut wrench while holding the flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:
·

Do not bend or damage the brake tube.

·

Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt and dust to enter the brake tube and 3-way from the connecting points.




b.

Remove the clip and flexible hose.

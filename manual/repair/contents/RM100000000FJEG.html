1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  ACCELERATOR PEDAL  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

78110K 1.REMOVE ACCELERATOR PEDAL SENSOR ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

This accelerator pedal does not require lubrication.

Do not apply oil or other lubrication to the accelerator pedal sensor assembly.

If applied, the accelerator pedal sensor assembly must be replaced.


a.

Disconnect the accelerator pedal sensor connector.


b.

Remove the 2 nuts and accelerator pedal sensor assembly.

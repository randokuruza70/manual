SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS  CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

62180A 1.INSTALL CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Check that the ignition switch is off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.

CAUTION:
·

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

·

If the airbag deploys for any reason, it may cause a serious accident.




c.

Attach the wire harness clamp.


d.

Attach the new clip and connect the vehicle rear side deployment portion of the curtain shield airbag assembly LH.


e.

Attach the new clip and connect the vehicle front side deployment portion of the curtain shield airbag assembly LH.


f.

Attach the 3 hooks.


g.

Temporarily install the 5 new bolts.


h.

Check that the deployment portion of the curtain shield airbag assembly LH is not twisted.


i.

Tighten the 5 bolts in the order shown in the illustration and install the curtain shield airbag assembly LH.

Torque:
11 N*m (112 kgf*cm, 8 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

Do not install the strap if it has been twisted 3 times or more.

·

Install the strap by passing it through the bottom of the assist grip bracket.

·

Do not install the strap if it is caught on the assist grip bracket.

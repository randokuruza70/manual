Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the seat frame may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the outer rear seat lower front track bracket or rear seatback lock cover due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.

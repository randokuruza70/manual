BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER(w/o ABS)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Make sure foreign matter does not attach to the master cylinder's piston. If foreign matter attaches, clean it off with a cloth. Then apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the entire outer circumference contact surface area of the piston.


HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

47287 1.REMOVE BRAKE FLUID INFORMATION LABEL

47230 2.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR FILLER CAP ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the brake master cylinder reservoir filler cap assembly from the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


47299 3.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR STRAINER

a.

Remove the brake master cylinder reservoir strainer from the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


47202 4.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the screw and brake master cylinder reservoir sub-assembly.


47255 5.REMOVE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR GROMMET


a.

Remove the 2 master cylinder reservoir grommets from the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


47210T 6.REMOVE PISTON STOPPER BOLT

a.
2.229,1.615 1.906,1.615 1.906,1.615 1.635,0.698 true 2.271,1.542 2.583,1.74 0.313,0.198 10 false *a
*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, push the piston in all the way, and remove the piston stopper bolt and gasket.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



7.REMOVE HOLE SNAP RING

a.

Push in the piston with a screwdriver and remove the snap ring with snap ring pliers.


8.REMOVE NO. 1 MASTER CYLINDER PISTON SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the No. 1 master cylinder piston sub-assembly and spring by hand.

NOTICE:

Pull the No. 1 piston and spring straight out. Do not pull them out at an angle.



9.REMOVE NO. 2 MASTER CYLINDER PISTON SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.
2.719,1.583 2.875,1.781 0.156,0.198 10 false A

Place a cloth and 2 wooden blocks on the work table, and lightly tap the cylinder flange against the block edges until the No. 2 piston and spring drop out of the cylinder.

HINT:

Make sure the distance (A) from the cloth to the top of the blocks is at least 100 mm (3.94 in.).

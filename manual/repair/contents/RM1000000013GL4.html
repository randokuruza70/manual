i.

Tire pressure adjustment method when warm (w/ Tire Pressure Warning System):

1.

Turn the ignition switch off.

2.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.

3.

Turn the engine switch on (IG).

4.

Turn the GTS on.

5.

Enter the following menus: Chassis / Tire Pressure Monitor / Data List.

6.

Adjust the tire pressure so that the displayed value is equal to the set pressure.

7.

Perform initialization and check that initialization completes.

Click hereSuspension>TIRE PRESSURE MONITORING>TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM>INITIALIZATION201701,999999,_53,_062777,_0306275,RM1000000012NUB,

8.

Check and record the value of the Data List item "Temperature in Tire". (Ts)

9.

Check and record the ambient temperature during tire pressure adjustment. (Tm)

10.

Readjust the tire pressure according to the difference between the tire internal temperature (Ts) and the ambient temperature (Tm). (P)

HINT:

Tire internal temperature: Ts, Ambient temperature: Tm, Tire pressure readjustment value: PP = (Specified Pressure) + (Ts - Tm)


11.

Check the pressure adjustment value with the Data List item "Tire Inflation Pressure".


NOTICE:

Check the tire pressure warning system description for the relationship between the inflation pressure and tire pressure warning pressure.

Click hereSuspension>TIRE PRESSURE MONITORING>TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM>SYSTEM DESCRIPTION201701,999999,_53,_062777,_0306275,RM1000000012NU9,

3.INSPECT WHEEL BALANCE

a.

for Aluminum Wheel Type (w/ Tire Pressure Warning System) :

Check and adjust the off-the-car balance.

Maximum imbalance after adjustment:

19.3 g (0.043 lb) or less


HINT:
·

The inner side balance weights should be installed by clipping them to the rim.

·

If the tires vibrate even after off-the-car balance adjustment, adjust the wheel balance with on-the-car balancing as necessary.

CLUTCH  CLUTCH RELEASE CYLINDER(for 1HZ)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

04313 1.REMOVE CLUTCH RELEASE CYLINDER KIT

a.

Remove the boot together with the push rod from the clutch release cylinder body.


b.

Remove the push rod from the boot.


c.

Using compressed air, remove the piston together with the spring from the clutch release cylinder body.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the inside of the cylinder body.



d.

Remove the 2 cylinder cups from the piston.


31477 2.REMOVE RELEASE CYLINDER BLEEDER PLUG

a.

Remove the bleeder plug cap.


b.

Remove the release cylinder bleeder plug from the clutch release cylinder body.

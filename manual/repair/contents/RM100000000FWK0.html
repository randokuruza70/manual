44731C 5.INSTALL BRAKE BOOSTER BRACKET

44610 7.INSTALL BRAKE BOOSTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Torque:
14.2 N*m (145 kgf*cm, 10 ft.*lbf)


47264D 8.TEMPORARILY INSTALL BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER PUSH ROD CLEVIS

a.

Temporarily install the push rod clevis to the brake booster by tightening the lock nut.

HINT:

The push rod clevis lock nut and push rod clevis will be tightened to a torque specification in the "CHECK AND ADJUST BRAKE PEDAL HEIGHT" procedure.

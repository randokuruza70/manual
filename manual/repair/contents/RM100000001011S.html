71861 2.REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER CUSHION SHIELD RH

a.

Detach the band.


b.

Using moulding remover A, detach the 2 claws and 4 guides.


c.

Using moulding remover A, detach the 2 claws and 2 guides to remove the front seat inner cushion shield RH.


73230 3.REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY RH

a.
2.031,3.75 2.906,3.604 true 2.958,3.479 3.188,3.688 0.229,0.208 10 true *a
*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, remove the clip and fold down the separate type front seat cushion cover.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



b.

Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and disconnect the connector.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



c.

Detach the 2 clamps and disconnect the wire harness.


d.

Remove the nut and front seat inner belt assembly RH.


e.

Remove the front seat belt anchor plate from the front seat cushion spring assembly RH.


73240 4.REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.
1.333,3.885 3,3.333 true 3.021,3.188 3.271,3.396 0.25,0.208 10 false *a
*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, remove the clip and fold down the separate type front seat cushion cover.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



b.

Disconnect the connector of the occupant detection sensor.


c.

Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and disconnect the connector.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



d.

Detach the 3 clamps and disconnect the wire harness.


e.

Remove the nut and front seat inner belt assembly LH.


f.

Remove the front seat belt anchor plate from the front seat cushion spring assembly LH.

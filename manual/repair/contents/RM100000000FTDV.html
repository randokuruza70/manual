EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  MUDGUARD(for Front Side)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedures for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

76604 1.REMOVE FRONT FENDER MUDGUARD SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

for Pickup:

i.

Remove the 3 screws and front fender mudguard sub-assembly LH.



b.

except pickup:

i.

Remove the 3 screws and front fender mudguard sub-assembly LH.



c.

w/ Over Fender:

i.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 2 screws.


ii.

Remove the bolt and nut.


iii.

Remove the front fender mudguard sub-assembly LH, as shown in the illustration.

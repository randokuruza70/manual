Tester Connection Condition Specified Condition

NOTICE:

In the table above, the terms "Cold" and "Hot" refer to the temperature of the crankshaft position sensor. "Cold" means approximately -10 to 50°C (14 to 122°F). "Hot" means approximately 50 to 100°C (122 to 212°F).


If the result is not as specified, replace the crankshaft position sensor.

1HZ INTAKE / EXHAUST  INTAKE MANIFOLD  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

17111 1.INSTALL INTAKE MANIFOLD

a.

Install a new intake manifold gasket and 5 new No. 2 intake manifold gaskets to the cylinder head sub-assembly.


b.

Install the intake manifold with 12 new seal washers and the 12 nuts.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


c.

w/ EGR System:

Install the 2 wire harness clamp brackets to the intake manifold with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
8.3 N*m (85 kgf*cm, 73 in.*lbf)


2.CONNECT HEATER HOSE BRACKET

a.

Connect the heater hose bracket with the bolt.

Torque:
17.5 N*m (178 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the heater hose valve bracket with the bolt.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


25860A 3.INSTALL NO. 1 VACUUM SWITCHING VALVE

a.

Install the No. 1 vacuum switching valve to the intake manifold with bolt.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the 2 vacuum hoses and connector to the No. 1 vacuum switching valve.


78103 4.INSTALL NO. 1 ACCELERATOR CONNECTING ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly to the intake manifold with the 2 bolts and nut.

Torque:
for Bolt : 19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)
for Nut : 9.8 N*m (100 kgf*cm, 87 in.*lbf)


b.

w/ EGR System:

i.

Attach the wire harness clamp to the No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly.


ii.

Connect the connector.



78104 5.INSTALL NO. 2 ACCELERATOR CONNECTING ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Attach the No. 2 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly to the injection pump assembly and No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly with the 2 clamps.


6.INSTALL INJECTION PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

15301 7.INSTALL ENGINE OIL LEVEL DIPSTICK GUIDE

a.

Install a new O-ring to the engine oil level dipstick guide.


b.

Apply a small amount of clean engine oil to the O-ring.


c.

Install the engine oil level dipstick guide with the bolt.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


d.

Install the engine oil level dipstick.


e.

Attach the clamp to the engine oil level dipstick guide.


44360 8.CONNECT VANE PUMP OIL RESERVOIR ASSEMBLY

a.

Connect the vane pump oil reservoir assembly with the 3 bolts.

Torque:
18 N*m (184 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)


17113 9.INSTALL INTAKE PIPE

a.

Install the accelerator control cable bracket sub-assembly with the bolt.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


b.

Install a new gasket and the intake pipe with the 3 bolts.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


c.

Connect the ventilation hose.


d.

w/o EGR System:

Install the intake pipe stay to the intake pipe and cylinder head sub-assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


e.

w/ EGR System:

i.

Connect the 2 vacuum hoses.


ii.

Attach the vacuum hose clamp.


iii.

Connect the 2 connectors and 2 vacuum hoses.



78180P 10.CONNECT ACCELERATOR CONTROL CABLE ASSEMBLY

a.

Connect the accelerator control cable assembly to the No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly.


b.

Tighten the 2 nuts.

Torque:
12.5 N*m (127 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Adjust the accelerator pedal play to 0 to 3 mm (0 to 0.118 in.) and tighten the nuts.



c.

w/ EGR System:

Attach the wire harness clamp to the accelerator control cable assembly.


d.

for LHD:

i.

Install the accelerator control cable bracket to the intake manifold with the bolt.

Torque:
12 N*m (122 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)


ii.

Install the accelerator control cable bracket to the cylinder head sub-assembly with the bolt.

Torque:
12 N*m (122 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

17705 12.INSTALL AIR CLEANER CAP SUB-ASSEMBLY WITH HOSE

a.

Install the air cleaner cap sub-assembly with hose.


b.

Connect the air cleaner hose and tighten the air cleaner hose clamp.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


c.

Install the butterfly nut and attach the 3 clamps.


d.

for RHD:

Attach the accelerator control cable assembly to the air cleaner hose claw.

1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER HEAD  REASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD STUD BOLT

HINT:

If a cylinder head stud bolt is deformed or the threads are damaged, replace it.


a.

Using an E8 "TORX" socket wrench, install the cylinder head stud bolts.

Torque:
12 N*m (122 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)


11117E 2.INSTALL NO. 1 STRAIGHT SCREW PLUG WITH HEAD

a.

Using a 6 mm hexagon wrench, install a new gasket and the No. 1 straight screw plug with head.

Torque:
25 N*m (255 kgf*cm, 18 ft.*lbf)


11117E 3.INSTALL NO. 1 STRAIGHT SCREW PLUG WITH HEAD (w/ DPF)

a.

Install a new gasket with the No. 1 straight screw plug with head.

Torque:
18 N*m (184 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)


23699A 4.INSTALL NOZZLE HOLDER CLAMP SEAT (w/o DPF)

a.

Install the 4 nozzle holder clamp seats.

Torque:
10 N*m (102 kgf*cm, 7 ft.*lbf)


13734 5.INSTALL VALVE SPRING SEAT

a.

Install the valve spring seats to the cylinder head sub-assembly.

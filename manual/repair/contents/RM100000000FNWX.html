1VD-FTV COOLING  THERMOSTAT  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1.INSPECT THERMOSTAT

HINT:
2.49,1.417 2.865,1.594 0.375,0.177 10 false *a
*a 76°C


The valve opening temperature is inscribed on the thermostat.


a.

Immerse the thermostat in water and then gradually heat the water.


b.

Check the valve opening temperature of the thermostat.

Valve opening temperature:

74 to 78°C (165 to 172°F)


If the valve opening temperature is not as specified, replace the thermostat.


c.
2.531,1.635 2.75,1.865 0.219,0.229 10 false *a
*a Valve Lift


Check the valve lift.

Valve lift:

10 mm (0.394 in.) or more at 90°C (194°F)


If the valve lift is not as specified, replace the thermostat.


d.

Check that the valve is fully closed when the thermostat is at low temperatures (below 40°C (104°F)).

If not fully closed, replace the thermostat.

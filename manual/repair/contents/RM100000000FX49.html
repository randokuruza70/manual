CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Body Side)

a.

Remove the 3 screws and rear combination light assembly LH.


b.

Disconnect the grommet and pull out the wire harness.


c.

Disconnect the connector.


2.REMOVE SCREW GROMMET (for Rear Body Side)

a.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the screw grommet.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the screw grommet on the other side.

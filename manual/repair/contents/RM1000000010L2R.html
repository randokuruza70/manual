1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM(w/ DPF)  P0234  Turbocharger / Supercharger Overboost Condition  P1251  Turbocharger / Supercharger Overboost Condition (Too High)  


DESCRIPTION

The variable nozzle vane type turbocharger operates the vacuum type actuator and controls the nozzle vane by using the VRV, which is controlled by the ECM. At a low to medium vehicle speed, when the nozzle vane moves in the closing direction and the exhaust gas rate of flow becomes faster, the engine torque increases. At a high vehicle speed, when the nozzle vane moves in the opening direction, the exhaust pressure decreases. This leads to improved fuel consumption and engine output.

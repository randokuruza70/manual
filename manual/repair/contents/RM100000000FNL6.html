1HZ FUEL  FUEL INJECTION NOZZLE  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

23600 1.INSTALL NOZZLE HOLDER AND NOZZLE SET

a.

Place 6 new injection nozzle seat gaskets and the 6 injection nozzle seats into the 6 injection nozzle holes of the cylinder head sub-assembly.


b.

Using SST, install the 6 nozzle holder and nozzle sets.

SST
09268-64010  

Torque:
63.7 N*m (650 kgf*cm, 47 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

Overtightening could cause nozzle deformation and needle adhesion or other defects.

·

When installing the injection nozzle, never tighten the nozzle holder body. Tighten the retaining nut section.




23760 2.INSTALL NOZZLE LEAKAGE PIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install 6 new gaskets and the nozzle leakage pipe assembly with the 6 nuts.

Torque:
29.4 N*m (300 kgf*cm, 22 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the fuel hose to the nozzle leakage pipe assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


23701 3.INSTALL INJECTION PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Temporarily install the 6 injection pipe sub-assemblies to the nozzle holder and nozzle set side and injection pump side.

5.BLEED INJECTION PIPE

a.

Move the priming pump in the upper part of the fuel filter assembly up and down, and fill the injection pump and fuel system with fuel.


b.

Loosen the injection pipe union nuts (in the nozzle side).


c.

Crank the engine until fuel comes out from the injection pipe union nut (in the nozzle side).


d.

Tighten the injection pipe union nut.

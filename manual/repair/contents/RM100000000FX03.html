BRAKE (REAR)  REAR BRAKE(for Rear Drum Brake)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE REAR BRAKE SHOE

a.

b.
2.365,1.177 2.615,0.875 2.615,0.875 2.781,0.875 false 2.844,0.802 3.188,0.969 0.344,0.167 10 false SST

Using SST, remove the 2 shoe hold down spring cups, shoe hold down spring and shoe hold down spring pin.

SST
09718-00011  


c.

Remove the rear brake shoe and shoe tension spring.


2.REMOVE FRONT BRAKE SHOE

a.
1.646,1.365 0.813,1.771 0.813,1.771 0.573,1.771 true 0.26,1.698 0.594,1.885 0.333,0.188 10 false SST

Using SST, remove the 2 shoe hold down spring cups, shoe hold down spring, shoe hold down spring pin and front brake shoe.

SST
09718-00011  


b.

Using pliers, disconnect the parking brake wire LH from the parking brake shoe lever.


47062 3.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE STRUT SET LH

a.

Remove the parking brake shoe strut set LH from the front brake shoe.

NOTICE:

Do not disassemble the strut set, and be careful not to damage or bend any of the components.



47430G 4.REMOVE REAR BRAKE AUTOMATIC ADJUST LEVER TENSION SPRING

a.
1.792,1.958 2.375,1.781 2.375,1.781 2.625,1.781 false 2.688,1.708 3.063,1.875 0.375,0.167 10 false SST

Using SST, remove the rear brake automatic adjust lever tension spring.

SST
09703-30011  


47664 5.REMOVE REAR BRAKE AUTOMATIC ADJUST LEVER

a.

Remove the E-ring and rear brake automatic adjust lever.


47612A 6.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE LEVER LH

a.

Remove the C-washer and rear brake parking brake shoe lever LH.


47619 7.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE WIRE LH

a.

Remove the parking brake wire LH from the parking brake bell crank LH.


47570 8.REMOVE REAR WHEEL BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake tube.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts and rear wheel brake cylinder assembly LH.


9.REMOVE REAR WHEEL BRAKE CYLINDER CUP

a.

Remove the 2 cylinder boots from the rear wheel brake cylinder assembly LH.


b.

Remove the 2 pistons and compression spring.


c.

Remove the 2 rear wheel brake cylinder cups from each piston.


47548R 10.REMOVE REAR BRAKE DRUM BLEEDER PLUG CAP

47547R 11.REMOVE REAR BRAKE DRUM BLEEDER PLUG

a.

Remove the rear brake drum bleeder plug from the rear wheel brake cylinder assembly LH.


47625A 12.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELL CRANK LH

a.

Remove the 2 tension springs from the parking brake bell crank LH.


b.

Remove the clip and pin, then disconnect the No. 1 parking brake cable assembly.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts and parking brake bell crank LH


d.

Remove the C-washer and pin.


e.

Remove the parking brake bell crank LH from the bell crank bracket.


f.

Remove the bell crank boot from the parking brake bell crank LH.

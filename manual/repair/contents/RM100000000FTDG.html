PROCEDURE

52113 1.REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BAR LH (w/ Over Fender)

a.

Remove the front No. 3 bumper bracket plate.


b.

Remove the 4 clips and front bumper bar LH from the front bumper cover.


52112 2.REMOVE FRONT BUMPER BAR RH (w/ Over Fender)

HINT:

Use the same procedures described for the LH side.


52128B 3.REMOVE FRONT BUMPER HOLE COVER LH (w/o Fog Light)

a.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the front bumper hole cover LH.


52127D 4.REMOVE FRONT BUMPER HOLE COVER RH (w/o Fog Light)

HINT:

Use the same procedures described for the LH side.

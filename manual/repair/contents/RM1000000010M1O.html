CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Always stop the engine when installing or removing the vacuum pump, or when removing the vacuum hoses.


PROCEDURE

25630 1.INSPECT NO. 2 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the vacuum hose of the No. 1 vacuum switching valve from the EGR cooler.


b.

SEAT  REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Model Code #J78 Series)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY

a.

Place the rear No. 1 seat assembly in the cabin.

NOTICE:
·

2 or more people are required when inserting the rear No. 1 seat assembly into the vehicle.

·

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.




b.

Install the rear No. 1 seat assembly with the 4 bolts.

Torque:
25.5 N*m (260 kgf*cm, 19 ft.*lbf)


71940A 2.INSTALL REAR SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the 2 rear seat headrest assemblies.

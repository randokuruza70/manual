88711 10.DISCONNECT NO. 1 COOLER REFRIGERANT DISCHARGE HOSE


a.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the No. 1 cooler refrigerant discharge hose from the cooler condenser assembly.

NOTICE:
·

When removing the bolt, do not allow any tools to contact the pipe.

·

When removing the bolt, hold a part of the pipe near the connector.




b.

Remove the O-ring from the discharge hose.

NOTICE:

Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.



88716B 11.DISCONNECT COOLER REFRIGERANT LIQUID PIPE B


a.

Remove the nut and cooler refrigerant liquid pipe B from the cooler condenser assembly.

NOTICE:
·

When removing the nut, do not allow any tools to contact the pipe.

·

When removing the nut, hold a part of the pipe near the connector.




b.

Remove the O-ring from the liquid pipe.

NOTICE:

Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.

1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER HEAD GASKET(w/ DPF)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

Before performing these installation procedures, sufficiently clean and remove any foreign matter from the cylinder block, cylinder head, camshaft and other parts.

·

When replacing an injector (including interchanging injectors between cylinders), common rail, cylinder head, or intake manifold, replace the corresponding injection pipes with a new one.

·

When fuel lines are disconnected, air may enter the fuel lines, leading to engine starting trouble. Therefore, perform forced regeneration and bleed the air from the fuel lines (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL>EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ DPF)>ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION201608,999999,_51,_029525,_0290812,RM1000000010800_01_0014,).



PROCEDURE

11115 1.SELECT NO. 1 CYLINDER HEAD GASKET

a.

Set the 2 crankshaft pulley set bolts to the crankshaft.


b.

Clean the cylinder block sub-assembly with solvent.


c.

Inspect the protrusions for each cylinder.

i.

Set the piston of the cylinder to be measured to slightly before TDC.


ii.

Place a dial indicator on the cylinder block sub-assembly, and set the measuring tip as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the dial indicator is at a right angle to the cylinder block sub-assembly top surface.



iii.

Set the dial indicator at 0 mm (0 in.).

HINT:

Make sure that the measuring tip is flat against the cylinder block sub-assembly surface and piston head when taking the measurements.



iv.

Find where the piston head protrudes most by slowly turning the crankshaft clockwise and counterclockwise.


v.

Measure the piston protrusion value of each cylinder at 2 places as shown in the illustration below, making a total of 8 measurements.

Standard piston protrusion:

0.520 to 0.780 mm (0.0205 to 0.0307 in.)


If the protrusion is not as specified, remove and reinstall the piston and connecting rod.

2.REMOVE REAR DISC BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the rear disc brake cylinder.

NOTICE:

Do not disconnect the flexible hose from the disc brake cylinder.



b.

Hang the disc brake cylinder with a piece of wire.

NOTICE:

Do not twist or bend the flexible hose.

46550G 4.REMOVE NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE SHOE RETURN TENSION SPRING

a.

Remove the No. 1 parking brake shoe return tension spring.


46550E 5.REMOVE NO. 2 PARKING BRAKE SHOE RETURN TENSION SPRING

a.

Remove the 2 No. 2 parking brake shoe return tension springs.


46590 6.DISCONNECT NO. 2 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 2 parking brake shoe hold down spring cups and parking brake shoe hold down compression spring to disconnect the No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly LH from the parking brake backing plate LH.


47614 7.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE STRUT LH

a.

Open No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly LH by hand.


b.

Remove the parking brake shoe strut LH and parking brake shoe strut compression spring.


46580 8.DISCONNECT NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 2 parking brake shoe hold down spring cups and parking brake shoe hold down compression spring to disconnect the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly LH from the parking brake backing plate LH.


47406A 9.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE ADJUSTING SCREW SET

a.

Pull the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly LH and No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly LH open by hand, and then pull them downward off the vehicle.


b.

Remove the parking brake shoe adjusting screw set.


46590 10.REMOVE NO. 2 PARKING BRAKE SHOE ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the No. 3 parking brake shoe return tension spring to remove the No. 2 parking brake shoe assembly LH.


46550A 11.REMOVE NO. 3 PARKING BRAKE SHOE RETURN TENSION SPRING

a.

Remove the No. 3 parking brake shoe return tension spring from the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly LH.


47616A 12.DISCONNECT PARKING BRAKE WIRE

a.

Using needle-nose pliers, disconnect the parking brake wire from the parking brake shoe lever as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the parking brake wire.



47612 13.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE LEVER LH

a.

Remove the C-washer, shim and parking brake shoe lever LH from the No. 1 parking brake shoe assembly LH as shown in the illustration.


47459B 14.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE GUIDE PLATE SET BOLT

a.

Remove the shoe guide plate set bolt and shoe guide plate.


47447E 15.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE HOLD DOWN SPRING PIN (for Front Side)

a.

Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring pin.


47447F 16.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE SHOE HOLD DOWN SPRING PIN (for Rear Side)

a.

Remove the parking brake shoe hold down spring pin.


47616A 17.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE WIRE

a.

Remove the C-washer, pin and parking brake wire from the parking brake bell crank LH.


46410 18.DISCONNECT NO. 1 PARKING BRAKE CABLE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the clip, pin and disconnect the No. 1 parking brake cable assembly from the parking brake bell crank LH.


47623B 19.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELL CRANK RETURN TENSION SPRING

a.

Remove the 2 parking brake bell crank return tension springs.


47625B 20.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELL CRANK LH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and parking brake bell crank LH from the parking brake backing plate LH.


47639A 21.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELLCRANK STOPPER BOLT

a.

Remove the parking brake bellcrank stopper bolt and nut from the parking brake bell crank LH.


47634 22.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELLCRANK BOOT

a.

Remove the parking brake bellcrank boot from the brake bell crank LH.


47629 23.REMOVE PARKING BRAKE BELLCRANK BRACKET

a.

Remove the C-washer, pin and disconnect the parking brake bellcrank bracket from the parking brake bell crank LH.

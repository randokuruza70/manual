1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EGR COOLER  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL STUD BOLT

a.

Using an E6 "TORX" socket wrench, install the 4 stud bolts to the EGR cooler assembly.

Torque:
6.0 N*m (61 kgf*cm, 53 in.*lbf)


16296 2.INSTALL NO. 8 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Install the 2 No. 8 water by-pass hoses to the EGR cooler assembly, and slide the 2 clamps to secure the hoses.


16279 3.INSTALL NO. 3 WATER BY-PASS PIPE

a.

Install the No. 3 water by-pass pipe with the bolt.

Torque:
10 N*m (102 kgf*cm, 7 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the 2 No. 12 water by-pass hoses to the EGR cooler assembly, and slide the 2 clamps to secure the hoses.


4.INSTALL NO. 1 AND NO. 2 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the No. 1 and No. 2 EGR valve assembly.

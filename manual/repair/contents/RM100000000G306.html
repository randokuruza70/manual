LIGHTING (EXT)  HIGH MOUNTED STOP LIGHT ASSEMBLY  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

81977 1.REMOVE CENTER STOP LIGHT COVER

a.

Detach the 2 clips and 2 claws and remove the center stop light cover.


81570 2.REMOVE CENTER STOP LIGHT ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 screws.


b.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the center stop light assembly.


c.

Disconnect the connector.


81572 3.REMOVE CENTER STOP LIGHT GASKET

a.

Remove the 3 screws and center stop light gasket.


4.REMOVE SCREW GROMMET

a.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the screw grommet.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the screw grommet on the other side.



82214A 5.REMOVE LIGHT WIRE


a.

Disconnect the grommet and pull out the light wire.


b.

Disconnect the connector and remove the light wire.

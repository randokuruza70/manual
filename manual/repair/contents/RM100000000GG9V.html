SEAT BELT  REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Semi-long Body)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

67918A 1.REMOVE REAR DOOR SCUFF PLATE LH

a.

Detach the 2 clips and remove the rear door scuff plate LH.


62552F 2.REMOVE REAR SEAT SIDE GARNISH LH


a.

Detach the 2 clips.


b.

Remove the rear seat side garnish LH in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration.


64707D 3.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM JACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY


a.

Rotate the knob in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration and remove the quarter trim jack cover sub-assembly.


62640P 4.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM PANEL ASSEMBLY LH


a.

Detach the 10 clips and remove the quarter trim panel assembly LH.


62630P 5.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM PANEL ASSEMBLY RH (for RH Side)


a.

Detach the 12 clips and remove the quarter trim panel assembly RH.


73370 6.REMOVE REAR SEAT OUTER 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the floor anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the rear seat outer 3 point type belt shoulder anchor cover.


c.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt from the vehicle body.

PROCEDURE

44310 1.INSTALL VANE PUMP ASSEMBLY

a.

Coat a new O-ring with MP grease and install it to the vane pump assembly.


b.

Install the vane pump assembly with the 2 nuts.

Torque:
29 N*m (296 kgf*cm, 21 ft.*lbf)

a.

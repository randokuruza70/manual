RM25T0E _51 Engine / Hybrid System _029525 1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL _0133832 MONOLITHIC CONVERTER A

1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  MONOLITHIC CONVERTER  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

53737B 1.REMOVE FRONT FENDER APRON SEAL LH


a.

Remove the 11 clips and front fender apron seal LH.


18421C 2.REMOVE CENTER CONVERTER PROTECTOR STAY

a.

Remove the 3 bolts and center converter protector stay.


18450A 3.REMOVE MONOLITHIC CONVERTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs.


b.

Remove the 3 nuts, monolithic converter assembly and gasket.


c.

Remove the gasket from the monolithic converter assembly.


18431 4.REMOVE UPPER CATALYTIC CONVERTER INSULATOR

a.

Remove the 2 bolts, nut and upper catalytic converter insulator.

R151F MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE  MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL  REPLACEMENT  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL

a.

Remove the manual transmission filler plug and gasket.


b.

Remove the drain plug sub-assembly and gasket to drain the manual transmission oil.


c.

Install a new gasket and the drain plug sub-assembly.

Torque:
37 N*m (377 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)


2.ADD MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL

a.
2.667,0.854 2.979,1.052 0.313,0.198 10 false *a
*a 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)


Add manual transmission oil until the oil level is within 0 to 5 mm (0.196 in.) from the bottom of the manual transmission filler plug opening.

Manual transmission oil:
Oil grade Oil viscosity Standard capacity
GL-4 SAE 75W-90 2.2 liters (2.3 US qts, 1.9 Imp. qts)



b.

Install a new gasket and the manual transmission filler plug.

Torque:
37 N*m (377 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)

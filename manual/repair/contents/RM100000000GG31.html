73220 2.REMOVE FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the front seat belt anchor.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the front seat outer belt shoulder anchor cover.


c.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts.


e.

Detach the guide and remove the front seat outer belt assembly LH.

INITIALIZATION PROCEDURE FOR DIESEL THROTTLE BODY ASSEMBLY


HINT:

When the diesel throttle body assembly is replaced, perform initialization.


a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.

NOTICE:

Do not start the engine.



b.

Turn the ignition switch off and wait for 30 seconds or more.


c.

Turn the ignition switch to ON and wait for 10 seconds or more.



FUEL SUPPLY PUMP INITIALIZATION PROCEDURE


HINT:

After replacing the fuel supply pump assembly and/or the ECM:

·

If the engine is defective or stalls immediately after startup, the ECM's learned values must be initialized. The engine can be initialized through the GTS or by connecting the DLC3 terminals.

·

If the engine starts normally, initialization is not necessary. Perform the step "idle the engine" procedure below only.



a.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


b.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.

NOTICE:

Do not start the engine.



c.

Turn the GTS ON.


d.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Utility / Supply Pump Initialization.

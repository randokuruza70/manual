1GR-FE FUEL  FUEL TANK SELECT VALVE(for Fuel Sub Tank)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

77268A 1.REMOVE NO. 3 FUEL TUBE PROTECTOR

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 fuel tube protector from the fuel tank solenoid valve with bracket.


77406 2.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID VALVE WITH BRACKET

a.

Slide the 3 clamps and disconnect the 3 fuel hoses from the fuel tank solenoid return valve assembly.


b.

Disconnect the 3 fuel tubes from the No. 3 fuel main tube and fuel tank solenoid main valve assembly.

c.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


d.

Detach the fuel tube clamp.


e.

f.

Remove the 3 bolts and fuel tank solenoid valve with bracket.


77710B 3.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID MAIN VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the bolt and fuel tank solenoid main valve assembly from the fuel tube protector sub-assembly.


77720E 4.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID RETURN VALVE ASSEMBLY

b.

HINT:

PROCEDURE

85160 1.INSTALL WINDSHIELD WIPER LINK ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the windshield wiper link assembly through the service hole, and then install the 6 bolts.

Torque:
5.4 N*m (55 kgf*cm, 48 in.*lbf)


85110 2.INSTALL WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

a.

Attach the claw of the windshield wiper link assembly to the vehicle body.


b.

Connect the windshield wiper motor assembly to the windshield wiper link assembly.


c.

Install the windshield wiper motor assembly with the 4 bolts.

Torque:
5.5 N*m (56 kgf*cm, 49 in.*lbf)


d.

Connect the connector.


55791E 3.INSTALL HEATER DUCT HOLE COVER

a.

Attach the 4 claws to install the heater duct hole cover.

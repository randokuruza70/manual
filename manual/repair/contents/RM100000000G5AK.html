SEAT  REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Short Body)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the LH and RH side.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

71086B 1.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 18 hog rings.



b.

w/o Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 2 pieces of fastening tape.


ii.

Remove the 16 hog rings.



c.

Remove the rear side seat cushion cover sub-assembly LH and rear side seat cushion pad LH from the rear side seat frame assembly LH.


71089B 2.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEATBACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 6 hog rings.


b.

While pulling up the rear side seatback cover sub-assembly LH, remove the 4 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear side seat seatback cover sub-assembly LH from the rear side seat frame assembly LH.


d.

Remove the 7 hog rings and rear side seatback pad LH.


71034A 3.REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 E-rings and 2 pins.


b.

Remove the 2 torsion springs.


c.

Remove the rear No. 2 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH from the rear side seat frame assembly LH.

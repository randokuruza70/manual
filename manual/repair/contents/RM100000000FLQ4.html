3.DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

4.DRAIN FUEL

a.

Loosen the fuel tank drain plug and drain the fuel from the fuel sub tank.


b.

Install a new gasket and the fuel tank drain plug to the fuel sub tank.

Torque:
6.5 N*m (66 kgf*cm, 58 in.*lbf)


5.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube sub-assembly.

6.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube sub-assembly.


77213 7.DISCONNECT FUEL INLET HOSE

a.

Loosen the clamp and disconnect the fuel inlet hose from the fuel tank filler lower pipe sub-assembly.


77100D 8.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel sub tank.


9.REMOVE FUEL SUB TANK

a.

Disconnect the fuel pump connector and sender gauge connector and detach the wire harness clamp.


b.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel sub tank.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 pins and disconnect the 2 fuel tank bands.


d.

Slightly lower the engine lifter.


e.

Remove the No. 1 fuel tank protector sub-assembly.

77203B 11.REMOVE FUEL SUCTION TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the nut.

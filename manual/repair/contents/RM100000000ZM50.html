MONITOR DESCRIPTION

When the air fuel ratio sensor output deviates to lean due to a malfunction in the sensor, white smoke occurs since the ECM performs control for a lean condition. The ECM monitors the current value of the sensor in the fuel cut to detect abnormal current. If the ECM detects an air fuel ratio sensor voltage of 2.65 V or higher for a cumulative time of 3.5 seconds or more, the ECM judges that there is a malfunction in the air fuel ratio sensor and stores DTC P2195.

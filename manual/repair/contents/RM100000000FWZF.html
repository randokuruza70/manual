PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE CYLINDER BOOT

a.
1.813,0.99 1.135,1.552 1.135,1.552 0.917,1.552 true 0.729,1.469 0.938,1.635 0.208,0.167 10 false *a
*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, remove the 4 set rings and 4 cylinder boots from the disc brake cylinder assembly LH.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.


NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the brake piston and disc brake cylinder assembly LH.



47731 2.REMOVE FRONT DISC BRAKE PISTON

a.
1.229,0.188 1.406,0.354 0.177,0.167 10 false *a 2.938,0.885 3.104,1.031 0.167,0.146 10 false *b 3.094,1.375 3.25,1.531 0.156,0.156 10 false *c
*a 170 mm (6.70 in.)
*b 50 mm (1.97 in.)
*c 25 mm (0.99 in.)


Prepare a wooden plate to hold the front disc brake pistons.


b.

Place the wooden plate between the front disc brake pistons and insert a front disc brake pad on one side.


c.

Use compressed air to remove the 4 front disc brake pistons from the disc brake cylinder assembly LH.

CAUTION:

Do not place your finger in front of the piston when using compressed air.


NOTICE:

Be careful not to spatter the brake fluid.

Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, remove the 4 piston seals from the disc brake cylinder assembly LH.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.


NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the inner cylinder and cylinder groove.

2.DRAIN BRAKE FLUID

NOTICE:

Wash off brake fluid immediately if it comes in contact with any painted surface.


47313A 3.REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE LH


a.

Remove the union bolt and gasket and disconnect the front flexible hose LH.


b.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the front flexible hose LH with a wrench.

NOTICE:
·

Do not kink or damage the brake line.

·

Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake line.




c.

Remove the clip and front flexible hose LH.


47313A 4.REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE (for 1HZ, 1VD-FTV)

a.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 4 brake lines while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:
·

Do not kink or damage the brake line.

·

Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake line.




b.

Remove the 4 clips and 2 front flexible hoses.


47313A 5.REMOVE FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE (for 1GR-FE)

NOTICE:

·

b.

84306 1.INSTALL SPIRAL WITH SENSOR CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:
·

Do not replace the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly with the battery connected and the ignition switch ON.

·

Do not rotate the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly with the battery connected and the ignition switch on (IG).

·

When rotating the steering angle sensor to check the operation of the sensor (checking for abnormal noise, performing diagnostics, etc.), make sure that the front wheels are aligned straight ahead and the steering wheel is installed.



a.

Turn the ignition switch off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.

NOTICE:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.



c.

Check that the front wheels are facing straight ahead.


d.

Attach the 3 claws and install the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

When replacing the spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly with a new one, remove the lock pin before installing the steering wheel assembly.



e.

f.

Connect the airbag connector and check that the slider is in the lock position.

HINT:

If the slider is not in the lock position, the airbag connector is not completely connected. Disconnect the airbag connector, check the airbag connector and spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly terminals and connector housings for deformation or foreign matter, and then reconnect the airbag connector.

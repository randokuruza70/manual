1HZ EMISSION CONTROL  EGR VALVE  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

25620 1.INSPECT EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.
2.833,0.406 3.146,0.563 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Vacuum


When a vacuum of 27 kPa (200 mmHg, 7.88 in.Hg) is applied to the diaphragm chamber, check that the shaft rises and air flows out.


b.

When applying more than 67 kPa (500 mmHg, 19.7 in.Hg), check that there are no leaks and that the shaft stays in position.


c.

Check that the valve does not have heavy carbon deposits and is not stuck.

If the result is not as specified, replace the EGR valve assembly.

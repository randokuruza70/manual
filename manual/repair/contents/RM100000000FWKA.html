BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM PUMP(for 1VD-FTV)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

The vacuum pump assembly is listed as one of the 200000 km (124000 mile) maintenance parts. Make sure to disassemble and inspect it every 200000 km (124000 miles) and replace parts as necessary.


PROCEDURE

44774 1.DISCONNECT UNION TO CONNECTOR TUBE HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the hose.


29300 2.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 3 bolts and vacuum pump from the engine.

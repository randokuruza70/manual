AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  STEERING KNUCKLE  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

PROCEDURE

43405 1.INSTALL FRONT AXLE OUTER SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Hold the front axle outer shaft in a vise and while compressing a new snap ring, install the front axle inner shaft to the front axle outer shaft.


b.

Make sure that the front axle inner shaft cannot be pulled out.


43211R 2.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE BUSHING

a.
2.583,0.719 2.958,0.719 false 2.99,0.646 3.365,0.844 0.375,0.198 10 false SST

Using SST and a press, install a new needle roller bearing and new steering knuckle bushing into the steering knuckle spindle.

SST
09618-60010  


43411D 3.INSTALL FRONT DRIVE SHAFT TYPE T OIL SEAL

a.
2.135,0.958 2.385,0.354 2.385,0.354 2.792,0.354 true 2.865,0.281 3.271,0.479 0.406,0.198 10 false SST

Using SST and a hammer, install a new front drive shaft type T oil seal into the front axle housing assembly.

SST
09618-60010  


b.

Coat the seal lip with MP grease.


45611F 4.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE ARM BEARING

a.
0.385,0.656 0.542,0.656 0.542,0.656 0.74,0.906 true 0.083,0.573 0.469,0.771 0.385,0.198 10 false SST

Using SST and a hammer, carefully install 2 new bearing outer races.

SST
09605-60010  


b.

Place MP grease in the palm of your hand.


c.

Pack grease into the steering knuckle arm bearing, continuing until the grease oozes out from the other side.


d.

Do the same around the bearing circumference.


43212 5.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE LH

a.

Place the 2 steering knuckle arm bearings in positions on the steering knuckle and front axle housing.


b.

Install the steering knuckle to the front axle housing assembly.


43204 6.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE OIL SEAL SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new steering knuckle outer oil seal, new steering knuckle inner oil seal and new steering knuckle oil seal inner ring to the steering knuckle.


43232 7.TEMPORARILY INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE BEARING CAP

45611G 8.TEMPORARILY INSTALL NO. 1 STEERING KNUCKLE ARM

a.

Install a new steering knuckle king pin dust or ring seal to the No.1 steering knuckle arm.


b.

c.

Using a hammer, install the No. 1 steering knuckle arm into the bearing inner race.


d.

Remove SST from the steering knuckle.


45612A 9.TEMPORARILY INSTALL NO. 2 STEERING KNUCKLE ARM

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the No. 1 steering knuckle arm.


43232 10.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE BEARING CAP

a.

Install the steering knuckle bearing cap with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
96.1 N*m (980 kgf*cm, 71 ft.*lbf)


45611G 11.INSTALL NO. 1 STEERING KNUCKLE ARM

a.

Install the No. 1 steering knuckle arm with the 4 cone washers and 4 nuts.

Torque:
123 N*m (1254 kgf*cm, 91 ft.*lbf)


45612A 12.INSTALL NO. 2 STEERING KNUCKLE ARM

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the No. 1 steering knuckle arm.


45611F 13.MEASURE BEARINGS PRELOAD

a.
1.802,1.177 2.26,1.375 0.458,0.198 10 false 90°

Using a spring tension gauge, measure the preload.

Preload (at starting):

25 to 44 N (3 to 4 kgf, 5.6 to 9.9 lbf)



45611F 14.ADJUST BEARINGS PRELOAD

a.

Remove the steering knuckle bearing cap and No. 1 steering knuckle adjust shim.


b.

Select the No. 1 steering knuckle adjust shim.

Adjusting Shim Thickness:
Thickness mm (in.)
0.1 (0.004)
0.2 (0.008)
0.5 (0.020)
1.0 (0.039)



c.

Install the steering knuckle bearing cap with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
96.1 N*m (980 kgf*cm, 71 ft.*lbf)


43241 15.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE OIL SEAL COVER

a.

Install the 2 steering knuckle oil seal covers to the steering knuckle with the 6 bolts.

Torque:
5.4 N*m (55 kgf*cm, 48 in.*lbf)


45047 16.CONNECT TIE ROD END SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Connect the tie rod end to the No. 1 steering knuckle arm with the nut.

Torque:
90.7 N*m (925 kgf*cm, 67 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Tighten the nut up to an additional 60° if the holes for the cotter pin are not aligned.



b.

Install a new cotter pin.


17.INSTALL FRONT AXLE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

a.

Position one flat part of the outer shaft upward and install the front axle shaft assembly.


18.PACK STEERING KNUCKLE WITH GREASE

a.
0.448,1.26 1.24,1.26 true 0.24,1.156 0.552,1.313 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Molybdenum Disulfide Lithium Base Grease, NLGI No. 2


Pack molybdenum disulfide lithium base grease, NLGI No. 2 into the steering knuckle to about 3 fourths of the steering knuckle.


43401 19.INSTALL STEERING KNUCKLE SPINDLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Place a new knuckle spindle gasket in position on the steering knuckle and install the knuckle spindle.


b.

Place the front disc brake dust cover, new front axle hub dust seal and a new knuckle spindle oil retainer gasket on the steering knuckle spindle.


c.

Install the 8 bolts.

Torque:
46.6 N*m (475 kgf*cm, 34 ft.*lbf)


20.CONNECT FRONT SPEED SENSOR LH (w/ ABS)

a.

Connect the front speed sensor LH to the steering knuckle with the bolt.

Torque:
12.8 N*m (131 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

PROCEDURE

1.CHECK BRAKE SHOE LINING THICKNESS

a.

Remove the shoe inspection hole plug, and check the shoe lining thickness through the hole.

Minimum thickness:

1.0 mm (0.0394 in.)


If the thickness is less than the minimum, replace the shoe.

EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  SIDE STEP(for Double Cab)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedures for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

51774A 1.REMOVE FRONT SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE COVER LH (w/o Over Fender)

a.
1.24,0.583 1.458,0.823 0.219,0.24 10 false A

Remove the 4 rivets.

i.

Put a 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit into a drill.


ii.

Wind tape around the drill bit approximately 5 mm (0.197 in.) from the tip of the drill, as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Tape the 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit to prevent the drill bit from going too deep.


Standard:
Area Specified Condition
A 5 mm (0.197 in.)



iii.

Lightly press the drill bit against the rivets, drill off the flanges of the rivets, and remove the 4 rivets.

NOTICE:
·

Pressing the drill too firmly will cause the rivet to turn and result in the rivet not being drilled through.

·

Do not pry the rivets with the drill, as this may cause damage to the installation holes of the rivets or the drill bit.



CAUTION:

Be careful of the drilled rivets, as they may become hot.



iv.

Using a vacuum cleaner, remove the rivet fragments and shavings from the drilled areas.



b.

Remove the 2 bolts and front side door step plate cover LH.

1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  MASS AIR FLOW METER(w/ DPF)  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

22204 1.CHECK MASS AIR FLOW METER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Check the mass air flow value.

i.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Turn the GTS on.


iv.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine / Data List / Primary / MAF.

Powertrain > Engine and ECT > Data List
Tester Display
MAF
50003 234 3

v.

Blow air into the mass air flow meter sub-assembly and check that the mass air flow value fluctuates. If the result is not as specified, check the wiring and ECM

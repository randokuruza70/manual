SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Separate Seat Type with Inner Belt Attached)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the RH side.



PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Place the front seat assembly RH in the vehicle.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.



b.

Attach the wire harness clamp and connect the connector.


c.

Temporarily install the front seat assembly RH with the 4 bolts.


d.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the rearmost position.


e.

f.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the frontmost position.

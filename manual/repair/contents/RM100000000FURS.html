RM25T0E _52 Drivetrain _029907 AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL _0138022 REAR AXLE SHAFT A

AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  REAR AXLE SHAFT  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

42312 1.REMOVE REAR AXLE SHAFT LH

a.

Remove the 6 nuts and 6 washers.


b.

Using a brass bar and hammer, tap out the bolts and remove the 6 cone washers.


c.

Install the 2 service bolts to the service hole.


d.

Remove the rear axle shaft together with the rear axle shaft outer flange gasket.


e.

Remove the 2 service bolts from the rear axle shaft.

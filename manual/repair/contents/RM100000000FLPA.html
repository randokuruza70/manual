23250 1.INSPECT FUEL INJECTOR ASSEMBLY

a.

Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:
Tester Connection Condition Specified Condition
1 - 2 20°C (68°F) 11.6 to 12.4 Ω


If the result is not as specified, replace the fuel injector assembly.


23250 2.INSPECT INJECTION VOLUME AND LEAKAGE

a.

Check the injector injection.

CAUTION:
·

This test involves high-pressure fuel and electricity.

·

Take every precaution regarding safe handling of both the fuel and the electrical parts.

·

Perform this test in a safe area, and avoid any sparks or flame.

·

Do not smoke.



i.

Discharge the fuel system pressure.

b.

Check for fuel leakage.

i.

In the condition above, disconnect the tester probes of SST (wire) from the battery and check for fuel leakage from each fuel injector assembly.

Standard:

1 drop or less per 17 minutes


If the leakage is not as specified, replace the fuel injector assembly.

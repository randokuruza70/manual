SEAT BELT  FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Bench Seat Type)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedures listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

73230 1.REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Loosen the bolt and the 2 seat belt floor anchors from the vehicle body.


b.

Disconnect the floor anchor of the front seat inner belt assembly RH from the floor anchor of the front seat lap outer belt sub-assembly.


c.

Pull out the front seat inner belt assembly RH through the hole of the front seat cushion, and remove the front seat inner belt assembly RH.

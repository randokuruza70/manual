PROCEDURE

11301K 1.INSTALL CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR

a.

Apply a light coat of engine oil to the O-ring of the crankshaft position sensor.

HINT:
·

When reusing the crankshaft position sensor, inspect the O-ring.

·

If the O-ring has scratches or cuts, replace the crankshaft position sensor.




b.

Install the crankshaft position sensor with the bolt to the timing chain cover sub-assembly.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


c.

Connect the crankshaft position sensor wire harness clamp.


d.

Connect the connector to the crankshaft position sensor.

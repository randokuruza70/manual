1HZ FUEL  FUEL TANK(for 90 L Tank Carried in Middle)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN FUEL


a.

Loosen the fuel tank drain plug, gasket and drain the fuel.


b.

Install the fuel tank drain plug and a new gasket.

Torque:
6.5 N*m (66 kgf*cm, 58 in.*lbf)

77207B 5.REMOVE NO. 3 FUEL MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

for Single Tank Type:

i.

Disconnect the 4 fuel hoses.


ii.

Remove the bolt and No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.



b.

for Double Tank Type:

i.

Disconnect the 4 fuel hoses.


ii.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.



6.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


7.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


77213 8.DISCONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the inlet fuel hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100D 9.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100 10.REMOVE FUEL TANK

a.

Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.


b.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel tank.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts and separate the 2 fuel tank bands.


d.

Slightly lower the engine lifter.


e.

Remove the fuel tank protector from the fuel tank assembly.


77026A 11.REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL EVAPORATION TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 8 screws, 2 nuts and No. 1 fuel evaporation tube sub-assembly from the fuel tank assembly.


b.

Remove the 2 gaskets from the No. 1 evaporation tube sub-assembly.


77380 12.REMOVE FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

77420 13.REMOVE NO. 2 FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

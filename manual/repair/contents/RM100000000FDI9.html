PROCEDURE

25685 1.INSTALL NO. 1 EGR COOLER GASKET

a.

Install a new No. 1 EGR cooler gasket to the EGR cooler assembly.


25630 2.INSTALL NO. 2 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the No. 2 EGR valve assembly to the EGR cooler assembly.


25620 3.INSTALL NO. 1 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the No. 1 EGR valve assembly to the EGR cooler assembly.


16268A 4.CONNECT WATER BY-PASS PIPE HOSE

a.

Connect the water by-pass pipe hose to the No. 2 EGR valve assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


16294 5.CONNECT NO. 11 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Connect the No. 11 water by-pass hose to the No. 1 EGR valve assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


16297B 6.INSTALL NO. 9 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Install the No. 9 water by-pass hose to the No. 1 EGR valve assembly and No. 2 EGR valve assembly, and slide the 2 clamps to secure the hose.


16281 7.INSTALL NO. 4 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Install the No. 4 water by-pass hose to the No. 3 water by-pass pipe, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


25613 8.INSTALL NO. 3 EGR PIPE

a.

Install 2 new gaskets.


b.

Install the No. 3 EGR pipe with the 4 nuts and 4 bolts.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)

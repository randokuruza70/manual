1HZ INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST PIPE  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves when removing the exhaust pipe.

·

The exhaust pipe is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.

·

Confirm that the exhaust pipe has cooled down before removing it.



PROCEDURE

17430 1.REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

for Model Code #J71, #J76 Series:

Remove the 2 bolts and 2 nuts holding the center exhaust pipe assembly to the tailpipe assembly.


b.

for Model Code #J78 Series:

Remove the 3 bolts and 3 nuts holding the center exhaust pipe assembly to the tailpipe assembly.


c.

for Model Code #J79 Series:

i.

except Double Cab:

Remove the 3 bolts and 3 nuts holding the center exhaust pipe assembly to the tailpipe assembly.


ii.

for Double Cab:

Remove the 3 bolts and 3 nuts holding the No. 2 center exhaust pipe assembly to the tailpipe assembly.



d.

Remove the 4 nuts from the 2 exhaust pipe support cushions and then separate the tailpipe assembly from the vehicle body.


e.

for Model Code #J78, #J79 Series:

Remove the 2 nuts from the support bracket.


f.

Remove the gasket from the center exhaust pipe assembly.


g.

Remove the 4 nuts and 2 exhaust pipe support cushions.


h.

for Model Code #J76, #J78, #J79 Series:

Remove the 2 bolts and exhaust pipe damper from the tailpipe assembly.


i.

for Model Code #J71, #J76 Series:

Remove the 2 nuts, No. 2 exhaust pipe support stay and exhaust pipe clamp from the tailpipe assembly.


17420 2.REMOVE CENTER EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

for Double Cab:

Remove the 2 nuts, No. 2 center exhaust pipe assembly and gasket.


b.

Remove the 2 nuts and 2 bolts holding the front exhaust pipe assembly to the center exhaust pipe assembly.


c.

Remove the 2 nuts from the exhaust pipe support cushion and then remove the center exhaust pipe assembly.


d.

Remove the gasket from the front exhaust pipe assembly.


e.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the vehicle body.


f.

Remove the 2 bolts and exhaust pipe damper from the center exhaust pipe assembly.

a.

Remove the 3 nuts holding the front exhaust pipe assembly from the exhaust manifold.

EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  REAR BUMPER(for Pickup)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

52109A 1.REMOVE REAR SIDE BUMPER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 2 nuts and rear side bumper sub-assembly LH.


52108A 2.REMOVE REAR SIDE BUMPER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the LH side.


52164A 3.REMOVE REAR BUMPER PLATE LH

a.

Remove the 4 bolts, 3 nuts and rear bumper plate LH from the rear bumper sub-assembly LH.


52151E 4.REMOVE REAR BUMPER BAR

a.

Remove the 4 bolts, 3 nuts and rear bumper bar.


52163A 5.REMOVE REAR BUMPER PLATE RH

a.

Remove the 11 bolts, 3 nuts and rear bumper plate RH from the rear bumper arm RH, rear bumper No. 1 plate and rear bumper sub-assembly RH.


52168B 6.REMOVE REAR BUMPER EXTENSION MOUNTING BRACKET LH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and rear bumper extension mounting bracket LH.


52185B 7.REMOVE REAR BUMPER SIDE MOUNTING BRACKET RH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and rear bumper side mounting bracket RH from the rear bumper side support RH.


52463 8.REMOVE REAR BUMPER PAD LH

a.

Remove the bolt and rear bumper pad LH.


52462 9.REMOVE REAR BUMPER PAD RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the LH side.


52581A 10.REMOVE REAR BUMPER FILLER PROTECTOR

HINT:

Use the same procedure for all rear bumper filler protector.


a.

Put a 8 mm (0.315 in.) drill bit into a drill.


b.

Wind tape around the drill bit approximately 5 mm(0.197 in.) from the tip of the drill as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Tape the 8 mm (0.315 in.) drill bit to prevent the drill bit from going too deep.



c.

Lightly press the drill against the rivet and drill off the flanges of the rivet.

CAUTION:

Be careful of the drilled rivet as it may become hot.


NOTICE:
·

Pressing the drill too firmly will cause the rivet to turn and result in the rivet not being drilled through.

·

Do not pry the rivet with the drill because this may cause damage to the installation holes of the rivet or the drill bit.




d.

Remove the rear bumper filler protector.


e.

Using a vacuum cleaner, remove the rivet fragments and shavings from the drilled area.

HINT:
·

If a speed signal of 6 km/h (4 mph) or more is input to the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) with the ignition switch turned to ON and the stop light switch off, the ECU performs self diagnosis of the motor and solenoid circuits.

·

If the normal system code is output (no trouble codes are output), slightly jiggle the connectors, wire harness, and fuses of the brake actuator assembly. Make sure that no DTCs are output.

·

If any DTCs are output while jiggling a connector or wire harness of the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly), inspect and repair the connector or wire harness.

·

The DTCs were probably output due to a bad connection of the connector terminal.

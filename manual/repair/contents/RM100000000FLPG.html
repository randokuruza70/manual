1GR-FE FUEL  FUEL PRESSURE PULSATION DAMPER  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

23270 1.INSTALL FUEL PRESSURE PULSATION DAMPER ASSEMBLY

a.

Apply a light coat of spindle oil or gasoline to a new O-ring and install the O-ring to the fuel pressure pulsation damper assembly.


b.

Install the fuel pressure pulsation damper assembly to the fuel delivery pipe sub-assembly.


c.

Install the fuel pressure regulator holder to the fuel pressure pulsation damper assembly.


2.CONNECT CABLE TO NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

3.INSPECT FOR FUEL LEAK

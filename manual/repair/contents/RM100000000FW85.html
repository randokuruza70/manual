SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS  AIRBAG SYSTEM  B1662/45  Indicator Light Circuit Malfunction  


DESCRIPTION

The indicator light circuit consists of the airbag sensor assembly and combination meter assembly.

When the airbag sensor assembly detects a malfunction in the SRS airbag system, the SRS warning light comes on to inform the driver. It also comes on when the source voltage drops, and automatically goes off in approximately 10 seconds after the source voltage returns to normal (DTC is not stored).

The SRS warning light comes on for 6 seconds after the ignition switch is turned to ON and goes off if the system is normal.

When an open circuit is detected, such as when the connector between the combination meter assembly and airbag sensor assembly is disconnected, the SRS warning light remains on even when approximately 6 seconds elapsed after the ignition switch is turned to ON.

DTC B1662/45 is stored when a malfunction is detected in the indicator light circuit.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
B1662/45 Indicator Light Circuit Malfunction When one of the following conditions is met:
·

The airbag sensor assembly receives an open circuit signal, short to ground signal, or short circuit to ground signal or short circuit to B+ signal in the indicator light circuit for 2 seconds.

·

A combination meter assembly malfunction.

·

An airbag sensor assembly malfunction.


·

Combination meter assembly (SRS warning light)

·

Airbag sensor assembly

·

Instrument panel wire

1VD-FTV LUBRICATION  ENGINE OIL COOLER  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

15781 1.INSTALL NO. 1 OIL COOLER BRACKET

a.

Install 3 new gaskets and the No. 1 oil cooler bracket to the oil pan sub-assembly with the washer and bolt.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


15710 2.INSTALL OIL COOLER ASSEMBLY

a.

Install 4 new O-rings and the oil cooler assembly to the No. 1 oil cooler bracket with the 4 washers and 2 bolts, 2 nuts.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


15690 3.INSTALL OIL COOLER RELIEF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket and the oil cooler relief valve assembly to the No. 1 oil cooler bracket.

Torque:
24.5 N*m (250 kgf*cm, 18 ft.*lbf)

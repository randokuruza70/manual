RM25T0E _59 Vehicle Exterior _029935 WINDOW / GLASS _0137675 QUARTER WINDOW GLASS(for Long Body) A

WINDOW / GLASS  QUARTER WINDOW GLASS(for Long Body)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

62375D 1.REMOVE NO. 1 SIDE WINDOW CLIP (for Front Side)

a.

Remove the No. 1 side window clip.


62775B 2.REMOVE QUARTER WINDOW CLIP LH (for Rear Side)

a.

Remove the quarter window clip LH.


3.REMOVE QUARTER WINDOW GLASS ASSEMBLY LH (for Front Side)

a.

Remove the 4 clips.


b.

Using suction cups and remove the quarter window glass assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the quarter window glass .



4.REMOVE QUARTER WINDOW GLASS ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Side)

a.

Remove the 4 clips.


b.

Using suction cups and remove the quarter window glass assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the quarter window glass .



62252A 5.REMOVE SIDE WINDOW WEATHERSTRIP LH (for Fixed Type, for Front Side)

a.

Remove the side window weatherstrip LH.


62742 6.REMOVE QUARTER WINDOW WEATHERSTRIP LH (for Fixed Type, for Rear Side)

a.

Remove the quarter window weatherstrip LH.


62211F 7.REMOVE SIDE WINDOW REAR GLASS LH (for Fixed Type, for Front Side)

a.

Remove the side window rear glass LH.


62712 8.REMOVE QUARTER WINDOW GLASS LH (for Fixed Type, for Rear Side)

a.

Remove the quarter window glass LH.

EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  SIDE STEP  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedures for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

51774A 1.REMOVE FRONT SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE COVER LH


a.

Remove the screw, nut and front side door step plate cover LH from the step plate LH.


51759A 2.REMOVE REAR SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE COVER LH


a.

Remove the 2 bolts and rear side door step plate cover LH from the step plate LH.


51793B 3.REMOVE SIDE STEP BRACKET LH


a.

Remove the 4 bolts and side step bracket LH.


51077B 4.REMOVE NO. 6 SIDE STEP BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY LH


a.

Remove the 4 bolts and 2 No. 6 side step bracket sub-assembly LH.


51078B 5.REMOVE NO. 3 SIDE STEP BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY LH


a.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 side step bracket sub-assembly LH.

EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  SIDE STEP(for Double Cab)  REASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedures for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

51078B 1.INSTALL NO. 3 SIDE STEP BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Install the 2 No. 3 side step bracket sub-assembly LH with the 4 bolts.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


51077B 2.INSTALL NO. 6 SIDE STEP BRACKET SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Install the 3 No. 6 side step bracket sub-assembly LH with the 6 bolts.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


51793B 3.INSTALL SIDE STEP BRACKET LH

a.

Install the side step bracket LH with the 4 bolts.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


51759A 4.INSTALL REAR SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE COVER LH

a.

Install the plate cover with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
3.5 N*m (36 kgf*cm, 31 in.*lbf)


51774A 5.INSTALL FRONT SIDE DOOR STEP PLATE COVER LH (w/o Over Fender)

a.

Install the front side door step plate cover LH with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
3.5 N*m (36 kgf*cm, 11 in.*lbf)


b.

Install 4 new rivets.

i.

Using an air riveter or hand riveter with a nose piece, install the plate with 2 new rivets.

HINT:

If the rivet cannot be cut, pull it once and cut it.

1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  SFI SYSTEM  P0724  Brake Switch "B" Circuit High  


DESCRIPTION

The purpose of this circuit is to prevent the engine from stalling while driving with the lock-up torque converter clutch on when the brakes are suddenly applied.

When the brake pedal is depressed, the stop light switch sends a signal to the ECM. Then the ECM cancels the operation of the lock-up clutch while braking is in progress.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0724 Brake Switch "B" Circuit High The stop light switch remains on even when vehicle is driven in a GO (30 km/h (18.63 mph) or more) and STOP (less than 3 km/h (1.86 mph)) pattern 5 times (2 trip detection logic).

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

This DTC indicates that the stop light switch remains ON. When the stop light switch remains ON during GO and STOP driving, the ECM interprets this as a fault in the stop light switch. Then the MIL illuminatesand the ECM stores the DTC. The vehicle must GO (30 km/h (18.63 mph) or more) and STOP (less than3 km/h (1.86 mph)) 5 times for 2 driving cycles in order for the DTC to be output.

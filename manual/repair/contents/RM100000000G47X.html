HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  COOLER EXPANSION VALVE  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

for Sub-cool Accelerator:

When removing the cooler expansion valve, be sure to replace the installation bolts of the cooler expansion valve and the air conditioner tube and accessory assembly with new bolts.

except Sub-cool Accelerator:

When removing the cooler expansion valve, be sure to replace the installation bolts of the cooler expansion valve and the No. 1 cooler refrigerant suction hose and cooler refrigerant liquid pipe C with new bolts.


PROCEDURE

88515 1.INSTALL COOLER EXPANSION VALVE

a.

Apply sufficient compressor oil to 2 new O-rings and the fitting surfaces of the new cooler expansion valve.

Compressor Oil:

ND-OIL 8 or equivalent



b.

Install the 2 O-rings.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-rings and O-ring fitting surfaces free of foreign matter.



c.

Temporarily install the cooler expansion valve.


d.

Using a 4 mm hexagon wrench, install the cooler expansion valve with the 2 hexagon bolts.

Torque:
3.5 N*m (36 kgf*cm, 31 in.*lbf)


88716C 2.INSTALL COOLER REFRIGERANT LIQUID PIPE C (except Sub-cool Accelerator)

ND-OIL 8 or equivalent



c.

Install the O-ring to the No. 1 cooler refrigerant suction hose.

NOTICE:

Keep the O-rings and O-ring fitting surfaces free of foreign matter.



d.

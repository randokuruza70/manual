1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

89438A 1.INSPECT POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH

a.

Check the power steering oil pressure switch.

i.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Turn the GTS on.


iv.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Data List / Power Steering Signal.

Powertrain > Engine > Data List
Tester Display
Power Steering Signal
50003 61 186

v.

Start the engine.


vi.

Read the value displayed on the GTS.

OK:
Condition Specified Condition
Power steering operating ON
Power steering not operating OFF


If the result is not as specified, check the wire harness and connector. If the wire harness and connector are normal, replace the power steering oil pressure switch.

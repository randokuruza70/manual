RM25T0E _51 Engine / Hybrid System _029759 1HZ ENGINE CONTROL _0135351 ECD SYSTEM C

1HZ ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  71  EGR System Malfunction  


DESCRIPTION

The EGR system recirculates exhaust gas, which is controlled to the proper quantity to suit driving conditions, into the intake air mixture to slow down combustion, reduce the combustion temperature and reduce NOx emissions.

The EGR valve lift varies in accordance with the vacuum which is regulated by the E-VRV for EGR as operated by the emission control ECU.

Under the following conditions, EGR is cut to maintain driveability.

·

Before the engine is warmed up

·

During deceleration

·

Light engine load (amount of intake air very small)

·

Engine speed over 4000 rpm

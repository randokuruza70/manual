3.CHECK VALVE BODY

a.

Install the valve body in the uppermost position.

HINT:

When the brakes are applied, the position will move down by approximately 0.8 mm (0.0315 in.). Even at this time, the piston should not make contact with or move the load sensing spring.


If the result is not as specified, replace the load sensing valve assembly.

3.DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

4.DRAIN FUEL

a.

Loosen the fuel tank drain plug and drain the fuel from the fuel tank assembly.


b.

Install a new gasket and the fuel tank drain plug to the fuel tank assembly.

Torque:
6.5 N*m (66 kgf*cm, 58 in.*lbf)


5.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.

6.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel return hose from the No. 2 fuel main tube.


77213 7.DISCONNECT FUEL INLET HOSE

a.

Loosen the clamp and disconnect the fuel inlet hose from the fuel tank inlet pipe sub-assembly.


77100D 8.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank inlet pipe sub-assembly.


77278A 9.DISCONNECT FUEL EVAPORATION HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel evaporation hose from the No. 2 fuel main tube.


77100 10.REMOVE FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.


b.

Disconnect the fuel pump connector.


c.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel tank assembly.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 pins and disconnect the 2 fuel tank bands.


e.

Slightly lower the engine lifter.


f.

Remove the No. 1 fuel tank protector sub-assembly.


11.REMOVE FUEL HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and remove the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube sub-assembly.


b.

Slide the clamp and remove the fuel evaporation hose from the fuel tank assembly.


c.

Slide the clamp and remove the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank assembly.


d.

Loosen the clamp and remove the fuel inlet hose from the fuel tank filler lower pipe sub-assembly.

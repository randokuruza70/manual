1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  SFI SYSTEM  ECM Power Source Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

When the ignition switch is turned to ON, the battery voltage is applied to IGSW of the ECM. The output signal from the MREL terminal of the ECM causes a current to flow to the coil, closing the contact of the EFI MAIN relay and supplying power to terminals +B and +B2 of the ECM.

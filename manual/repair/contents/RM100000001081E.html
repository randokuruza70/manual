1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  DIFFERENTIAL PRESSURE SENSOR(for DPF)  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

89481B 1.INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL PRESSURE SENSOR

a.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


b.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the air hose from the differential pressure sensor.


c.
0.771,1.25 0.896,1.25 0.896,1.25 1.167,1.906 true 0.427,1.156 0.74,1.333 0.313,0.177 10 true SST

Pressure

Front of the Vehicle


Install SST to the differential pressure sensor.

SST
09992-00242  


d.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


e.

Turn the GTS on.


f.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Data List / DPF Differential Pressure.

Powertrain > Engine and ECT > Data List
Tester Display
DPF Differential Pressure
50003 61 238

g.

Apply pressure to the differential pressure sensor.


h.

Check the pressure value of the differential pressure sensor.

Standard pressure:

Approximately the same pressure as the applied pressure is indicated on the GTS.


NOTICE:

Perform the inspection within the range of 0 to 98 kPa (0 to 1.0 kgf/cm2, 0 to 14.2 psi).


If the result is not as specified, check the differential pressure sensor circuit.


i.

Remove SST from the differential pressure sensor.


j.

Connect the air hose to the differential pressure sensor, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.

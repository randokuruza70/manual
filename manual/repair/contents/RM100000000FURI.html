43530 13.INSPECT FREE WHEEL HUB ASSEMBLY (w/ Free Wheel Hub)

a.

Check the handle moves smoothly.

43530 14.INSTALL FREE WHEEL HUB ASSEMBLY (w/ Free Wheel Hub)

a.

Install a new gasket to the front axle hub.


b.

Install the body with 6 cone washers, washers and new nuts.

Torque:
32.6 N*m (332 kgf*cm, 24 ft.*lbf)


c.

Select the snap ring which ensures the clearance between the tip of the body and the free wheel hub ring is less than 0.2 mm (0.008 in.).

Snap ring thickness:
Mark Thickness

9.8 N*m (100 kgf*cm, 87 in.*lbf)

c.

f.

g.

Remove the bolt.


43423 16.INSTALL FRONT AXLE OUTER SHAFT FLANGE CAP (w/o Free Wheel Hub)

a.

b.

Install the front axle outer shaft flange cap to the front axle outer shaft flange.

18.INSTALL FRONT SPEED SENSOR LH (w/ ABS)

a.

Connect the front speed sensor with the nut.

Torque:
8.5 N*m (87 kgf*cm, 75 in.*lbf)

·

·

·

Do not twist the sensor wire when installing the speed sensor.

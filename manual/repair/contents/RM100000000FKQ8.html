BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM  C1241  Low Power Supply Voltage Malfunction  


DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the IG1 terminal voltage deviates from the DTC detection condition due to a malfunction in the power supply or charging circuit, such as the battery or generator circuit, etc.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1241 Low Power Supply Voltage Malfunction One of the following conditions is met:
1.

At a vehicle speed of 3 km/h (2 mph) or more, the IG1 terminal voltage is less than 9.5 V for 10 seconds or more.

2.

When the solenoid relay remains ON and the IG1 terminal voltage is less than 9.5 V, the relay contact is open for 0.22 seconds or more.

3.

When the motor relay remains ON and the IG1 terminal voltage is less than 9.5 V, the relay contact is open for 0.12 seconds or more.

4.

With the IG1 terminal voltage 9.5 V or less, the vehicle speed sensor power supply decreases for 60 seconds or more.

84140 5.REMOVE HEADLIGHT DIMMER SWITCH ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the connector.


b.

While loosening the band clamp as shown in the illustration and detach the claw.


c.

Pull the headlight dimmer switch assembly to remove it.

NOTICE:

If the claw is pressed forcefully, it will break.

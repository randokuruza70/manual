1GR-FE INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST PIPE(for Model Code #J78, #J79 Series)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves when removing the exhaust pipe assembly.

·

The exhaust pipe assembly is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.

·

Confirm that the exhaust pipe assembly has cooled down before removing it.



PROCEDURE

17430 1.REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 4 nuts and disconnect the 2 exhaust pipe support cushions.


b.

Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the exhaust pipe support cushion from the support bracket.


c.

Remove the 3 bolts, 3 nuts and tailpipe assembly.



d.

Remove the 4 nuts and 2 exhaust pipe support cushions from the tailpipe assembly.


e.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the muffler clamp bracket.


f.

for Double Cab:

Remove the gasket from the No. 2 center exhaust pipe assembly.


g.

except Double Cab:

Remove the gasket from the No. 3 center exhaust pipe assembly.

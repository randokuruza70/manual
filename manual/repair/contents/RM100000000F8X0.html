1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER HEAD  REPAIR  


PROCEDURE

1.REPAIR INTAKE VALVE SEAT


NOTICE:
·

Repair the intake valve seat while checking the seating position.

·

Keep the lip free of foreign matter.



a.

Using a 45° cutter, resurface the intake valve seat so that the intake valve seat width is more than the specification.


b.
2.365,1.479 2.677,1.635 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 2.708,0.396 3.167,0.594 0.458,0.198 10 false 45° 2.385,0.198 2.844,0.396 0.458,0.198 10 false 65° 2.875,0.688 3.333,0.885 0.458,0.198 10 false 20°
*a Width


Using 20° and 65° cutters, correct the intake valve seat so that the valve contacts the entire circumference of the intake valve seat. The contact should be in the center of the intake valve seat, and the intake valve seat width should be maintained within the specified range around the entire circumference of the intake valve seat.

Standard width:

1.0 to 1.4 mm (0.0394 to 0.0551 in.)



c.

Hand-lap the intake valve and valve seat with an abrasive compound.


d.

Check the valve seating position.

NOTICE:
·

Repair the exhaust valve seat while checking the seating position.

·

Keep the lip free of foreign matter.



a.

Using a 45° cutter, resurface the exhaust valve seat so that the exhaust valve seat width is more than the specification.


b.

The contact should be in the center of the exhaust valve seat, and the exhaust valve seat width should be maintained within the specified range around the entire circumference of the exhaust valve seat.

Standard width:

1.0 to 1.4 mm (0.0394 to 0.0551 in.)



c.

Hand-lap the valve and exhaust valve seat with an abrasive compound.


d.

Check the valve seating position.

BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER(w/ ABS)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Make sure to release the vacuum from the brake booster before removing the master cylinder from the brake booster.


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN BRAKE FLUID

NOTICE:

Wash off brake fluid immediately if it comes in contact with any painted surface.


47201 2.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

for LHD:

i.

Disconnect the clutch reservoir tube from the master cylinder.


ii.

Disconnect the brake fluid level warning switch connector.


iii.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 2 brake lines from the master cylinder.


iv.

Remove the 2 nuts and pull out the master cylinder from the brake booster.

NOTICE:

Make sure foreign matter does not adhere to the piston of the master cylinder. If foreign matter adheres, clean it off with a cloth. Then apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the entire outer circumference contact surface area of the piston.



v.

Remove the brake master cylinder gasket from the master cylinder.


vi.

Remove the O-ring from the master cylinder.



b.

for RHD:

i.
1.885,1.104 2.594,0.25 2.594,0.25 2.823,0.25 true 2.865,0.177 3.177,0.375 0.313,0.198 10 false *1
*1 Front No. 1 Brake Tube


Disconnect the brake fluid warning switch connector.


ii.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 5 brake lines from the master cylinder and 3-way.


iii.

Remove the front No. 1 brake tube from the master cylinder and 3-way.


iv.

Remove the nut and 3-way from the bracket.


v.

Remove the 2 nuts and bracket.


vi.

Pull out the master cylinder from the brake booster.

NOTICE:

Make sure foreign matter does not adhere to the piston of the master cylinder. If foreign matter adheres, clean it off with a cloth. Then apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the entire outer circumference contact surface area of the piston.



vii.

Remove the O-ring and gasket from the master cylinder.

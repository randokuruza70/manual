38110 1.INSTALL WINCH ASSEMBLY


a.

Using a crane or equivalent, install the winch assembly to the vehicle.

CAUTION:

Be careful as the winch assembly is very heavy.


NOTICE:
·

Be sure to perform the operation with 2 persons or more.

·

Be careful not to damage the winch assembly.

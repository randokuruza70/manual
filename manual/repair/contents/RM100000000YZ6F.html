BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER(w/o VSC)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

47202 1.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the screw.


b.

Remove the brake master cylinder reservoir from the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


47255 2.REMOVE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR GROMMET


a.

Remove the 2 master cylinder reservoir grommets from the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


47287 3.REMOVE BRAKE FLUID INFORMATION LABEL

47230 4.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR FILLER CAP ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the brake master cylinder reservoir filler cap assembly from the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


47299 5.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR STRAINER

a.

Remove the brake master cylinder reservoir strainer from the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


04493 6.REMOVE BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER KIT

a.

Push in the piston, position the reservoir installation area downwards, and remove the straight pin.

NOTICE:

Use a cloth to prevent parts from becoming damaged or lost.



b.

Push in the piston, and using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring.


c.

Pull out the No. 1 piston straight from the master cylinder body to remove it.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the inside of the cylinder is not damaged.



d.

Lightly strike the flange against wooden blocks until the end of the No. 2 piston protrudes. When the end protrudes, pull the No. 2 piston straight from the master cylinder body.

NOTICE:

Make sure that the inside of the cylinder is not damaged.

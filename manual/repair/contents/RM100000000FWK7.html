BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM PUMP(for 1HZ)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL VACUUM PUMP ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new O-ring.


b.

Install the vacuum pump assembly to the engine with the 2 nuts.

Torque:
39.2 N*m (400 kgf*cm, 29 ft.*lbf)


44774 2.INSTALL UNION TO CONNECTOR TUBE HOSE

a.

Connect the 2 hoses to the vacuum pump.


3.INSTALL VACUUM PUMP TUBE

a.

Install 4 new gaskets and the vacuum pump tube to the vacuum pump and cylinder block with the 2 union bolts.

Torque:
13.7 N*m (140 kgf*cm, 10 ft.*lbf)


b.

Install the flange bolt to the vacuum pump tube.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)

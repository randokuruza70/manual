SEAT  REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Semi-long Body)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the LH and RH side.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

71086B 1.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for LH Side)

a.

Remove the 2 pieces of fastening tape.


b.

Remove the 14 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear side seat cushion cover sub-assembly LH from the rear side frame assembly LH.


71085B 2.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH (for RH Side)

a.

Remove the 2 pieces of fastening tape.


b.

Remove the 15 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear side seat cushion cover sub-assembly RH from the rear side frame assembly RH.


71722 3.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION PAD LH


a.

Remove the rear side seat cushion pad LH.


71089B 4.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEATBACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH


a.

Remove the 5 hog rings.


b.

While pulling up the rear side seatback cover sub-assembly LH, remove the 4 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear side seatback cover sub-assembly LH.


71761B 5.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEATBACK PAD LH


a.

Remove the 6 hog rings and rear side seatback pad LH from the rear side seat frame assembly LH.


71034A 6.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH


a.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 E-rings and 2 pins.


b.

Remove the rear No. 1 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH.

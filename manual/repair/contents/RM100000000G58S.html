SEAT  REAR NO. 2 SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Long Body)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the LH and RH side.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

71086B 1.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear No. 2 Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 18 hog rings.



b.

w/o Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 2 pieces of fastening tape.


ii.

Remove the 16 hog rings.



c.

Remove the rear side seat cushion cover sub-assembly LH and rear No. 2 side seat cushion pad LH.


71089C 2.REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SIDE SEATBACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear No. 2 Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.

Remove the 6 hog rings.


b.

While pulling up the rear No. 2 side seatback cover sub-assembly LH, remove the 4 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear No. 2 side seatback cover sub-assembly LH.


d.

Remove the 7 hog rings and rear No. 2 side seatback pad LH.


72660A 3.REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION SUPPORT ASSEMBLY (for Rear No. 2 Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 spacers, and rear seat cushion support assembly from the rear No. 2 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH.



79929A 4.REMOVE NO. 2 FOLD SEAT LEG SUPPORT FRAME (for Rear No. 2 Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.
2.25,0.927 2.479,0.448 false 2.448,0.229 2.76,0.427 0.313,0.198 10 false *1
*1 Clip Remover


w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Using clip remover, remove the 2 spring nuts and 4 plate washers.


ii.

Remove the No. 2 fold seat leg support frame.



71034B 5.REMOVE REAR NO. 2 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear No. 2 Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 E-rings and 2 pins.


b.

Remove the rear No. 2 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH from the rear No. 2 side seat frame assembly LH.


71086B 6.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEAT CUSHION COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 18 hog rings.



b.

w/o Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the Fastening tape.


ii.

Remove the 16 hog rings.



c.

Remove the rear side seat cushion cover sub-assembly LH and rear side seat cushion pad LH.


71089B 7.REMOVE REAR SIDE SEATBACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.

Remove the 6 hog rings.


b.

While pulling up the rear side seatback cover sub-assembly LH, remove the 4 hog rings.


c.

Remove the rear side seatback cover sub-assembly LH from the rear side seat frame assembly LH.


d.

Remove the 7 hog rings and rear side seatback pad LH.


72660A 8.REMOVE REAR SEAT CUSHION SUPPORT ASSEMBLY (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 spacers, and rear seat cushion support assembly from the rear No. 1 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH.



79929 9.REMOVE FOLD SEAT LEG STOPPER FRAME (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.
2.25,0.927 2.479,0.448 false 2.448,0.229 2.76,0.427 0.313,0.198 10 false *1
*1 Clip Remover


w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Using clip remover, remove the 2 spring nuts and 4 plate washers.


ii.

Remove the fold seat leg stopper frame.



71034A 10.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 E-rings and 2 pins.



b.

w/o Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Using a screwdriver, remove the 2 E-rings, 2 pins and 2 torsion springs.



c.

Remove the rear No. 1 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH.

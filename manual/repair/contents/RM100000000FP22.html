SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Separate Seat Type)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the front seat inner track assembly LH or front seat outer track assembly LH due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.


HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the RH side.



PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY

a.

Place the front seat assembly in the cabin.

NOTICE:
·

Before placing the seat in the vehicle, operate the seat track adjusting handle to slide the seat track to the center position.

·

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.




b.

Temporarily install the front seat assembly with the 4 bolts.


c.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the rearmost position.


d.

Tighten the 2 bolts.

Torque:
40 N*m (408 kgf*cm, 30 ft.*lbf)


e.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the foremost position.


f.

Tighten the 2 bolts.

Torque:
40 N*m (408 kgf*cm, 30 ft.*lbf)


g.

During sliding operation of the front seat, check that the left and right adjusters move together smoothly and lock simultaneously. If the seat adjusters do not lock simultaneously, loosen the seat mounting bolts to adjust the adjuster position.


72137 2.INSTALL OUTER SEAT TRACK COVER RH (except Walk in Seat Type, w/ Seat Track Cover)

a.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the foremost position.


b.

Attach the guide and 2 claws to install the outer seat track cover RH.

72137 4.INSTALL OUTER SEAT TRACK COVER RH (for Walk in Seat Type)

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the foremost position.


b.

Attach the 2 guides to install the outer seat track cover RH.


c.

Install the clip.

SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM DESCRIPTION


*: w/ Front Passenger Side Seat Belt Warning System


a.

Seat belt warning light operation for driver side seat belt:

The seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly illuminates, blinks or turns off in accordance with the driver side seat belt state, vehicle speed, shift lever position and parking brake state.


b.

Seat belt warning light operation for front passenger side seat belt*:

The seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly illuminates, blinks or turns off in accordance with whether the front passenger seat is occupied, the front passenger side seat belt state, vehicle speed, shift lever position and parking brake state.


c.

Seat belt warning buzzer (except 1VD-FTV):

After turning the ignition switch to ON, if the driver side seat belt is not fastened within 1.8 seconds, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly will sound at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 6 seconds.

When the vehicle is driven at 20 km/h (12 mph) or more with the driver side seat belt not fastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds once.

When 24 seconds have passed after the buzzer sounded, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly will sound at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 9.6 seconds.

After that, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly will sound at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 20 seconds.


d.

Seat belt warning buzzer (for 1VD-FTV):

When the vehicle is driven at 20 km/h (12 mph) or more with the front passenger seat occupied and the driver or front passenger seat belt not fastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds once.

When 30 seconds have passed after the buzzer sounded, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 second for 30 seconds.

After that, the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 90 seconds.



FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENT


The following functions are available.
Components Function
Front seat inner belt assembly LH*1
Front seat inner belt assembly RH*2 		Informs combination meter assembly of driver side seat belt condition (fastened or unfastened).
Front seat inner belt assembly LH*3 Informs the occupant detection ECU of the front passenger side seat belt condition (fastened or unfastened).
Occupant detection sensor*3 Informs combination meter assembly of front passenger seat condition (occupied or unoccupied)
Seat belt warning light for Driver Side:
If driver side seat belt is unfastened and ignition switch is ON, combination meter assembly flashes seat belt warning light in combination meter assembly as reminder.
for Front Passenger Side*3:
When front passenger seat is occupied and ignition switch is ON, if front passenger side seat belt is unfastened, combination meter assembly flashes front seat belt warning light of combination meter assembly as reminder
HINT:

The seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly is used for both the driver side seat and front passenger side seat.


Combination meter assembly (seat belt warning buzzer) If vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more while the driver or front passenger side seat belt*3 is unfastened, the seat belt warning buzzer in combination meter assembly sounds as reminder.

*1: for LHD

*2: for RHD

*3: w/ Front Passenger Side Seat Belt Warning System

BF1B TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD  TRANSFER CONTROL SOLENOID  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

85440A 1.INSTALL FOUR WHEEL DRIVE CONTROL SOLENOID ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

If the four wheel drive control solenoid assembly is dropped or subjected to a strong impact, replace it with a new one.


a.

Connect the 4 front drive shift vacuum hoses to the four wheel drive control solenoid assembly.

Hose Insert Length:

9.7 to 10.0 mm (0.3819 to 0.3937 in.)


NOTICE:

Make sure the front drive shift vacuum hoses are connected to the correct locations.



b.

Attach the guide to temporarily install the four wheel drive control solenoid assembly.


c.

Install the bolt.

Torque:
12.5 N*m (127 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)


d.

Connect the 2 connectors to the four wheel drive control solenoid assembly.

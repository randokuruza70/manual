1VD-FTV INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST PIPE(except 5 Door)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves when removing the exhaust pipe.

·

The exhaust pipe is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.

·

Confirm that the exhaust pipe has cooled down before removing it.



PROCEDURE

17430 1.REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 3 bolts and 3 nuts.


b.

Remove the 4 nuts from the 2 exhaust pipe support cushions. Then remove the tailpipe assembly.



c.

Remove the 4 nuts and 2 exhaust pipe support cushions from the tailpipe assembly.


d.

Remove the gasket from the center exhaust pipe assembly.

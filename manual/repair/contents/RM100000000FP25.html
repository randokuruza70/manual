SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Bench Seat Type)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the front inner seat track assembly RH or front outer seat track assembly RH due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.


HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

a.

Remove the 2 screws and reclining adjuster cover RH.

a.

74841B 3.REMOVE FRONT SEATBACK PANEL (w/ Seatback Panel)

a.

Remove the 4 screws and front seatback panel from the front seatback assembly.


b.

Remove the front seatback panel ornament and clip from the front seatback panel.


71073S 4.REMOVE BENCH TYPE FRONT SEATBACK COVER

a.

Remove the 4 hog rings.


b.

Remove the hog ring.


c.

Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 front seat headrest support assemblies.


d.

Remove the 3 hog rings and bench type front seatback cover from the bench type front seatback pad.


71551S 5.REMOVE BENCH TYPE FRONT SEATBACK PAD

a.

Remove the bench type front seatback pad from seatback frame sub-assembly.


72510D 6.REMOVE OUTER RECLINING SEAT ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and outer reclining seat adjuster assembly RH from the front seatback frame sub-assembly RH.


72120 7.REMOVE FRONT OUTER SEAT TRACK ASSEMBLY RH (except Walk in Seat Type)

a.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the rearmost position.


b.

Remove the pin from the outer seat track assembly RH.


c.

Remove the bolt and front outer seat track assembly RH.


d.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the foremost position.


e.

Remove the bolt and front outer seat track assembly RH.


72130A 8.REMOVE FRONT INNER SEAT TRACK ASSEMBLY RH (except Walk in Seat Type)

a.

Move the front inner seat track assembly RH to the rearmost position.


b.

Remove the bolt.


c.

Move the front inner seat track assembly RH to the foremost position.


d.

Remove the bolt and front inner seat track assembly RH.


71072S 9.REMOVE BENCH TYPE SEAT CUSHION COVER

a.

except Walk in Seat Type:

Detach the clip and remove the 10 hog rings.


b.

for Walk in Seat Type:

Detach the clip and remove the 10 hog rings.


c.

Remove the 3 hog rings and bench type seat cushion cover from the bench type front seat cushion pad.


71512S 10.REMOVE BENCH TYPE FRONT SEAT CUSHION PAD

a.

Remove the bench type front seat cushion pad from the bench type front seat cushion spring assembly.


71521A 11.REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SILENCER PAD

a.

Remove the 4 hog rings and 2 front seat cushion silencer pads.


71537A 12.REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION PROTECTOR

a.

Remove the front seat cushion protector.


72053C 13.REMOVE RECLINING REMOTE CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and reclining remote control lever sub-assembly RH from the bench type front seat cushion spring assembly.

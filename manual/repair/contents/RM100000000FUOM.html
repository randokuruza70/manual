SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS  FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

89174A 1.INSTALL FRONT AIRBAG SENSOR LH

a.

Check that the ignition switch is off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the battery negative (-) terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.



c.

Attach the guide and hold the front airbag sensor LH in place.


d.

Install the nut.

Torque:
9.0 N*m (92 kgf*cm, 80 in.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

If the front airbag sensor LH has been dropped, or there are any cracks, dents or other defects in the case, bracket or connector, replace it with a new one.

·

When installing the front airbag sensor LH, be careful that the SRS wiring does not interfere with other parts and that it is not pinched between other parts.

·

Tighten the nut while holding the front airbag sensor LH because the front airbag sensor LH guide is easily damaged.




e.

Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.


i.

Before connecting the connector, check that the position of the housing lock is correct as shown in the illustration.

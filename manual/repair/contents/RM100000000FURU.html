AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  REAR AXLE SHAFT  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

42312C 1.INSTALL REAR AXLE SHAFT OIL SEAL LH

a.
2.708,1.115 2.771,0.729 2.771,0.729 2.906,0.729 true 2.958,0.646 3.344,0.865 0.385,0.219 10 true SST

Using SST and a hammer, tap in a new rear axle shaft oil seal LH.

SST
09517-36010  


b.

Apply MP grease to the oil seal lip.


42312 2.INSTALL REAR AXLE SHAFT LH

a.

Install a new rear axle shaft outer flange gasket to the rear axle hub.


b.

Install the rear axle shaft to the rear axle housing.


c.

Install the 6 cone washers, 6 spring washers and 6 nuts to the rear axle hub.

Torque:
32.6 N*m (332 kgf*cm, 24 ft.*lbf)

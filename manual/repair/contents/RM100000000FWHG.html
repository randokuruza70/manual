LIGHTING (EXT)  REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY(for Pickup)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

81563C 1.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS LH

a.

Remove the rear combination light lens LH from the rear combination light body.


81550D 2.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT BULB (for Rear Turn Signal Light)

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Push

Counterclockwise


Push the rear combination light bulb in the direction indicated by the black arrow shown in the illustration, turn the bulb counterclockwise, and then remove the bulb from the rear combination light body.


81550B 3.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT BULB (for Tail and Stop Light)

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Push

Counterclockwise


Push the side rear combination light bulb in the direction indicated by the black arrow shown in the illustration, turn the bulb counterclockwise, and then remove the bulb from the rear combination light body.


81550D 4.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT BULB (for Backup Light)

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Push

Counterclockwise


Push the rear combination light bulb in the direction indicated by the black arrow shown in the illustration, turn the bulb counterclockwise, and then remove the bulb from the rear combination light body.


81562 5.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS GASKET LH

a.

Remove the rear combination light lens gasket LH from the rear combination light body.

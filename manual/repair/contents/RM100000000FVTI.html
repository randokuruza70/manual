44105C 2.INSTALL VALVE BODY HOUSING UNION SEAT

a.

Using a plastic-faced hammer and extension bar, tap in 2 new valve body housing union seats.

44114A 3.INSTALL POWER PISTON NUT RING ("TEFLON" RING)

a.

Apply power steering fluid to a new O-ring.


b.

Install the O-ring to the power piston nut.


c.

Expand a new power piston nut ring ("Teflon" Ring) with your fingers.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to over-expand the power piston nut ring ("Teflon" Ring).



d.

Install the power piston nut ring ("Teflon" Ring) to the power piston nut.


e.

Coat the power piston nut ring ("Teflon" Ring) with power steering fluid and snug it down with piston ring compressor for 5 to 7 minutes.

44154 6.INSTALL POWER PISTON PLUNGER GUIDE NUT

a.

Apply power steering fluid to a new O-ring.


b.

Install the O-ring to the plunger guide nut.


c.

Install the power piston plunger, power piston plunger guide and power piston plunger spring.


d.

Install the plunger guide nut with hexagon wrench.

Torque:
20 N*m (204 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


e.

Stake the plunger guide nut.


44111 7.INSTALL CROSS SHAFT

a.

Apply power steering fluid to a new O-ring.


b.

Install the O-ring on the side cover sub-assembly.


c.

Using a screwdriver, assemble the cross shaft to the side cover sub-assembly.

HINT:

Fully loosen the sector shaft adjusting screw.



d.

Set the worm gear at the center of the power steering gear housing.


e.

Insert and push the cross shaft into the power steering gear housing so that the center teeth mesh together.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the oil seal.



44108 8.INSTALL SIDE COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the 4 bolts, Tighten the bolts in a diagonal pattern.

Torque:
61 N*m (622 kgf*cm, 45 ft.*lbf)

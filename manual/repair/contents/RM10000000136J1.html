DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0106 Manifold Absolute Pressure / Barometric Pressure Circuit Range / Performance Problem Either condition is met (2 trip detection logic):
(a) The manifold absolute pressure output is 2.5 V or higher for 10 seconds when idling the engine after warming it up.
(b) The manifold absolute pressure sensor output is 0.25 V or less for 10 seconds when the accelerator opening amount is 15% or more and the engine speed is 6000 rpm or less.

·

DTC No. DTC Detection Drive Pattern

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

If DTCs P0107 and P0108 are output simultaneously, the manifold absolute pressure sensor circuit may have an open or short. Perform troubleshooting for these DTCs first.

·

Read freeze frame data using the GTS. Freeze frame data records the engine condition when malfunctions are detected. When troubleshooting, freeze frame data can help determine if the vehicle was moving or stationary, if the engine was warmed up or not, and other data from the time the malfunction occurred.

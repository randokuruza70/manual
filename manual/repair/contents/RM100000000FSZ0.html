Check the door lock actuator operation.

i.

Apply battery voltage to the actuator connector and check the operation of the door lock actuator.

OK:
Measurement Condition Specified Condition
Battery positive (+) → Terminal 2
Battery negative (-) → Terminal 1 		Lock
Battery positive (+) → Terminal 1
Battery negative (-) → Terminal 2 		Unlock


If the result is not as specified, replace the rear door lock actuator assembly LH.

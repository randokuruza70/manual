TIRE PRESSURE MONITORING  TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM  C2174/74  Vehicle Speed Signal Malfunction  C2191/91  Vehicle Speed Signal Error (Test Mode DTC)  


DESCRIPTION

Speeds signals are sent from the combination meter assembly to the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver.

DTC C2191/91 is stored only during the signal check.

PROCEDURE

83520 1.INSPECT OIL PRESSURE SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

a.
1.615,1.052 1.896,0.802 1.896,0.802 2.083,0.802 true 2.125,0.729 2.24,0.906 0.115,0.177 10 false 1 0.052,0.042 0.365,0.198 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 1.271,2.01 1.583,2.167 0.313,0.156 10 false *b
*a Component without harness connected
(Oil Pressure Sender Gauge Assembly)
*b Test LED


Disconnect the oil pressure sender gauge assembly connector.


b.

Apply battery positive (+) voltage to the oil pressure sender gauge assembly terminal through a test LED.


c.

Check that the LED does not illuminate when the engine is stopped.


d.

Check that the LED flashes when the engine is running. The number of flashes varies with engine speed.

If the result is not as specified, replace the oil pressure sender gauge assembly.


e.

Connect the oil pressure sender gauge assembly connector.

HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  REAR HEATER UNIT(for Quarter Panel)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

87212A 1.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 AIR DUCT

a.

Remove the 6 screws and rear No. 1 air duct.


87256F 2.REMOVE HEATER UNIT REAR HEATER WATER OUTLET HOSE

a.

Using pliers, grip the claws of the clip and slide the clip to disconnect the heater unit rear heater water outlet hose.

NOTICE:
·

Do not apply excessive force to the water hose and heater unit rear heater water outlet hose.

·

Prepare a drain pan or cloth in case the coolant leaks.




87255F 3.REMOVE HEATER UNIT REAR HEATER WATER INLET HOSE

a.

Using pliers, grip the claws of the clip and slide the clip to disconnect the heater unit rear heater water inlet hose.

NOTICE:
·

Do not apply excessive force to the water hose and heater unit rear heater water inlet hose.

·

Prepare a drain pan or cloth in case the coolant leaks.

a.

Remove the 2 screws and rear heater blower resistor.


87107R 5.REMOVE REAR HEATER UNIT SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 6 screws and air duct assembly.


b.

Disconnect the snap and remove the shaft as shown in the illustration.


c.

Remove the screw and clamp clip.


d.

Remove the water unit valve assembly.


e.

Remove the screw and valve lever.


f.

Remove the 4 screws and case.


g.

Remove the rear heater unit sub-assembly.


87105R 6.REMOVE REAR HEATER BLOWER FAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the nut and rear heater blower fan sub-assembly.


b.

Remove the washer.


87104R 7.REMOVE REAR HEATER BLOWER MOTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 3 screws and rear heater blower motor sub-assembly.

RM25T0E _58 Vehicle Interior _029926 LIGHTING (INT) _0141623 ROOM LIGHT BULB A

LIGHTING (INT)  ROOM LIGHT BULB  REPLACEMENT  


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE DOME NO. 1 FUSE

a.

Remove the dome No. 1 fuse.


81241 2.REMOVE NO. 1 ROOM LIGHT LENS

a.
1.313,1.854 1.167,1.521 true 1.302,1.885 1.521,2.031 0.219,0.146 10 true *1
*1 Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws and remove the No. 1 room light lens.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



81240B 3.REMOVE NO. 1 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Remove the No. 1 room light bulb.


81240B 4.INSTALL NO. 1 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Install the No. 1 room light bulb.


81241 5.INSTALL NO. 1 ROOM LIGHT LENS

a.

Attach the 3 claws to install the No. 1 room light lens.


81241A 6.REMOVE NO. 2 ROOM LIGHT LENS

a.
1.313,1.854 1.167,1.521 true 1.302,1.885 1.521,2.031 0.219,0.146 10 true *1
*1 Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, detach the 3 claws and remove the No. 2 room light lens.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



81240E 7.REMOVE NO. 2 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Remove the No. 2 room light bulb.


81240E 8.INSTALL NO. 2 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Install the No. 2 room light bulb.


81241A 9.INSTALL NO. 2 ROOM LIGHT LENS

a.

Attach the 3 claws to install the No. 2 room light lens.


81290 10.DISCONNECT NO. 3 ROOM LIGHT ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 screws and disconnect the No. 3 room light assembly.


81290B 11.REMOVE NO. 3 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Remove the No. 3 room light bulb.


81290B 12.INSTALL NO. 3 ROOM LIGHT BULB

a.

Install the No. 3 room light bulb.


81290 13.CONNECT NO. 3 ROOM LIGHT ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the 2 screws to connect the No. 3 room light assembly.


14.INSTALL DOME NO. 1 FUSE

a.

Install the dome No. 1 fuse.

1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  THROTTLE BODY  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

22030 1.CHECK THROTTLE BODY WITH MOTOR ASSEMBLY

a.

Check the throttle actuator operating sounds.

i.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


ii.

When depressing the accelerator pedal, check the operating sound of the running motor. Make sure that no friction noises are emitted from the throttle actuator. If friction noise exists, replace the throttle body with motor assembly.



b.

Check the throttle position sensor.

i.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Turn the GTS on.


iv.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Data List / Throttle Sensor Position.

Powertrain > Engine > Data List
Tester Display
Throttle Sensor Position
50003 61 72

v.

Depress the accelerator pedal. When the throttle valve is fully opened, check that the value of "Throttle Sensor Position" is within the specification.

Standard throttle valve opening percentage:

60% or more


HINT:

When checking the standard throttle valve opening percentage, the shift lever should be in N.


If the percentage is less than 60%, replace the throttle body with motor assembly.

5.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SUPPORT ASSEMBLY

a.

for 1VD-FTV:

Disconnect the clutch switch connector.


b.

Remove the bolt and clutch pedal support assembly.


31301R 6.REMOVE TURN OVER SPRING SEAT COMPRESSION SPRING

a.

Move the pedal to the full stroke position.


b.

Remove the turn over spring seat compression spring.


31301A 7.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SPRING (for 1GR-FE, 1VD-FTV)

a.

Remove the 2 E-rings and clutch pedal spring with spacer.


b.

Remove the spacer from the clutch pedal spring.


31301 8.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the nut and bolt.


b.

Remove the clutch pedal sub-assembly from the clutch pedal support sub-assembly.


31321 9.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL PAD

31324A 10.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SHAFT COLLAR

a.

Remove the clutch pedal shaft collar from the clutch pedal sub-assembly.


31301C 11.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL BUSH

a.

Remove the 2 clutch pedal bushes from the clutch pedal sub-assembly.


31301B 12.REMOVE NO. 1 CLUTCH PEDAL CUSHION

a.

Remove the No. 1 clutch pedal cushion from the clutch pedal sub-assembly.


31301P 13.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL TURNOVER BUSH

a.

Remove the clutch pedal turnover bush from the clutch pedal sub-assembly.


31453B 14.REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER PUSH ROD CLEVIS BUSH

a.

Remove the clutch master cylinder push rod clevis bush from the clutch pedal sub-assembly.


31353 15.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL SPRING HOLDER

a.

Remove the clutch pedal spring holder from the clutch pedal support sub-assembly.


88280A 16.REMOVE CLUTCH SWITCH ASSEMBLY (for 1VD-FTV)

a.

Loosen the lock nut and remove the clutch switch assembly.


b.

Remove the lock nut from the clutch switch assembly.


31301F 17.REMOVE CLUTCH PEDAL STOPPER BOLT (for 1GR-FE, 1HZ)

a.

Loosen the lock nut and remove the clutch pedal stopper bolt.


b.

Remove the lock nut from the clutch pedal stopper bolt.

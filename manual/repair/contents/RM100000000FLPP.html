1GR-FE FUEL  FUEL PUMP RESISTOR  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

28380A 1.INSTALL FUEL PUMP RESISTOR

a.

Install the fuel pump resistor with the bolt.

Torque:
19.5 N*m (199 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the fuel pump resistor connector to the fuel pump resistor.


28800 2.INSTALL BATTERY

74481 3.INSTALL BATTERY HOLD DOWN CLAMP

a.

Attach the hook of the battery hold down clamp to the front battery bracket.


b.

Partially tighten the nut and temporarily install the bolt.


c.

Tighten the nut and bolt.

Torque:
for bolt : 8.0 N*m (82 kgf*cm, 71 in.*lbf)
for nut : 5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)


d.

for Model Code #J79 Series:

Tighten the 2 nuts and bolt.

Torque:
for bolt : 8.0 N*m (82 kgf*cm, 71 in.*lbf)
for nut : 5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)

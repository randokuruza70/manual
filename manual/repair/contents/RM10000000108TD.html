SEAT BELT  SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM  OPERATION CHECK  


INSPECT driver side seat belt WARNING LIGHT (except 1VD-FTV)


HINT:

The seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly is used for both the driver seat.


a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks for 6 seconds.

After 1.8 seconds, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly changes from blinking to illuminated.


c.

Lift up the vehicle using a swing arm type lift.

Click hereGeneral>INTRODUCTION>REPAIR INSTRUCTION>VEHICLE LIFT AND SUPPORT LOCATIONS201408,999999,_V1,_030611,_0140189,RM100000000G4HN,


d.

Start the engine.


e.

Check the state of the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly under the following conditions:

i.

Step 1:

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened, the parking brake is released for 24 seconds or more, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks.


ii.

Step 2:

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened and the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks.

After 53.6 seconds, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly changes from blinking to illuminated.


iii.

Step 3:

Starting from step 1 with the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinking, when the parking brake is applied, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly changes from blinking to illuminated while the vehicle is stopped.


iv.

Step 4:

Starting from step 1 with the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinking, when the driver side seat belt is fastened, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly turns off.

INSPECT FRONT passenger side seat belt WARNING LIGHT (w/ FRONT PASSENGER SIDE SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM)


HINT:

The seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly is used for both the driver seat and front passenger seat.


a.

Place a weight of 20 kg (44.0 lb) on the front passenger seat.

NOTICE:

Place the weight on the seat cushion and allow it to come into contact with the seatback.



b.

Fasten the driver side seat belt.


c.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


d.

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly illuminated.


e.

Lift the vehicle using a swing arm type lift.

f.

Start the engine.


g.

Check the state of the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly under the following conditions:

i.

Step 1:

When the front passenger side seat belt is not fastened, the parking brake is released for 24 seconds or more, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks.


ii.

Step 2:

When the front passenger side seat belt is not fastened and the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks.

After 144 seconds, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly changes from blinking to illuminated.

iv.

Step 4:

Starting from step 1 with the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinking, with the front passenger side seat belt not fastened and the weight on the front passenger seat, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly turns off when the weight is removed from the front passenger seat.


v.

Step 5:

Starting from step 1 with the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinking, when the front passenger side seat belt is fastened, check that the seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly turns off.




INSPECT SEAT BELT WARNING BUZZER (except 1VD-FTV)


HINT:

The seat belt warning buzzer can be customized. Make sure that this function is ON.

Click hereVehicle Interior>SEAT BELT>SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM>CUSTOMIZE PARAMETERS201408,999999,_58,_029931,_0138409,RM100000000FWMN,


a.

After turning the ignition switch to ON, wait for approximately 1.8 seconds with the driver side seat belt not fastened check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 6 seconds.


b.

Lift the vehicle using a swing arm type lift.

Click hereGeneral>INTRODUCTION>REPAIR INSTRUCTION>VEHICLE LIFT AND SUPPORT LOCATIONS201408,999999,_V1,_030611,_0140189,RM100000000G4HN,


c.

Start the engine.


d.

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened and the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds once.


e.

When 30 seconds have passed after the buzzer sounded, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 30 seconds.


f.

After the seat belt warning buzzer has been sounding for 9.6 seconds at an interval of 1.2 seconds, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 20 seconds.


g.

Check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly stops 29.6 seconds after it started sounding at an interval of 1.2 second.


h.

After the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly stops, the ignition switch is turned off and the engine is restarted, check that the buzzer sounds once again and then sounds according to the appropriate pattern when the vehicle is driven at 20 km/h (12 mph) or more with the driver side seat belt not fastened.


i.

After the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly stops and the vehicle is driven at 20 km/h (12 mph) or more with the driver side seat belt fastened, when the driver side seat belt is unfastened, check that the buzzer sounds once again and then sounds according to the appropriate pattern.


j.

After the buzzer has stopped, stop the vehicle, move the shift lever to reverse gear, and then move it to 1st gear. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, and check that the buzzer sounds again.

a.

NOTICE:

Place the weight on the seat cushion and do not allow it to come into contact with the seatback.



b.

Lift up the vehicle using a swing arm type lift.

c.

Start the engine.


d.

When the driver or front passenger side seat belt is not fastened and the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds once.


e.

When 24 seconds have passed after the buzzer sounded, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 30 seconds.


f.

After the seat belt warning buzzer has been sounding for 30 seconds at an interval of 1.2 second, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 0.4 second for 90 seconds.


g.

Check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly stops 120 seconds after it started sounding at an interval of 1.2 second.


h.

After the buzzer has stopped, turn the ignition switch off and then start the engine again. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more while the driver side seat belt, front passenger side seat belt is not fastened, and check that the buzzer sounds again.


i.

After the buzzer has stopped, fasten the driver and front passenger side seat belt. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more and unfasten the driver side seat belt, front passenger side seat belt and check that the buzzer sounds again.


j.

After the buzzer has stopped, stop the vehicle, move the shift lever to reverse gear, and then move it to 1st gear. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, and check that the buzzer sounds again.

EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  QUARTER PANEL WHEEL OPENING MOULDING(for Model Code #J79 Series)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the LH and RH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

76918C 1.REMOVE REAR SIDE MUDGUARD LH

a.
0.385,0.385 0.625,0.625 0.24,0.24 10 false A

Put a 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit into a drill.


b.

Wind tape around the drill bit approximately 5 mm (0.197 in.) from the tip of the drill as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Tape the 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit to prevent the drill bit from going too deep.


Standard:
Area Specified Condition
A 5 mm (0.197 in.)



c.

Lightly press the drill against the 2 rivets and drill off the flanges of the 2 rivets.

CAUTION:

Be careful of the drilled rivet as it may become hot.


NOTICE:
·

Pressing the drill too firmly will cause the rivet to turn and result in the rivet not being drilled through.

·

Do not pry the rivet with the drill because this may cause damage to the installation holes of the rivet or the drill bit.




d.

Using a vacuum cleaner, remove the rivet fragments and shavings from the drilled areas.


e.

Remove the bolt and rear side mudguard LH.


61062C 2.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 WHEEL OPENING EXTENSION SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 4 bolts.


b.

Detach the 6 clips and remove the rear No. 1 wheel opening extension sub-assembly LH.


c.

Remove the 6 clips from the rear No. 1 wheel opening extension sub-assembly LH.


61783E 3.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 WHEEL OPENING EXTENSION PAD

a.

Remove the rear No. 1 wheel opening extension pad.


58742J 4.REMOVE REAR WHEEL EXTENSION PROTECTOR LH

a.

Remove the rear wheel extension protector LH.


61683D 5.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 WHEEL OPENING BRACKET RH

a.
0.958,2.552 1.188,2.792 0.229,0.24 10 false A

Remove the 2 rivets and rear No. 1 wheel extension bracket RH.

i.

Put a 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit into a drill.


ii.

Wind tape around the drill bit approximately 5 mm (0.197 in.) from the tip of the drill, as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Tape the 4 mm (0.157 in.) drill bit to prevent the drill bit from going too deep.


Standard:
Area Specified Condition
A 5 mm (0.197 in.)



iii.

Lightly press the drill bit against the rivets, drill off the flanges of the rivets, and remove the 2 rivets and rear No. 1 wheel opening bracket RH.

NOTICE:
·

Pressing the drill too firmly will cause the rivet to turn and result in the rivet not being drilled through.

·

Do not pry the rivets with the drill, as this may cause damage to the installation holes of the rivets or the drill bit.



CAUTION:

Be careful of the drilled rivets, as they may become hot.



iv.

Using a vacuum cleaner, remove the rivet fragments and shavings from the drilled areas.

HINT:

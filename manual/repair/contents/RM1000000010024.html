SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Separate Seat Type with Inner Belt Attached)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the RH side.



PROCEDURE

71910A 1.REMOVE FRONT SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the front seat headrest assembly.


2.REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the foremost position.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts.


c.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the rearmost position.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts.


e.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the center position.


f.

Operate the reclining adjuster release handle to set the seatback of the front seat assembly RH to the perpendicular position.


g.

Disconnect the connector and detach the wire harness clamp.


h.

Remove the front seat assembly RH from the vehicle.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

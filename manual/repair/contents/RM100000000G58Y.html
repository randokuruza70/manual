71034A 1.INSTALL REAR NO. 1 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH


a.

71761B 2.INSTALL REAR SIDE SEATBACK PAD LH

a.
1.927,2.917 2.01,2.385 false 1.917,2.188 2.125,2.365 0.208,0.177 10 false *1
*1 Hog Ring Pliers


Using hog ring pliers, install the rear side seatback pad LH and 6 new hog rings to the rear side seat frame assembly LH.

NOTICE:
·

Be careful not to damage the rear side seatback pad LH.

·

When installing the hog rings, take care to minimize wrinkles as much as possible.

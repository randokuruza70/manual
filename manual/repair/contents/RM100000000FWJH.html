ix.

2.CHECK BRAKE PEDAL FREE PLAY

a.
2.146,1.5 2.396,1.708 0.25,0.208 10 *a
*a Pedal Free Play


Stop the engine. Depress the pedal several times until there is no vacuum in the booster. Then release the pedal.


b.

Depress the pedal until resistance is felt.


c.

Check the pedal free play by measuring the distance between the position in the previous step and the pedal's released position.

Standard pedal free play:

1.0 to 6.0 mm (0.0394 to 0.236 in.)


If the free play is not as specified, check the switch clearance in the next step.

If the free play is as specified, proceed to the Check Brake Pedal Reserve Distance procedure.


d.

Check the switch clearance.

Standard stop light switch clearance:

0.5 and 2.4 mm (0.0197 and 0.0944 in.)


If the clearance is not as specified, reinstall the switch and recheck the pedal free play.

If the clearance is as specified, troubleshoot the brake system and proceed to the Check Brake Pedal Reserve Distance procedure.


3.CHECK BRAKE PEDAL RESERVE DISTANCE

a.

Release the parking brake lever. Stop the engine and depress the brake pedal several times until there is no more vacuum left in the booster.


b.

Depress the pedal and check the pedal reserve distance.

i.

Depress the pedal with a force of 300 N (31 kgf, 67 lbf).


ii.

Measure the distance between the pedal and floor panel.

Standard pedal reserve distance:
Item Specified Condition
for LHD without ABS More than 128 mm (5.04 in.)
for LHD with ABS More than 136 mm (5.35 in.)
for RHD without ABS More than 127 mm (4.99 in.)
for RHD with ABS More than 136 mm (5.35 in.)


If the result is not as specified, troubleshoot the brake system.

HINT:

Check the brake pedal reserve distance at the same location as that used when checking the brake pedal height.

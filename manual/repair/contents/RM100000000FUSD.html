REAR SUSPENSION  REAR SHOCK ABSORBER  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

48540 1.TEMPORARILY INSTALL REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Temporarily install the upper mounting bolt, rear No. 1 shock absorber cushion washer and 2 bushing and rear shock absorber assembly LH to the frame.


b.

Temporarily install the lower mounting bolt, rear No. 1 shock absorber cushion washer and 2 bushing and rear shock absorber assembly LH to the spring U bolt seat sub-assembly LH.


2.INSTALL REAR WHEEL

3.STABILIZE SUSPENSION

a.

Lower the vehicle.


b.

Bounce the vehicle up and down several times to stabilize the suspension.


48540 4.TIGHTEN REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Tighten the 2 bolts.

Torque:
64 N*m (653 kgf*cm, 47 ft.*lbf)

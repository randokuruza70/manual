SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Separate Seat Type)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the front inner seat track assembly RH or front outer seat track assembly RH due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.


HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the RH side.



PROCEDURE

71811C 1.REMOVE RECLINING ADJUSTER COVER RH

a.

Remove the 2 screws and reclining adjuster cover RH.


2.REMOVE FRONT SEATBACK ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the bolt.

i.

Remove the tension spring.


ii.

iv.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.




d.

for Walk in Seat Type, for Long Body

i.

Remove the tension spring.


ii.

Remove the clamp and disconnect the 2 cables from the separate type front seat cushion spring assembly.


iii.

Loosen the nut and disconnect the seat track control cable assembly.


iv.

Using a screwdriver, unstake the stake part of the front inner seat track assembly RH.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.

f.

g.

Remove the center seat hinge spacer and washer from the front seatback assembly.


h.

Remove the washer from the separate type front seat cushion spring assembly.


74841B 3.REMOVE FRONT SEATBACK PANEL (w/ Front Seatback Panel)

a.

Remove the 4 screws and front seatback panel.


71073S 4.REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEATBACK COVER

a.

w/o Seatback Panel:

Remove the 3 hog rings.


b.

w/ Seatback Panel:

Remove the 3 hog rings.


c.

Detach the 2 hooks and disconnect the straps of the separate type front seatback cover.


d.

Detach the 4 claws and remove the 2 front seat headrest support assemblies.


e.

Remove the 3 hog rings and separate type front seatback cover from the separate type front seatback pad.


71551S 5.REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEATBACK PAD

a.

Remove the separate type front seatback pad from front seatback frame sub-assembly RH.


72510D 6.REMOVE OUTER RECLINING SEAT ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and outer reclining seat adjuster assembly RH from the front seatback frame sub-assembly RH.


71521A 7.REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SILENCER PAD (for Walk in Seat Type)

a.

Remove the 2 hog rings and front seat cushion silencer pad.


72110A 8.REMOVE FRONT OUTER SEAT TRACK ASSEMBLY RH

a.

w/ Seat track adjusting handle

i.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the rearmost position.


ii.

Remove the bolt.


iii.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the foremost position.


iv.

Remove the bolt and front outer seat track assembly RH.



b.

w/o Seat track adjusting handle

i.

Move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the rearmost position.


ii.

Remove the bolt.


iii.

Move the front outer seat track assembly RH to the foremost position.


iv.

Remove the bolt and front outer seat track assembly RH.



72130A 9.REMOVE FRONT INNER SEAT TRACK ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the front outer seat track assembly RH.


71071S 10.REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEAT CUSHION COVER

a.

Remove the 11 hog rings.


b.

Remove the 9 hog rings and separate type front seat cushion cover from the separate type front seat cushion pad.


71511S 11.REMOVE SEPARATE TYPE FRONT SEAT CUSHION PAD

a.

Remove the separate type front seat cushion pad from the separate type front seat cushion spring assembly.


72053C 12.REMOVE RECLINING REMOTE CONTROL LEVER SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and reclining remote control lever sub-assembly RH from the separate type front seat cushion spring assembly.


71148 13.REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION SILENCER

a.

Remove the 2 front seat cushion silencers from the separate type front seat cushion spring assembly.

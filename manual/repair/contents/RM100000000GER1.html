INTERIOR PANELS / TRIM  ROOF HEADLINING(for Semi-long Body)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

63310 1.INSTALL ROOF HEADLINING ASSEMBLY

a.

Place the roof headlining assembly into the vehicle through the back door.

NOTICE:
·

Check that the corners of the roof headlining assembly are not folded, twisted or otherwise deformed and that none of the mounted parts have fallen off.

·

Make sure that the roof headlining assembly does not get caught on anything as it may become bent or damaged.

·

Do not damage the roof headlining assembly or vehicle interior.




b.

Install the roof headlining assembly with the 11 clips.


c.

Install the upper part of the quarter window glass weatherstrip.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the other quarter window glass weatherstrip.

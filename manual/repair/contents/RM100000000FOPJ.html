AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  DIFFERENTIAL SYSTEM(w/ Differential Lock)  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1.INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL LOCK SYSTEM

a.

Inspect the indicator light.

i.

Check that the indicator light lights up for approximately 1 second when the ignition switch is turned to ON.



b.

Inspect the differential lock operation.

i.

Jack up the vehicle and start the engine.


ii.

Shift the transfer shift lever to the L position.


iii.

When the differential lock control switch is set to the ON position, the indicator light turns on. Differential lock is applied to the rear wheel at this time.

HINT:

If the gears of the differential lock system are not meshed, the indicator light remains blinking, so rotate the tires to mesh the gears.



iv.

When the differential lock control switch is at the OFF position, the indicator light goes off. Differential lock is released for the rear wheel at this time.

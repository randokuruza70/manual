69410 2.ADJUST DOOR LOCK STRIKER

a.

Using a T40 "TORX" socket wrench, adjust the striker position by slightly loosening the striker mounting screws and hitting the striker with a brass bar and hammer.

Torque:
12 N*m (122 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

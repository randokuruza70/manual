RM25T0E _58 Vehicle Interior _029931 SEAT BELT _0142833 FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Short Body) A

SEAT BELT  FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Short Body)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedures listed below is for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

73298B 1.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM BELT HOLE COVER LH

a.

Remove the 2 screws and quarter trim belt hole cover LH.


73299 2.REMOVE NO. 2 QUARTER TRIM BELT HOLE COVER LH

a.

Remove the 2 screws and No. 2 quarter trim belt hole cover LH.


62512 3.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD LH

a.

Detach the 13 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.


73220 4.REMOVE FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the front seat belt anchor.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the front seat outer belt shoulder anchor cover.


c.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts.


e.

Detach the guide and remove the front seat outer belt assembly LH.

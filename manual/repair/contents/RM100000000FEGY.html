12101 16.REMOVE OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 27 bolts and 3 nuts.


b.

Insert the blade of an oil pan seal cutter between the oil pan sub-assembly and cylinder block sub-assembly, cut through the applied sealer and remove the oil pan sub-assembly.

NOTICE:
·

Do not use the oil pan seal cutter for the area between the oil pan sub-assembly and cylinder block sub-assembly.

·

Be careful not to damage the oil pan sub-assembly flange.

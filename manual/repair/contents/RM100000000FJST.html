17705 5.REMOVE AIR CLEANER CAP SUB-ASSEMBLY WITH NO. 1 AIR CLEANER HOSE

a.

Detach the clamp and disconnect the mass air flow meter connector.


b.

Disconnect the vacuum hose from the No. 1 air cleaner hose.


c.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 2 ventilation hose from the No. 1 air cleaner hose.


d.

Detach the clamp and disconnect the wire harness.


e.

Remove the bolt.


f.

Loosen the hose clamp and disconnect the No. 1 air cleaner hose from the cylinder head cover sub-assembly.


g.

Detach the 3 clamps and remove the air cleaner cap sub-assembly with No. 1 air cleaner hose.


17129 6.REMOVE INTAKE AIR SURGE TANK

a.

Disconnect the throttle body connector.


b.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 4 water by-pass hose from the water inlet housing.


c.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 5 water by-pass hose from the water inlet housing.


d.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 1 fuel vapor feed hose from the No. 1 vacuum switching valve assembly.


e.

Disconnect the No. 1 vacuum switching valve connector.


f.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 1 ventilation hose from the intake air surge tank.


g.

Disconnect the vacuum switching valve (for ACIS) connector.


h.

Remove the 2 bolts and throttle body bracket.


i.

Using a clip remover, detach the wire harness clamp from the No. 1 surge tank stay.


j.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the ground wire from the No. 1 surge tank stay.


k.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the engine oil level dipstick guide from the No. 1 surge tank stay.


l.

Remove the bolt and oil baffle plate.


m.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 1 surge tank stay.


n.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


o.

Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the clutch hose bracket.


p.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 2 surge tank stay.


q.

Remove the 2 nuts.


r.

Using an 8 mm hexagon wrench, remove the 4 bolts. Then remove the intake air surge tank and gasket.

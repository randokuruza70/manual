a.

Disconnect the connector clamp.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 1 engine oil level dipstick guide from the oil pan sub-assembly and transverse engine mounting bracket RH.


c.

Remove the O-ring from the No. 1 engine oil level dipstick guide.

2.REMOVE STEERING COLUMN UPPER WITH SWITCH BRACKET ASSEMBLY

a.

b.

45833 10.LOOSEN NO. 1 TILT STEERING ADJUSTING NUT

a.

Lock the steering tilt lever.


b.

Using a hammer and chisel, loosen the staked part of the No. 1 tilt steering adjusting nut.


c.

Loosen the No. 1 tilt steering adjusting nut.

NOTICE:

Be sure to only loosen the No. 1 tilt steering adjusting nut. Do not remove it.



45821B 11.REMOVE NO. 1 TILT STEERING SUPPORT REINFORCEMENT

a.

Remove the steering tilt lever bolt and No. 1 tilt steering support reinforcement from the steering tilt lever.

NOTICE:

The steering tilt lever bolt has a left-handed thread.



45831 12.REMOVE TILT NO. 1 LEVER LOCK BOLT

a.

Using an 8 mm socket hexagon wrench, loosen the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt until it can be turned by hand.


b.

Remove the No. 1 tilt steering adjusting nut from the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt.


c.

Remove the tilt steering rotor, thrust needle roller bearing and steering column tube stopper from the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt.


d.

Remove the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt, steering column tube stopper and steering tilt lever from the break away bracket.


45878A 13.REMOVE BREAK AWAY BRACKET

a.

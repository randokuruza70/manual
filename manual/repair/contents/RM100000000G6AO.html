PRECAUTION FOR WASHER NOZZLE HANDLING

Do not adjust the washer nozzle with metal objects such as a safety pin, as the washer nozzle tip is made of plastic and could be damaged.

b.

When the washer nozzle is clogged with things such as wax, do not use metal objects such as a safety pin to clean the washer nozzle hole. Be sure to use a soft plastic brush, etc.

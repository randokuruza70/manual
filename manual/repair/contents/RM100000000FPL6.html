RM25T0E _51 Engine / Hybrid System _029900 1VD-FTV STARTING _0137002 STARTER A

1VD-FTV STARTING  STARTER  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

28213 1.REMOVE STARTER CLUTCH PINION


a.

Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring.

HINT:

While pushing down the starter clutch pinion, remove the snap ring.



b.

Remove the pinion stop collar, starter clutch pinion and starter drive spring.


28140 2.REMOVE STARTER BRUSH HOLDER ASSEMBLY

a.
1.51,0.677 2.104,1.542 2.104,1.542 2.24,1.542 true 2.281,1.469 2.5,1.656 0.219,0.188 10 false *a
*a Terminal C


Remove the nut from terminal C.


b.

Remove the 2 through bolts and 2 screws, and then remove the commutator end frame from the starter yoke assembly.


c.

Using a screwdriver, hold the starter brush spring back and disconnect the 4 starter brushes from the starter brush holder assembly.


d.

Remove the starter brush holder assembly from the starter armature assembly.


28120 3.REMOVE STARTER YOKE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the starter yoke assembly.


b.

Remove the 2 O-rings from the starter yoke assembly.


28160 4.REMOVE STARTER ARMATURE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the starter armature assembly from the starter plate.


28256 5.REMOVE STARTER INTERNAL GEAR

a.

Remove the starter plate.


b.

Remove the starter washer.


c.

Remove the 3 starter planetary gears.


d.

Remove the starter internal gear.


28110 6.REMOVE STARTER DRIVE HOUSING ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and starter drive housing assembly from the main switch assembly.


28011 7.REMOVE STARTER CLUTCH SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring.


b.

Remove the pinion drive lever and 3 starter washers.


c.

Remove the starter clutch sub-assembly from the main switch assembly.

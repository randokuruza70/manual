1VD-FTV INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST PIPE(for 5 Door)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves when removing the exhaust pipe.

·

The exhaust pipe is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.

·

Confirm that the exhaust pipe has cooled down before removing it.



PROCEDURE

17430 1.REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.


b.

Remove the 4 nuts from the 2 exhaust pipe support cushions. Then remove the tailpipe assembly.



c.

Remove the 4 nuts and 2 exhaust pipe support cushions from the tailpipe assembly.


d.

Remove the gasket from the front exhaust pipe assembly.


17410 2.REMOVE FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs.


b.

Remove the 2 nuts from the exhaust pipe support cushion. Then remove the front exhaust pipe assembly.



c.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the front exhaust pipe assembly.


18421C 3.REMOVE CENTER CONVERTER PROTECTOR STAY

a.

Remove the 3 bolts and center converter protector stay.


18450A 4.REMOVE MONOLITHIC CONVERTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 3 nuts, monolithic converter assembly and gasket.


b.

Remove the gasket from the monolithic converter assembly.


18431 5.REMOVE UPPER CATALYTIC CONVERTER INSULATOR

a.

Remove the 2 bolts, nut and upper catalytic converter insulator.

PROCEDURE

47313A 1.INSTALL FRONT FLEXIBLE HOSE

a.

Connect the 2 front flexible hoses to the connecting point with each brake line, and then install 4 new clips.


b.

Torque:
Specified tightening torque : 15.2 N*m (155 kgf*cm, 11 ft.*lbf)

HINT:
·

Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

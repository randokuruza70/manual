DESCRIPTION

The driver side squib circuit consists of the airbag sensor assembly, spiral cable sub-assembly and horn button assembly.

The circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction is detected in the driver side squib circuit.

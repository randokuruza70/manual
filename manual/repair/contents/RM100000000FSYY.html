69320 29.REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Detach the 2 snaps and 2 control links as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

The control link can be easily removed by pushing the end of the control link with the tip side of a screwdriver.



b.

w/ Power Door Lock, Type A:

i.

Detach the clamp and disconnect the connector.


ii.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and front door lock assembly LH.



c.

w/ Power Door Lock, Type B:

i.

Remove the bolt.


ii.

Detach the clamp and disconnect the connector.


iii.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and front door lock assembly LH.



d.

w/o Power Door Lock:

i.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and front door lock assembly LH.



69120A 30.REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY LH (w/ Power Door Lock, Type B)

a.

Remove the 2 screws.


b.

Detach the guide and front door lock actuator assembly LH in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.


69310 31.REMOVE FRONT DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY RH (w/ Double Inside Handles)

a.

w/ Power Door Lock:

i.

Remove the bolt.


ii.

Detach the clamp and disconnect the connector.


iii.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and front door lock assembly RH.



b.

w/o Power Door Lock:

i.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and front door lock assembly RH.

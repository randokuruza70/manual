BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM  C1337  Diameter of the Tire is not Uniform  


DESCRIPTION

The brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU) measures the speed of each wheel by receiving signals from the speed sensors.

These signals are used for recognizing that all 4 wheels are operating properly.

Therefore, all wheel signals must indicate the same speed.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1337 Diameter of the Tire is not Uniform The following occurs in 3 consecutive trips:
At a vehicle speed of 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, there is a difference of 20% or more in the average speed between the front wheels and rear wheels for 20 seconds or more.

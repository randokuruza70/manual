20.CHECK THRUST CLEARANCE NO. 1 IDLE GEAR

a.

Using a thickness gauge, measure the thrust clearance.

Standard thrust clearance:

0.05 to 0.15 mm (0.00197 to 0.00591 in.)


Maximum thrust clearance:

0.30 mm (0.0118 in.)


If the thrust clearance is more than the maximum, replace the thrust plate. If necessary, replace the No. 1 idle gear and/or No. 1 idle gear shaft.

iii.

b.

Determine the replacement adjusting valve shim size according to the formula and charts below.

i.

Using a micrometer, measure the thickness of the removed adjusting valve shim.


ii.

Calculate the thickness of a new adjusting valve shim so that the valve clearance comes within the specified value.

T = Thickness of removed adjusting valve shim

A = Measured valve clearance

N = Thickness of new adjusting valve shim

Intake N = T + (A - 0.20 mm (0.00787 in.))
Exhaust N = T + (A - 0.40 mm (0.0157 in.))


iii.

Select a new adjusting valve shim with a thickness as close as possible to the calculated value.

HINT:

Adjusting valve shims are available in 20 sizes in increments of 0.05 mm (0.00197 in.), from 2.35 to 3.30 mm (0.0925 to 0.1299 in.).

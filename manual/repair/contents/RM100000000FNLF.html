1HZ FUEL  FUEL TANK(for 90 L Tank Carried in Rear)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN FUEL


a.

Loosen the fuel tank drain plug, gasket and drain the fuel.


b.

Install the fuel tank drain plug and a new gasket.

Torque:
6.5 N*m (66 kgf*cm, 58 in.*lbf)


2.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


3.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


77213 4.DISCONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the inlet fuel hose from the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly.


77100D 5.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100 6.REMOVE FUEL TANK

a.

Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.


b.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel tank assembly.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts and separate the 2 fuel tank bands.


d.

Slightly lower the engine lifter.


7.REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

8.REMOVE FUEL SUCTION TUBE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 5 screws and fuel suction tube assembly.


b.

Remove the gasket from the fuel suction tube assembly.


77011 9.REMOVE LOWER FUEL TANK FILLER PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 6 screws and lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly from the fuel tank assembly.


b.

Remove the gasket from the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly.

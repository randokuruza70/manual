28100 1.INSTALL STARTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the starter assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
80 N*m (816 kgf*cm, 59 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the 2 hoses to the starter assembly.


c.

Attach the harness clamp and connect the wire harness.


d.

Connect the starter wire with the nut.

Torque:
19.6 N*m (200 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)


e.

Connect the starter connector.

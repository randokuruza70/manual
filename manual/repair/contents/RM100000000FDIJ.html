1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  MONOLITHIC CONVERTER  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

18431 1.INSTALL UPPER CATALYTIC CONVERTER INSULATOR

a.

Install the upper catalytic converter insulator with the 2 bolts and nut.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


18450A 2.INSTALL MONOLITHIC CONVERTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Temporarily install a new gasket to the monolithic converter assembly by hand.

HINT:

Be sure to install the gasket as evenly as possible.

Using a plastic-faced hammer and wooden block, tap the gasket onto the monolithic converter assembly so that the gasket is flush with the pipe end.

NOTICE:
·

Install the gasket in the correct direction.

·

Do not damage the outer surface of the gasket.

·

Do not reuse the gasket.

·

Do not push in the gasket with the exhaust pipe when connecting it.




c.

Install a new gasket onto the turbine outlet elbow.


d.

Loosely install the monolithic converter assembly with the 3 new nuts.

HINT:

Tighten the nuts until the contact surface of the monolithic converter assembly contacts with the turbine outlet elbow.



e.

Loosely install the center converter protector stay with the 3 bolts.

HINT:

Tighten the bolts so that the position of the monolithic converter assembly can be adjusted in a later step.



f.

Tighten the 3 nuts of the monolithic converter assembly.

Torque:
47 N*m (479 kgf*cm, 35 ft.*lbf)


g.

Tighten the 3 bolts of the center converter protector stay.

Torque:
43 N*m (438 kgf*cm, 32 ft.*lbf)


h.

Inspect the compression spring.

i.

Using a vernier caliper, measure the free length of the compression spring.

Standard free length:

43 mm (1.69 in.)


If the free length is not as specified, replace the compression spring.

Connect the monolithic converter assembly to the front exhaust pipe assembly with the 2 bolts and 2 compression springs.

Torque:
43 N*m (438 kgf*cm, 32 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

When tightening the bolts, make sure the lengths of the springs are approximately the same.

·

Make sure the joint part is movable.

·

Make sure the bolts do not get caught on the flange.

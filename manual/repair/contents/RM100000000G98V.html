BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM RESERVOIR(for 1HZ, 1VD-FTV)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

44778 1.DISCONNECT CONNECTOR TO CHECK VALVE HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the connector to check valve hose from the brake vacuum check valve assembly.


44773F 2.DISCONNECT UNION TO CHECK VALVE HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the union to check valve hose from the vacuum reservoir tube.


44740 3.REMOVE VACUUM RESERVOIR ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the connector from the vacuum warning switch assembly.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts and vacuum reservoir assembly.

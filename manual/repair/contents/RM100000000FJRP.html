77100 7.INSTALL FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY

a.

Set the fuel tank assembly and fuel tank protector on an engine lifter.


b.

Raise the engine lifter.


c.

Connect the 2 fuel tank bands with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
39 N*m (398 kgf*cm, 29 ft.*lbf)


d.

Connect the fuel sender gauge connector.


77100D 8.CONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel breather hose to the fuel tank assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


77213 9.CONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Connect the inlet fuel hose to the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


10.CONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel return hose to the fuel suction tube assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


11.CONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel main hose to the fuel suction tube assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.

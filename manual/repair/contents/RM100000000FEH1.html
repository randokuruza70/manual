2.ADD ENGINE OIL

a.

Using a 5 mm hexagon wrench, remove the taper screw plug.


b.

Add 10 cc (0.61 cu.in.) of engine oil into the oil hole shown in the illustration.


c.

Remove any old adhesive from the taper screw plug.


d.

Apply adhesive to 3 or more threads of the taper screw plug.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent



e.

Using a 5 mm hexagon wrench, install the taper screw plug.

Torque:
9.8 N*m (100 kgf*cm, 87 in.*lbf)

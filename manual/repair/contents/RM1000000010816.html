1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR(for DPF)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

To prevent burns, do not touch the engine, exhaust manifold or other high temperature components while the engine is hot.


PROCEDURE

89425 1.REMOVE EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR

NOTICE:

If the exhaust gas temperature sensor is dropped, replace it with a new one.


a.

Disconnect the connector and clamp.


b.

Using a 14 mm union nut wrench, remove the exhaust gas temperature sensor.


89425A 2.REMOVE NO. 2 EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR

NOTICE:

If the No. 2 exhaust gas temperature sensor is dropped, replace it with a new one.


a.

Disconnect the connector and clamp.


b.

Using a 14 mm union nut wrench, remove the No. 2 exhaust gas temperature sensor.


89425B 3.REMOVE NO. 3 EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR

NOTICE:

If the No. 3 exhaust gas temperature sensor is dropped, replace it with a new one.


a.

Disconnect the connector and clamp.


b.

Using a 14 mm union nut wrench, remove the No. 3 exhaust gas temperature sensor.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Always stop the engine when installing or removing the vacuum pump, or when removing the vacuum hoses.


PROCEDURE

25860 1.CHECK NO. 1 VACUUM SWITCHING VALVE OPERATION (for EGR Cooler Bypass Valve)

a.

Remove the vacuum hose of the No. 1 vacuum switching valve from the EGR cooler.


b.

Using a 3-way connector, connect a vacuum pump to the hose between the No. 2 EGR valve assembly and No. 1 vacuum switching valve assembly.

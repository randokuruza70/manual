75441B 2.INSTALL BACK DOOR NAME PLATE

HINT:

When installing the back door name plate, heat the vehicle body and back door name plate using a heat light.


Standard:
Item Temperature
Vehicle Body 40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)
Back Door Name Plate 20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)


NOTICE:

Do not heat the vehicle body or back door name plate excessively.


2.031,3.708 2.771,3.708 true 1.865,4.229 2.604,4.229 true 2.802,3.635 3.115,3.833 0.313,0.198 10 false *1 2.635,4.156 2.948,4.354 0.313,0.198 10 false *1
*1 Boss


a.

Clean the back door panel surface.

i.

Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body surface.


ii.

Remove the double-sided tape from the vehicle body.


iii.

Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.



b.

Install a new back door name plate.

i.

Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body and a new back door name plate.


ii.

Remove the peeling paper from the face of the back door name plate.

HINT:

After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.



iii.

Align the boss positions and install the back door name plate.

HINT:

Press the back door name plate firmly to install it.

1GR-FE EMISSION CONTROL  CANISTER  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

77740 1.INSTALL CHARCOAL CANISTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the charcoal canister assembly to the charcoal canister base bracket.


b.

Connect the No. 1 charcoal canister outlet hose to the charcoal canister assembly.


c.

Connect the fuel emission hose to the charcoal canister assembly and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


d.

Connect the No. 1 fuel vapor feed hose to the charcoal canister assembly and slide the clamp to secure the hose.

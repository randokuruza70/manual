BF1B TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD  TRANSFER OIL  REPLACEMENT  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN TRANSFER OIL

a.
0.854,1.844 1.01,1.844 1.01,1.844 1.271,1.417 true 2.208,1.99 2.823,1.198 2.823,1.198 3.01,1.198 true 0.635,1.75 0.948,1.948 0.313,0.198 10 false *1 3.094,1.104 3.406,1.302 0.313,0.198 10 false *2
*1 Transfer Case Plug (Filler Plug)
*2 Transfer Case Plug (Drain Plug)


Remove the transfer case plug (filler plug) and transfer case gasket.


b.

Remove the transfer case plug (drain plug) and transfer case gasket, and drain the transfer oil.


c.

Install a new transfer case gasket to the transfer case plug (drain plug) and then install the transfer case plug (drain plug).

Torque:
37.24 N*m (380 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)


2.ADD TRANSFER OIL

a.
2.615,0.885 2.927,1.042 0.313,0.156 10 *a
*a 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)


Pour oil so that the oil level is between 0 to 5.0 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug hole.

Transfer Oil:
Oil grade Oil viscosity
API GL-4 or GL-5 SAE 90


Standard capacity:

2.1 liters (2.2 US qts, 1.8 Imp. qts)


NOTICE:
·

When adding oil, pour it slowly.

·

Add oil a little at a time, waiting several minutes between each addition of oil.

·

Too much or too little oil will lead to transfer problems.




b.

Wait approximately 5 minutes and check that the oil level has not changed.


c.

Install a new transfer case gasket to the transfer case plug (filler plug) and then install the transfer case plug (filler plug).

Torque:
37.24 N*m (380 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)

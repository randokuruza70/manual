PROCEDURE

73370 1.INSPECT REAR SEAT OUTER 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.
0.75,0.052 1.063,0.208 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 2.833,0.938 3.146,1.094 0.313,0.156 10 false *b 1.948,0.271 2.26,0.427 0.313,0.156 10 false *c
*a Unlock
*b Lock
*c 45°


Before installing the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH, check the ELR function.

NOTICE:

Do not disassemble the retractor.


i.

When the inclination of the retractor is 15° or less, check that the belt can be pulled from the retractor. When the inclination of the retractor is over 45°, check that the belt locks.

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH.

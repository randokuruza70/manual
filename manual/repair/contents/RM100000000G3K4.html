85035 2.ADJUST WASHER NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Using a Toyota 4 Way Nozzle Adjustment Tool, insert the convex portion of the adjustment tool into the concave portion of the nozzle and make the adjustment.

NOTICE:
·

Check which type of washer nozzle is being adjusted because two types (φ4 mm and φ5 mm) exist.

·

Do not use a metal pin or other such tool because it may damage the nozzle.

PROCEDURE

1.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the hose clip and disconnect the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


2.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Slide the hose clip and disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


77213 3.DISCONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Loosen the clamp and disconnect the inlet fuel hose from the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly.


77100D 4.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the hose clip and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100 5.REMOVE FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.


b.

Detach the wire harness clamp from the frame.


c.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel tank assembly.

BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM PUMP(for 1HZ)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

The vacuum pump assembly is listed as one of the 200000 km (124000 mile) maintenance parts. Make sure to disassemble and inspect it every 200000 km (124000 miles) and replace parts as necessary.


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP TUBE

a.

Remove the flange bolt from the vacuum pump tube.


b.

Remove the 2 union bolts, 4 gaskets and vacuum pump tube from the vacuum pump and cylinder block.


44774 2.DISCONNECT UNION TO CONNECTOR TUBE HOSE

a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the 2 union to connector tube hoses from the vacuum pump.


29300 3.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 nuts and vacuum pump from the engine.


b.

Remove the O-ring from the vacuum pump.

1HZ ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  42  Vehicle Speed Sensor Signal Circuit Malfunction  


DESCRIPTION

The vehicle speed sensor outputs a 4-pulse signal for every revolution of the rotor shaft, which is rotated by the transmitted output shaft via the driven gear. After this signal is converted into a more precise rectangular waveform by the waveform shaping circuit inside the combination meter, it is then transmitted to the emission control ECU. The emission control ECU determines the vehicle speed based on the frequency of these pulse signals.

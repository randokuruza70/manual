1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM(w/ DPF)  P0489  Exhaust Gas Recirculation Control Circuit Low  P0490  Exhaust Gas Recirculation Control Circuit High  


DESCRIPTION

The EGR actuator is operated by the ECM and opens and closes the EGR valve using gears. The opening angle of the EGR valve is detected by the EGR position sensor, which is mounted on the electric EGR control valve assembly.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0489 Exhaust Gas Recirculation Control Circuit Low Both of the following conditions are met for 2.0 seconds (1 trip detection logic):
·

The EGR actuator duty cycle is 80% or higher.

·

EGR actuator current is below 0.5 A.


·

Open or short in EGR actuator circuit

·

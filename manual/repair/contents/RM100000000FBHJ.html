13568 7.INSTALL TIMING BELT

NOTICE:

The engine should be cold.


HINT:

If re-using timing belt, align the points marked during removal, and install the timing belt with the arrow pointing in the direction of engine revolution.


a.

Remove any oil or water on the pulleys, and keep them clean.

NOTICE:

Only wipe the pulleys, do not use any cleansing agent.



b.

Install the timing belt under tension between the camshaft timing pulley and camshaft drive gear.

SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS  SIDE AIRBAG SENSOR  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

89865 1.INSTALL DOOR SIDE AIRBAG SENSOR LH

a.

Check that the ignition switch is off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.



c.

Attach the guide and hold the door side airbag sensor LH in place.


d.

Install the bolt.

Torque:
9.0 N*m (92 kgf*cm, 80 in.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

If the door side airbag sensor LH has been dropped, replace it with a new one.

·

When installing the door side airbag sensor LH, be careful that the SRS wiring does not interfere with or is not pinched between other parts.

·

Tighten the bolt while holding the door side airbag sensor LH because the door side airbag sensor LH guide is easily damaged.




e.

Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.


i.

Before connecting the airbag connector, check that the position of the housing lock is correct as shown in the illustration.

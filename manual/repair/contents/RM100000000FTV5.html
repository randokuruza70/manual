HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  COMPRESSOR(for 1GR-FE)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE COOLER BRACKET

0.052,0.073 0.365,0.229 0.313,0.156 10 false *A 0.063,2.438 0.375,2.594 0.313,0.156 10 false *B
*A for Type A
*B for Type B


a.

Detach the clamp.


b.

Remove the screw and the cooler bracket.


88410 2.REMOVE MAGNET CLUTCH ASSEMBLY

1.177,1.573 0.833,1.573 false 0.688,1.49 1,1.646 0.313,0.156 10 false *1
*1 SST


a.

Clamp the compressor assembly with magnet clutch in a vise.


b.

Using SST, hold the magnet clutch hub.

SST
09985-00270  


c.

Remove the bolt, magnet clutch hub and magnet clutch washer(s).

HINT:

There is no set number of magnet clutch washers since they are used for adjusting.



d.

Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring and the magnet clutch rotor.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the seal cover of the bearing when removing the snap ring.



e.

Disconnect the connector.


f.

Using a snap ring expander, remove the snap ring and magnet clutch stator.

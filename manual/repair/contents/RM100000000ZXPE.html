AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  REAR DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY(for Standard)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

41101 1.INSTALL REAR DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Clean the contact surfaces of the rear differential carrier sub-assembly and axle housing.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the installation surface.



b.

Install the rear differential carrier sub-assembly, and a new gasket and the washers with the 10 nuts.

Torque:
72 N*m (734 kgf*cm, 53 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Be careful to install the gasket without tearing it.



2.INSTALL WIRE HARNESS CLAMP BRACKET (w/ ABS,VSC)

a.

Install the wire harness clamp bracket with the nut.

Torque:
36 N*m (367 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the clamp.

1GR-FE ENGINE CONTROL  POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

89438A 1.INSTALL POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH

a.

Install a new O-ring to the power steering oil pressure switch.


b.

Apply a light coat of power steering fluid to the O-ring.

HINT:
·

When reusing the power steering oil pressure switch, inspect the O-ring.

·

If the O-ring has scratches or cuts, replace the power steering oil pressure switch.




c.

Install the power steering oil pressure switch.

Torque:
20.6 N*m (210 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


d.

Connect the power steering oil pressure switch connector.


2.ADD POWER STEERING FLUID

3.BLEED AIR FROM POWER STEERING SYSTEM

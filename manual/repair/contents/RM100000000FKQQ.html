BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM  ABS Warning Light Remains ON  


DESCRIPTION

When one of the following conditions is met, the ABS warning light remains on:

·

The brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU) connector is disconnected.

·

There is a malfunction in the brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU) internal circuit.

·

There is an open in the harness between the combination meter and the brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU).

·

The anti-lock brake system is defective.

·

w/ Rear Differential Lock:

The rear differential is locked or the rear differential and front differential are locked.

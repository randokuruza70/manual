1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM(w/o EGR System)  P0045  Turbocharger / Supercharger Boost Control Solenoid Circuit / Open  


DESCRIPTION

The vacuum regulating valve (VRV) is controlled by the ECM with the duty control. According to the engine state, the VRV controls the vacuum applied to the actuator, which is installed to the turbocharger. If an open or short malfunction occurs in the VRV circuit, this DTC is output.

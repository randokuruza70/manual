1VD-FTV INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST MANIFOLD W/ TURBOCHARGER  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

17201 1.VISUALLY CHECK TURBOCHARGER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.
1.896,1.563 2.406,0.927 2.406,0.927 2.625,0.927 true 2.688,0.833 2.969,1.052 0.281,0.219 10 false *1
*1 Turbine Wheel


Check for deformation and cracks in the turbine wheel on the exhaust side.

If the turbine wheel is cracked or deformed, replace the turbocharger sub-assembly.


2.INSPECT AXIAL PLAY OF TURBINE SHAFT

a.

Insert the needle of a dial indicator into the intake side of the turbine shaft.


b.

Move the turbine shaft in an axial direction and measure the axial play of the turbine shaft using the dial indicator.

Maximum axial play:

0.10 mm (0.00394 in.)


If the axial play is more than the maximum, replace the turbocharger sub-assembly.


17201 3.CHECK TURBINE SHAFT ROTATION

a.

Rotate the turbine wheel on the exhaust side with your finger and check that it rotates smoothly.

HINT:

Even if it takes a little effort to rotate the turbine wheel, this is not a problem.


If the turbine wheel does not rotate smoothly, replace the turbocharger sub-assembly.

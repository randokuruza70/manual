SEAT BELT  FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Separate Seat Type with Inner Belt Attached)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.



HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the RH side.



PROCEDURE

73230 1.INSTALL FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY RH

a.

Install the front seat belt anchor plate to the front seat cushion spring assembly RH.


b.
1.854,1.771 2.979,1.865 2.979,1.865 2.167,1.604 true 3.021,1.76 3.333,1.917 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Protruding Part


Install the front seat inner belt assembly RH with the nut.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Do not allow the anchor part of the front seat inner belt assembly RH to overlap the protruding part of the front seat cushion spring.



c.

Attach the 2 clamps to connect the wire harness.


d.

Attach the claw to connect the connector.


e.

Return the separate type front seat cushion cover to its original position and install the clip.


73240 2.INSTALL FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Install the front seat belt anchor plate to the front seat cushion spring assembly LH.


b.
1.385,1.771 0.271,1.813 0.271,1.813 1.063,1.604 true 0.063,1.708 0.375,1.865 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Protruding Part


Install the front seat inner belt assembly LH with the nut.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Do not allow the anchor part of the front seat inner belt assembly LH to overlap the protruding part of the front seat cushion spring.



c.

Attach the 3 clamps to connect the wire harness.


d.

Attach the claw to connect the connector.


e.

Connect the connector of the occupant detection sensor.


f.

Return the separate type front seat cushion cover to its original position and install the clip.


71861 3.INSTALL FRONT SEAT INNER CUSHION SHIELD RH

a.

Attach the 2 claws and 2 guides.


b.

Attach the 2 claws and 4 guides to install the front seat inner cushion shield RH.


c.

Attach the band.


4.INSTALL FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY

1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  INTAKE AIR TEMPERATURE SENSOR  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

89424D 1.REMOVE INTAKE AIR TEMPERATURE SENSOR


a.

Disconnect the connector from the intake air temperature sensor.


b.

Using a 22 mm deep socket wrench, remove the intake air temperature sensor from the intercooler.


c.

Remove the gasket from the intake air temperature sensor.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

After replacing the ECM, the new ECM needs registration (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL>ECD SYSTEM>REGISTRATION201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJKB,) and initialization (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL>ECD SYSTEM>INITIALIZATION201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJIX,).

·

After replacing a fuel supply pump, the ECM needs initialization (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL>ECD SYSTEM>INITIALIZATION201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJIX,).

·

After replacing an injector, the ECM needs registration (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL>ECD SYSTEM>REGISTRATION201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJKB,).

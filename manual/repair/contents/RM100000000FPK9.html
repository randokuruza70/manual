19871 3.REMOVE NO. 1 GLOW PLUG CONNECTOR

a.

Remove the 6 glow plug screw grommets.


b.

Remove the 6 nuts, No. 1 glow plug connector and disconnect the wire harness.


19850 4.REMOVE GLOW PLUG ASSEMBLY

a.

Using a 12 mm deep socket wrench, remove the 6 glow plug assemblies.

NOTICE:

Do not remove the glow plug assembly before warming up the engine.

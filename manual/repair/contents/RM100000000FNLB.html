1HZ FUEL  FUEL TANK(for 90 L Tank Carried in Middle)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

77011 1.INSTALL LOWER FUEL TANK FILLER PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket and the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly with the 6 screws.

Torque:
3.0 N*m (31 kgf*cm, 27 in.*lbf)


2.INSTALL FUEL SUCTION TUBE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket and the fuel suction tube assembly with the 5 screws.

Torque:
1.5 N*m (15 kgf*cm, 13 in.*lbf)


3.INSTALL FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

77420 4.INSTALL NO. 2 FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the No. 2 fuel cut off valve assembly onto the fuel tank assembly.


77380 5.INSTALL FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the fuel cut off valve assembly onto the fuel tank assembly.


77026A 6.INSTALL NO. 1 FUEL EVAPORATION TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install 2 new gaskets and the No. 1 evaporation tube sub-assembly to the fuel tank assembly with the 8 screws and 2 nuts.

Torque:
for screw : 1.5 N*m (15 kgf*cm, 13 in.*lbf)
for nut : 5.5 N*m (56 kgf*cm, 49 in.*lbf)


77100 7.INSTALL FUEL TANK

a.

Set the fuel tank and fuel tank protector on an engine lifter.


b.

Raise the engine lifter.


c.

Connect the 2 fuel tank bands with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
39 N*m (398 kgf*cm, 29 ft.*lbf)


d.

Connect the fuel sender gauge connector.


77100D 8.CONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel breather hose to the fuel tank assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


77213 9.CONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Connect the inlet fuel hose to the lower fuel tank filler pipe sub-assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


10.CONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel return hose to the fuel suction tube assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


11.CONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel main hose to the fuel suction tube assembly, and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


77207B 12.INSTALL NO. 3 FUEL MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

for Single Tank Type:

i.

Install the No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly with the bolt.

Torque:
32 N*m (326 kgf*cm, 24 ft.*lbf)


ii.

Connect the 4 fuel hoses to the No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly, and slide the 4 clamps to secure the 4 hoses.



b.

for Double Tank Type:

i.

Install the fuel No. 3 main tube sub-assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
32 N*m (326 kgf*cm, 24 ft.*lbf)


ii.

Connect the 4 fuel hoses to the No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly, and slide the 4 clamps to secure the 4 hoses.

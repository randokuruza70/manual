BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM PUMP(for 1HZ)  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1. OPERATION CHECK

*1 Plug
*a Vacuum Gauge


a.

Slide the clip and disconnect the union to connector tube hose from the vacuum pump.


b.

Connect the hose of a vacuum gauge to the vacuum pump.


c.

Insert a plug into the other hose of the gauge.


d.

Start the engine and warm it up for more than 2 minutes.


e.

With the engine idling, measure the negative pressure of the vacuum pump.

Standard pressure:

More than 87 kPa (650 mmHg, 25.6 in.Hg)


HINT:
·

The vacuum pump assembly is listed as one of the 200000 km (124000 mile) maintenance parts. Make sure to disassemble and inspect it every 200000 km (124000 miles) and replace parts as necessary.

·

After disassembling and checking the vacuum pump, check the vacuum pump assembly function.




f.

Remove the vacuum gauge from the vacuum pump.


g.

Connect the union to connector tube hose to the vacuum pump with the clip.

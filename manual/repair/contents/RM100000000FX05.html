PROCEDURE

47625A 1.INSTALL PARKING BRAKE BELL CRANK LH

a.

Apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the bell crank boot.


b.

Install the bell crank boot to the parking brake bell crank LH.


c.

Install the parking brake bell crank LH to the bell crank bracket.


d.

Apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the pin.


e.

Install the pin with a new C-washer.


f.

Install the parking brake bell crank LH with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
12.5 N*m (127 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)


g.

Connect the No. 1 parking brake cable assembly to the parking brake bell crank LH with the pin and clip.


h.

Install the 2 tension springs from the parking brake bell crank LH.

d.

Install 2 new cylinder boots to the rear wheel brake cylinder assembly LH.

a.

b.

Measure the brake drum inner diameter and the diameter of the brake shoes. Check that the difference between the diameters is equal to the specified shoe clearance.

Standard shoe clearance:

0.6 mm (0.0236 in.)


NOTICE:

There should be no oil or grease on the contact surfaces of the shoe lining and drum.



47625A 15.ADJUST PARKING BRAKE BELL CRANK LH

a.
0.479,1.063 0.646,1.229 0.167,0.167 10 false A 1.552,0.177 1.708,0.375 0.156,0.198 10 false B

Pull


Lightly pull the parking brake bell crank LH in direction until there is no slack at part B.


b.

Adjust the gap between the stopper bolt and the backing plate.

Gap A:

0.4 to 0.8 mm (0.0157 to 0.0315 in.)



c.

Lock the adjust bolt with the lock nut.

Torque:
5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)

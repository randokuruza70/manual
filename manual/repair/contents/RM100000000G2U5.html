LIGHTING (EXT)  LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY(for Canvas Top)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

81276A 1.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT PLATE (for Back Door)

a.

Remove the 4 screws and license plate light plate from the license plate light body.


81278 2.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT COVER (w/ Auxiliary Fuel Tank)

a.

Remove the 2 screws and license plate light cover from the license plate light socket and wire.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the license plate light cover on the other side.



81271 3.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT LENS

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the license plate light lens on the other side.


a.

for Back Door:

Remove the 2 screws and license plate light lens from the license plate light body.


b.

w/ Auxiliary Fuel Tank:

Remove the license plate light lens from the license plate light socket and wire.


81272 4.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT LENS GASKET

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the license plate light lens gasket on the other side.


a.

for Back Door:

Remove the license plate light lens gasket from the license plate light body.


b.

w/ Auxiliary Fuel Tank:

Remove the license plate light lens gasket from the license plate light lens.

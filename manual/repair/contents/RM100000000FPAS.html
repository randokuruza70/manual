45831 10.INSTALL NO. 1 TILT LEVER LOCK BOLT

a.

Apply MP grease to the sliding surface of the steering tilt lever and install it to the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt.


b.

Install the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt to the break away bracket.

NOTICE:
·

Do not drop the steering column tube stopper.

·

Pass the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt through the hole of the tilt steering pawl assembly.




c.

Apply MP grease to the sliding surface of the thrust needle roller bearing and install it to the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt.


d.

Temporarily install the tilt steering rotor to the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt with a new No. 1 tilt steering adjusting nut.


e.

Using an 8 mm socket hexagon wrench, tighten the No. 1 steering tilt lever lock bolt.

Torque:
2.0 N*m (20 kgf*cm, 18 in.*lbf)


45821B 11.INSTALL NO. 1 TILT STEERING SUPPORT REINFORCEMENT

a.

Install the No. 1 tilt steering support reinforcement to the steering tilt lever with the steering tilt lever bolt.

Torque:
5.4 N*m (55 kgf*cm, 48 in.*lbf)

NOTICE:

The tightening direction for the steering tilt lever bolt is the tightening direction for bolts with a left-handed thread.



45833 12.TIGHTEN NO. 1 TILT STEERING ADJUSTING NUT

a.

Lock the steering tilt lever.


b.

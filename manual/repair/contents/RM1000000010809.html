1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EGR COOLER(w/ DPF)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

25630 1.INSTALL NO. 2 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket to the EGR cooler assembly in the illustration.


b.

Install the No. 2 EGR valve assembly to the EGR cooler assembly.


c.

Install a new gasket to the No. 2 EGR valve assembly in the illustration.


d.

Install the No. 3 EGR pipe with the 2 nuts and 2 bolts.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


25601 2.INSTALL NO. 1 EGR PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket to the No. 1 EGR pipe sub-assembly.

HINT:

Make sure that the claw of the gasket faces the No. 1 EGR pipe sub-assembly.



b.

Temporarily install the No. 1 EGR pipe sub-assembly with the 2 bolts.


1628A 3.INSTALL NO. 6 WATER BY-PASS PIPE WITH NO. 7 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Connect the No. 7 water by-pass hose to the No. 2 EGR valve assembly, slide the clamp to secure the hose, and then install the No. 6 water by-pass pipe with No. 7 water by-pass hose.

SEAT BELT  FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Short Body)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedures listed below is for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

73298B 1.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM BELT HOLE COVER LH

a.

Remove the 2 screws.


b.

Pass the front seat outer belt assembly LH through the gap of the quarter trim belt hole cover LH and remove the quarter trim belt hole cover LH.


73299 2.REMOVE NO. 2 QUARTER TRIM BELT HOLE COVER LH (for 3 Point Type Rear Seat Belt)

a.
1.438,1.781 2.104,1.615 true 1.469,0.833 1.615,0.323 true 1.229,1.729 1.542,1.885 0.313,0.156 10 false *1 1.563,0.104 1.792,0.292 0.229,0.188 10 true *2
*1 Rear Seat Outer 3 Point Type Belt Assembly LH

Remove the 2 screws.


b.

Pass the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH through the gap of the No. 2 quarter trim belt hole cover LH and remove the No. 2 quarter trim belt hole cover LH.


62512 3.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD LH

a.

for 3 Point Type Rear Seat Belt:

i.

Detach the 13 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.



b.

except 3 Point Type Rear Seat Belt:

i.

for LH Side:

Detach the 12 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.


ii.

for RH Side:

Detach the 12 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.



73220 4.REMOVE FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the bolt and the floor anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the shoulder anchor cover of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.


c.

Loosen the bolt and remove the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts.


e.

Detach the guide and remove the front seat outer belt assembly LH.

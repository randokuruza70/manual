PROCEDURE

81740 1.DISCONNECT SIDE TURN SIGNAL LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Place your hand at the location indicated by the white arrow shown in the illustration and slide the side turn signal light assembly LH toward the front of the vehicle.


b.

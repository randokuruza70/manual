BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM  C1425  Open in Stop Light Switch Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

The brake actuator assembly (skid control ECU) has an open detection circuit, which stores this DTC when detecting an open in the stop light input line or the ground line of the stop light circuit with the stop light switch off (brake pedal not depressed).

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1425 Open in Stop Light Switch Circuit When the IG1 terminal voltage is between 9.5 and 17.4 V, an open in the stop light circuit continues for 0.3 seconds or more.
·

Stop light circuit

·

STP terminal circuit

·

Brake actuator assembly (Skid control ECU)

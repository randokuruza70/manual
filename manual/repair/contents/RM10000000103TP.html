19850 1.CHECK GLOW PLUG ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the No. 1 glow plug connector.

Item Tester Connection Condition Specified Condition
w/ DPF Glow plug terminal - Body ground 20°C (68°F) 0.7 Ω or higher
w/o DPF Glow plug terminal - Body ground 20°C (68°F) Approximately 1.0 Ω


NOTICE:
·

Be careful not to damage the glow plug assembly, as this may cause an open circuit or shorten the life of the glow plug assemblies.

·

Do not allow oil or gasoline to contact the glow plug assembly when cleaning it.

·

Clean any oil on the terminal and bakelite washer with a dry cloth.

·

Do not apply more than 11 V to the glow plug assembly, as this may cause an open circuit.



If the result is not as specified, replace the glow plug assembly.



c.

Install the No. 1 glow plug connector.

(Magnet Clutch Assembly)


a.

Check the magnet clutch operation.

i.

Confirm that the magnet clutch hub and magnet clutch rotor lock when the battery positive lead is connected to terminal 3 of the magnet clutch assembly, and the negative lead is connected to the ground wire.

If the operation is not as specified, replace the magnet clutch assembly.



b.

Measure the resistance between terminals 1 and 2.

Standard resistance:

65 to 125 Ω at 20°C (68°F)


If the result is not as specified, replace the magnet clutch assembly.

STEERING GEAR / LINKAGE  STEERING GEAR  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.

·

When using a vise, do not overtighten it.



HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

44110 1.FIX POWER STEERING GEAR ASSEMBLY

a.
0.781,0.99 1.198,0.719 true 0.49,0.927 0.948,1.125 0.458,0.198 10 false SST

Fix the power steering gear assembly on SST and clamp SST in a vise.

09630-00014  (09631-00142)


44108 2.REMOVE SIDE COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the lock nut and cross shaft adjusting screw seal washer.


b.

Remove the 4 bolts.


c.

Screw in the sector shaft adjusting screw until the cover comes off.


44111 3.REMOVE CROSS SHAFT

a.

Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap on the cross shaft end and pull out the cross shaft.


44154 4.REMOVE POWER PISTON PLUNGER GUIDE NUT

a.

Using a 10 mm hexagon wrench, remove the power piston plunger guide nut.


b.

Remove the power piston plunger compression spring, power piston plunger and power piston plunger guide.


c.

Remove the O-ring from power piston plunger guide nut.


44120 5.REMOVE VALVE ASSEMBLY (WITH BALL NUT)

a.

b.

44114A 7.REMOVE POWER PISTON NUT RING ("TEFLON" RING)

a.

*a Power piston nut


Using a screwdriver, remove the power piston nut ring ("Teflon" Ring) and O-ring.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the power piston nut.



44105C 8.REMOVE VALVE BODY HOUSING UNION SEAT

a.

Using a screw extractor, remove the 2 valve body housing union seats.

NOTICE:
·

Do not allow any shards into the port.

·

In order to prevent shards, forcefully push down on the screw extractor, and then turn a very small amount to the left to pull out the valve body housing union sheet.

·

If the screw extractor is too small, the valve assembly port will be damaged.



HINT:

The valve body housing union seat has an inner diameter of 6 mm.

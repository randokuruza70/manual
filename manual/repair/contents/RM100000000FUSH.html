REAR SUSPENSION  REAR LEAF SPRING  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

48375 1.REMOVE LEAF SPRING DYNAMIC DAMPER (w/ Dynamic Damper)

a.

Remove the bolt, nut and dynamic damper.


48210B 2.REMOVE REAR SPRING PIN BUSH (for 1VD-FTV 76, 78 Series)

a.

Using a chisel and hammer, tap out the bush (inner side).


b.

Using a chisel and hammer, pry the flange of the bush (outer side) outward.


c.

48210Q 3.REMOVE REAR SPRING SHACKLE BUSH (except 1VD-FTV 76, 78 Series)

a.

Remove the 2 bushes from the rear spring.


48220 4.DISASSEMBLE REAR SPRING ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Secure the rear leaf spring assembly in a vise between aluminum plates.


b.

Remove the hexagon bolt, washer and rear spring clip bolt case from the rear leaf spring.


c.

Using a brass bar and hammer, pry open the spring clip of the rear leaf spring.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the leaf spring.



d.

Remove the nut and center bolt.


e.

Remove the rear spring silencers from the leaf spring.

DESCRIPTION

DTC output mode is set by connecting terminals TC and CG of the DLC3. The DTCs are communicated through SRS warning light blinking patterns.

HINT:

When one or more of the warning lights blink continuously, the cause may be a ground short in the wiring of terminal TC of the DLC3 or an internal ground short in an ECU.

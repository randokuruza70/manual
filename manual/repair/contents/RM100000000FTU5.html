PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE CUSHION

a.

Remove the 2 cushions.


53381 2.REMOVE HOOD TO RADIATOR SUPPORT SEAL

a.

Using a clip remover, detach the 8 clips and remove the hood to radiator support seal.


53341B 3.REMOVE HOOD INSULATOR (w/ Hood Insulator)

a.

w/o Hood Bulge:

i.

Using a clip remover, detach the 12 clips and remove the hood insulator.



b.

w/ Hood Bulge:

i.

Using a clip remover, detach the 11 clips and remove the hood insulator.



53390 4.REMOVE WINDSHIELD REST CRADLE ASSEMBLY (for Canvas Top)

a.

Remove the 4 nuts and 2 windshield rest cradle assembly.

a.

Detach the 4 clamps and disconnect the washer hose assembly.


76181 7.REMOVE HOOD BULGE (w/ Hood Bulge)

a.

Remove the 7 nuts and hood bulge.


76187 8.REMOVE NO. 1 HOOD AIR INTAKE GUIDE RETAINER (w/ Hood Bulge)

a.

Remove the screw and No. 1 hood air intake guide retainer from the hood bulge.

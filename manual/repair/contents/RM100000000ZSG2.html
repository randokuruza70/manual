1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER HEAD  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

13711 1.REMOVE VALVE

HINT:

Arrange the valves, inner compression springs, valve spring retainers and valve spring retainer locks in the correct order.


a.

b.

Using SST, compress the inner compression spring and remove the valve spring retainer lock.

09202-70020  (09202-00010)


c.

Remove the valve spring retainer, inner compression spring and valve.


13711A 2.REMOVE VALVE STEM OIL SEAL

a.

Using pliers, remove the valve stem oil seal.


13734 3.REMOVE VALVE SPRING SEAT

a.

Using compressed air and a magnet hand, remove the valve spring seats by blowing air.

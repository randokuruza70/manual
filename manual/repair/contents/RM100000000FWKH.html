BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  LOAD SENSING PROPORTIONING VALVE  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN BRAKE FLUID

NOTICE:

Wash off brake fluid immediately if it comes in contact with any painted surface.


2.DISCONNECT BRAKE TUBE

a.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the 3 brake tubes from the valve body.


3.REMOVE LOAD SENSING PROPORTIONING VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts from the No. 1 load sensing shackle bracket.


b.

Remove the 4 bolts and load sensing proportioning valve.


47932A 4.REMOVE LOAD SENSING VALVE UNION BRACKET

a.

Remove the 4 bolts and bracket.

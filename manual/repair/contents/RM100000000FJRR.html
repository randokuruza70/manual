1VD-FTV FUEL  FUEL TANK(for 90 L Tank Carried in Middle)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN FUEL


a.

Remove the fuel tank drain plug and gasket, and then drain the fuel.


b.

Install a new gasket and the fuel tank drain plug.

Torque:
6.5 N*m (66 kgf*cm, 58 in.*lbf)

4.REMOVE FUEL TUBE PROTECTOR


a.

Remove the 4 bolts and fuel tube protector.


77206C 5.DISCONNECT NO. 2 FUEL MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

for Single Tank Type:

i.

Remove the 3 bolts.


ii.

Slide the 6 clamps and disconnect the 6 fuel hoses from the No. 2 fuel main tube sub-assembly.


iii.

Disconnect the No. 2 fuel main tube sub-assembly from the fuel tube clamps.




b.

for Double Tank Type:

i.

Remove the 3 bolts.


ii.

Slide the 8 clamps and disconnect the 8 fuel hoses from the No. 2 fuel main tube sub-assembly.


iii.

Disconnect the No. 2 fuel main tube sub-assembly from the fuel tube clamps.




77207B 6.REMOVE NO. 3 FUEL MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

for Single Tank Type:

i.

Slide the 4 clamps and disconnect the 4 fuel hoses from the No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.


ii.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.



b.

for Double Tank Type:

i.

Slide the 6 clamps and disconnect the 6 fuel hoses from the No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.


ii.

Remove the 2 bolts and No. 3 fuel main tube sub-assembly.



7.DISCONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel main hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


8.DISCONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel return hose from the fuel suction tube assembly.


77213 9.DISCONNECT INLET FUEL HOSE

a.

Loosen the clamp and disconnect the inlet fuel hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100D 10.DISCONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the fuel breather hose from the fuel tank assembly.


77100 11.REMOVE FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.


b.

Set an engine lifter underneath the fuel tank assembly.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts and disconnect the 2 fuel tank bands.


d.

Slightly lower the engine lifter.


e.

Remove the fuel tank protector from the fuel tank assembly.


77026A 12.REMOVE NO. 1 FUEL EVAPORATION TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 8 screws, 2 nuts and No. 1 fuel evaporation tube sub-assembly from the fuel tank assembly.


b.

Remove the 2 gaskets from the No. 1 evaporation tube sub-assembly.


77380 13.REMOVE FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the fuel cut off cut off valve assembly from the fuel tank assembly.


77420 14.REMOVE NO. 2 FUEL CUT OFF VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the No. 2 fuel cut off valve assembly from the fuel tank assembly.

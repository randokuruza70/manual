NOTICE:

Do not damage the glow plug pipes as this could cause an open circuit or shorten the life of the glow plug assemblies.

·

Prevent oil and fuel from spraying onto the glow plug assemblies when cleaning.

·

Wipe any oil off the terminal and Bakelite washer with a dry cloth if they are stained with oil during the inspection.

11381A 1.INSTALL REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL

a.

Apply engine oil to the lip of a new rear crankshaft oil seal.


b.

Standard depth A:

2.7 to 3.7 mm (0.106 to 0.146 in.)


NOTICE:
·

Keep the lip free from foreign matter.

·

Do not tap on the rear crankshaft oil seal at an angle.

·

Make sure that the lip of the rear crankshaft oil seal is properly installed.

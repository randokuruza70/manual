PROCEDURE

28120 1.REMOVE STARTER YOKE ASSEMBLY

a.

Open the terminal cap.


b.

Remove the nut and disconnect the lead wire from the terminal C.


c.

Remove the 2 through bolts.


d.

Pull out the starter yoke and starter commutator end frame together with the starter armature assembly.


e.

Remove the felt washer from the starter armature assembly.

28140 3.REMOVE STARTER BRUSH HOLDER ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 screws, and then remove the commutator end frame from the starter yoke assembly.

NOTICE:

While holding down the lead wire, remove the starter commutator end frame.



b.

Remove the dust protector from the starter commutator end frame assembly.


c.

Disconnect the 4 brushes from the starter brush holder assembly.

i.

Using a screwdriver, hold back the brush spring.


ii.

Disconnect the brush from the starter brush holder assembly.



d.

Remove the starter brush holder assembly.

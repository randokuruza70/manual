CRUISE CONTROL  CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM  Clutch Switch Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

While depressing the clutch pedal, the clutch switch sends a signal to terminal CLSW of the ECM. The ECM cancels cruise control when terminal CLSW receives the signal.

CRUISE CONTROL  CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM  P0571  Brake Switch "A" Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

When the brake pedal is depressed, the stop light switch assembly sends a signal to the ECM. Upon receiving the signal, the ECM cancels the cruise control system.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
P0571 Brake Switch "A" Circuit When voltage of STP terminal and that of ST1- terminal of ECM are less than 1 V for 0.5 seconds or more

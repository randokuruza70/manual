1.INSTALL FRONT ENGINE MOUNTING INSULATOR

a.
0.104,0.083 0.417,0.24 0.313,0.156 10 false *A 1.75,0.083 2.063,0.24 0.313,0.156 10 false *B
*A for RH Side
*B for LH Side


Install the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH to the 2 mounting brackets with a new 2 nut.

Torque:
83 N*m (846 kgf*cm, 61 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Do not reuse the removed nut.



2.REMOVE ENGINE STAND

a.

Attach an engine sling device and hang the engine with a chain block.


b.

Lift the engine and remove it from the engine stand.


c.

Place the engine onto a work bench.


3.INSTALL ENGINE ASSEMBLY

a.

Attach an engine sling device and hang the engine with a chain block.


b.

Slowly lower the engine into the engine compartment.


c.
0.083,0.073 0.333,0.292 0.25,0.219 10 false *A 1.854,0.063 2.115,0.271 0.26,0.208 10 false *B
*A for RH Side
*B for LH Side


Install 2 new front engine support spacers and 2 new nuts to the front engine mounting insulator RH and front engine mounting insulator LH.

Torque:
for RH side : 74 N*m (755 kgf*cm, 55 ft.*lbf)
for LH side : 87 N*m (887 kgf*cm, 64 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

The torque of the nut that connects the front engine mounting insulator to the body is different for the front engine mounting insulator RH and engine mounting insulator LH.

·

Do not reuse the removed 2 front engine support spacers and 2 nuts.

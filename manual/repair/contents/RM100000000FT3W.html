WINDOW / GLASS  BACK WINDOW GLASS  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

64811 1.REMOVE BACK WINDOW GLASS

a.

When reusing the back window glass:

Pry the lip of the back window glass windshield weatherstrip outward from the interior part of the body flange.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.



b.

When reusing the back window glass:

Using suction cups and remove the back window glass.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the back window glass.



c.

When replacing the back window glass:

Using a knife, cut off the back window glass weatherstrip as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the body with the knife.


HINT:

After partially disconnecting the part, pull by hand to finish removing it.



d.

When replacing the back window glass:

Using suction cups and remove the back window glass.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the back window glass.

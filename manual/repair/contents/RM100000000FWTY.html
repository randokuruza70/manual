RM25T0E _58 Vehicle Interior _029931 SEAT BELT _0138409 SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM D

SEAT BELT  SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM  OPERATION CHECK  


INSPECT DRIVER SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM


a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

When the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the driver side seat belt warning light in the combination meter blinks.


c.

When the driver side seat belt is fastened, check that the driver side seat belt warning light in the combination meter turns off.



INSPECT SEAT BELT WARNING BUZZER


a.

except 1VD-FTV:

i.

When the ignition switch is turned to ON while the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds 5 times.


ii.

When the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h or more while the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the buzzer in the combination meter sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 9.6 seconds, and then sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 20 seconds.


iii.

Check that the buzzer stops 29.6 seconds after the buzzer starts sounding.


iv.

After the buzzer stops, turn the ignition switch off and then start the engine again. Then drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h or more while the driver side seat belt is not fastened and check that the buzzer sounds again.


v.

After the buzzer stops, fasten the driver side seat belt. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h or more and unfasten the driver side seat belt, and then check that the buzzer sounds again.



b.

for 1VD-FTV:

i.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


ii.

When the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h or more while the driver side seat belt is not fastened, check that the buzzer in the combination meter sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 30 seconds, and then sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 90 seconds.


iii.

Check that the buzzer stops 2 minutes after the buzzer starts sounding.


iv.

After the buzzer stops, turn the ignition switch off and then start the engine again. Then drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h or more while the driver side seat belt is not fastened and check that the buzzer sounds again.


v.

After the buzzer stops, fasten the driver side seat belt. Drive the vehicle at approximately 20 km/h or more and unfasten the driver side seat belt, and then check that the buzzer sounds again.

DESCRIPTION

The door side airbag sensor RH circuit consists of the airbag sensor assembly and door side airbag sensor RH.

The door side airbag sensor RH detects impacts to the vehicle and sends signals to the airbag sensor assembly to determine if the airbag should be deployed.

DTC B1622/81 or B1622/81 is stored when a malfunction is detected in the door side airbag sensor RH circuit.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
B1622/81 Lost communication with Side Airbag Sensor RH One of the following conditions is met:
·

The airbag sensor assembly detects a line short circuit signal, open circuit signal, short circuit to ground signal or short circuit to B+ signal in the door side airbag sensor RH circuit for 2 seconds.

1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  DTC CHECK / CLEAR  


NOTICE:

When the diagnosis system is changed from the normal mode to check mode or vice versa, all DTCs and freeze frame data recorded in normal mode are erased. Before changing modes, always check and make a note of DTCs and freeze frame data.


HINT:
·

DTCs which are stored in the ECM can be displayed on the GTS. The GTS can display current and pending DTCs.

·

The same DTCs are not set if the ECM does not detect the same malfunction again during a second consecutive driving cycle. However, such malfunctions, detected on only one occasion, are stored as pending DTCs.



CHECK DTC (Using GTS)


a.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


b.

Turn the ignition switch ON.


c.

Turn the GTS ON.


d.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Trouble Codes.

Powertrain > Engine and ECT > Trouble Codes
50001 61

e.

Check the DTC(s) and freeze frame data, and then write them down.


f.

Check the details of the DTC(s).

CLEAR DTC (Using GTS)


a.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


b.

Turn the ignition switch ON.


c.

Turn the GTS ON.


d.

Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine and ECT / Trouble Codes / Clear.

Powertrain > Engine and ECT > Clear DTCs
50002 61

e.

Press the YES button.



CLEAR DTC (Without using GTS)


a.

Perform either one of the following operations.

i.

Disconnect the negative battery cable for more than 1 minute.

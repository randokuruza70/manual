1GR-FE LUBRICATION  OIL PRESSURE SENSOR  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

83520 1.INSTALL OIL PRESSURE SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

a.

Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads of the oil pressure sender gauge assembly.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1344, Three Bond 1344 or equivalent


NOTICE:

Do not apply adhesive to the oil inlet port of the oil pressure sender gauge assembly.



b.

Using a 27 mm deep socket wrench, install the oil pressure sender gauge assembly.

Torque:
15 N*m (153 kgf*cm, 11 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Do not start the engine within 1 hour of installation.



c.

Connect the oil pressure sender gauge connector.

73370 9.REMOVE REAR SEAT OUTER 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the floor anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the rear seat outer 3 point type belt shoulder anchor cover.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



c.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt from the vehicle body.


d.

Remove the bolt.


e.

Detach the 3 guides and remove the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH.

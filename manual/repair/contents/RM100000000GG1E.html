SEAT BELT  REAR SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Double Cab)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedures listed below are for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

62512 1.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD LH

a.

Using a clip remover, remove the 3 clips.


b.

Using a clip remover, detach the 2 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.


73370 2.REMOVE REAR SEAT OUTER 3 POINT TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the floor anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


b.

Using a moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the rear seat outer 3 point type belt shoulder anchor cover.


c.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH from the vehicle body.


d.

Remove the 2 bolts.


e.

Detach the guide and remove the rear seat outer 3 point type belt assembly LH.

POWER ASSIST SYSTEMS  VANE PUMP(for 1GR-FE)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

44310 1.INSTALL VANE PUMP ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the vane pump assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


2.INSTALL WIRE HARNESS BRACKET

a.

Install the wire harness bracket with the bolt.

Torque:
40 N*m (408 kgf*cm, 30 ft.*lbf)


44410 3.CONNECT PRESSURE FEED TUBE ASSEMBLY

a.
2.281,1.906 2.01,1.615 true 2.323,1.906 2.635,2.104 0.313,0.198 10 false *a
*a Stopper


Install a new gasket to the pressure feed tube assembly.


b.

Connect the pressure feed tube with the union bolt.

Torque:
50 N*m (510 kgf*cm, 37 ft.*lbf)

HINT:

Make sure that the stopper of the tube touches the vane pump assembly body as shown in the illustration, then tighten the union bolt.



4.CONNECT POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH CONNECTOR

a.

Connect the power steering oil pressure switch connector.


44348A 5.CONNECT NO. 1 OIL RESERVOIR TO PUMP HOSE

a.

Connect the No. 1 oil reservoir to pump hose to the vane pump assembly, and slide the clip to secure it.

15.INSPECT AIR CLEANER FILTER

a.

Remove the air cleaner filter.


b.

Visually check that the air cleaner filter is not excessively damaged or oily. If necessary, replace the air cleaner filter with a new one.


c.

If cleaning an air cleaner filter element, blow compressed air to clean it.


d.

Reinstall the air cleaner filter.


16.INSPECT AIR CLEANER CASE

a.

Check if foreign matter has accumulated in the bottom of the air cleaner case. If foreign matter has accumulated in the bottom of the air cleaner case, clean the air cleaner case.

NOTICE:

When cleaning the air cleaner case, prevent any foreign matter from entering into the engine.

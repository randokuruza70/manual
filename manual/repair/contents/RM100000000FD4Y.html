HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM  SYSTEM DESCRIPTION  


FUNCTION OF MAIN COMPONENTS


a.

The main components of the air conditioning system have the following functions:

Component Function

*1: w/ Air Conditioning System

*2: w/ Heater

Heater Blower Switch Assembly Allows operation and adjustment of the air conditioning system via switches.
Cooler Stabilizer Amplifier Assembly Transmits and receives signals to and from the switches and sensors.
Cooler Compressor Assembly*1 A compact, lightweight, and low-noise 10-cylinder swash plate type cooler compressor is used.
Cooler Condenser Assembly*1 A multi-flow sub-cool condenser is used to improve heat exchange efficiency.
Heater Radiator Assembly*2 A multi-flow type heater radiator is used for compactness and high performance.
Blower with Motor Sub-assembly The blower with fan motor sub-assembly is controlled by the blower switch on the heater blower switch assembly.
No. 1 Cooler Evaporator Sub-assembly*1 A multi-flow type evaporator is used for compactness.
No. 1 Cooler Thermistor*1 Detects the temperature of the cool air past the No. 1 cooler evaporator sub-assembly and transmits the data to the cooler stabilizer amplifier assembly.
ECM Transmits the air conditioning off request signal to the cooler stabilizer amplifier assembly.

DESCRIPTION

The driver side knee squib circuit consists of the airbag sensor assembly and lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly.

This circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction is detected in the driver side knee squib circuit.

·

The airbag sensor assembly detects a line short circuit signal 5 times in the driver side knee squib circuit during the primary check.

·

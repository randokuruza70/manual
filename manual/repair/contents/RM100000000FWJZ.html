BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE BOOSTER  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1.INSPECT BRAKE VACUUM CHECK VALVE ASSEMBLY (for 1GR-FE)

a.
0.833,0.302 1.146,0.5 0.313,0.198 10 false *a 2.094,0.313 2.406,0.51 0.313,0.198 10 false *b
*a Ventilation
*b No Ventilation


Check that there is ventilation from the booster to the engine, and no ventilation from the engine to the booster.

If the result is not as specified, replace the brake vacuum check valve assembly.

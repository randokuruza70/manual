89175 2.INSTALL AIRBAG SENSOR BRACKET

a.

Check that the ignition switch is off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.



c.

Check that the waterproof sheet is properly set.


d.

Temporarily install the airbag sensor bracket with the 3 bolts.

NOTICE:
·

If the airbag sensor has been dropped, or there are any cracks, dents or other defects in the case, bracket or connector, replace airbag sensor assembly with a new one.

·

When installing the airbag sensor, be careful that the SRS wiring does not interfere with other parts and that it is not pinched between other parts.

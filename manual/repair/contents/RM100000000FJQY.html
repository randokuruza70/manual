NOTICE:

Be sure to install the fuel injector LH, nozzle holder clamp and bolt in their original positions.


a.

NOTICE:

·

Always install a fuel injector LH to the same place it was removed from.

NOTICE:
·

·

p.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

If the union bolt is accidentally tightened beyond the torque specification, it must be replaced.

·

If the No. 2 nozzle leakage pipe is accidentally tightened beyond the torque specification, it must be replaced.

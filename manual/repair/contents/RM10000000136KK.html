DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0101 Mass or Volume Air Flow Circuit Range / Performance Problem When one of the following conditions is met:
·

After engine is warmed up, condition (a) continues for more than 10 seconds at idling (2 trip detection logic):

1.

Mass air flow meter output more than 4.4 V


·

Conditions (a) and (b) continue for more than 10 seconds with engine speed at 1250 rpm or more (2 trip detection logic):

1.

VTA more than 0.1 V

2.

Mass air flow meter output less than 0.7 V



Mass air flow meter Comes on DTC stored

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

Read freeze frame data using the GTS. Freeze frame data records the engine condition when malfunctions are detected. When troubleshooting, freeze frame data can help determine if the vehicle was moving or stationary, if the engine was warmed up or not, and other data from the time the malfunction occurred.

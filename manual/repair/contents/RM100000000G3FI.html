PROCEDURE

84340 1.INSTALL STOP LIGHT SWITCH ASSEMBLY

a.
0.313,1.104 0.625,1.26 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Protrusion Amount Measurement Area


Turn the stop light switch assembly in the clockwise direction until it reaches the standard shaft protrusion amount and temporarily install it.

Standard:

0.5 to 2.4 mm (0.0197 to 0.0945 in.)


NOTICE:

Do not depress the brake pedal.



b.

Tighten the lock nut.

Torque:
16.7 N*m (170 kgf*cm, 12 ft.*lbf)


c.

Connect the connector.

WINDOW / GLASS  WINDSHIELD GLASS(for Canvas Top)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

56111 1.CLEAN WINDSHIELD GLASS

a.

Clean the outer circumference of the glass with non-residue solvent.

NOTICE:
·

Do not touch the glass surface after cleaning it.

·

Be careful not to damage the glass.

·

Even if using new glass, clean the glass with non-residue solvent.

a.

Apply primer to the windshield weatherstrip on the surface with body as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:
·

Do not apply too much primer.

·

Let the primer coating dry for 3 minutes or more.




b.

Install the windshield glass by pulling the cord from the room side, while pushing windshield weatherstrip from the outside with your open hand.


c.

To mark the glass fit in place, tap from the outside with your open hand.


d.

Put masking tape around the weatherstrip to protect the paint and glass.


e.

Conduct a leak test after the adhesive has completely hardened.


f.

Apply auto glass sealer to the weatherstrip lip as shown in the illustration.


g.

Remove the protected tape.


h.

Using a cleaner, clean the weatherstrip contacting surface of the body and glass.

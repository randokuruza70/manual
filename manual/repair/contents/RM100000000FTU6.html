DOOR / HATCH  HOOD  ADJUSTMENT  


PROCEDURE

53301 1.ADJUST HOOD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Adjust the hood's position.

i.

Loosen the hood's hinge bolts and adjust the hood's position.


ii.

Move the hood and adjust the clearance between the hood and front fender.


iii.

Tighten the 4 hood's hinge bolts after the adjustment.

Torque:
18 N*m (184 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)


iv.

Adjust the cushion rubber so that the height of the hood and fender are aligned.

HINT:

Raise or lower the hood's front end by turning the cushion rubber.




b.

Adjust the hood lock.

i.

Loosen the 3 bolts.


ii.

Adjust the hood lock position so that the striker can enter it smoothly.


iii.

Tighten the 3 bolts after the adjustment.

Torque:
7.8 N*m (80 kgf*cm, 69 in.*lbf)

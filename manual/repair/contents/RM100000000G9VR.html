2.INSPECT STEERING LINKAGE AND GEAR BOX

a.

Check the steering wheel free play.


b.

Check the steering linkage for looseness or damage.

i.

Check that the tie rod ends do not have excessive play.


ii.

Check that the steering relay rod ends do not have excessive play.


iii.

Check that the dust seals and boots are not damaged.


iv.

Check that the boot clamps are not loose.


v.

Check that the steering gear box is not damaged.


vi.

Check that the connectors are properly connected to the steering gear box.



3.LUBRICATE STEERING KNUCLE, DRAG LINK, KINGPIN, CENTER ARM AND STEERING LINKAGE GREASE

a.

Lubricate the relay rod. Before pumping in grease, wipe off any mud and dust on the grease fitting.

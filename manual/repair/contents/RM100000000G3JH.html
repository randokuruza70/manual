WIPER / WASHER  FRONT WIPER RUBBER  REPLACEMENT  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.


·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.


·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.


·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

85220 1.REMOVE WINDSHIELD WIPER BLADE ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the claw as shown in the illustration.


b.

Remove the windshield wiper blade assembly as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not lower the front wiper arm with the front wiper blade removed. The arm tip may damage the windshield surface.



85214B 2.REMOVE WIPER RUBBER LH

a.

Detach the head part (large side) of the wiper rubber LH from the wiper blade.


b.

*a Head Part (Large Side)


Remove the wiper rubber LH in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not pull out the wiper rubber forcibly. Doing so will deform the backing plate or damage the blade claw.



85224A 3.REMOVE NO. 1 WIPER RUBBER BACKING PLATE

a.

Remove the No. 1 wiper rubber backing plate from the wiper rubber LH.


85224B 4.REMOVE NO. 2 WIPER RUBBER BACKING PLATE

a.

Remove the No. 2 wiper rubber backing plate from the wiper rubber LH.

85214B 7.INSTALL WIPER RUBBER LH

Install the wiper rubber LH to the claw of the wiper blade in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

HINT:

Install the wiper rubber LH so that the head part (large side) of the wiper rubber faces the arm axle side.



b.

Attach the head part (large side) of the wiper rubber LH to the claw on the end of the wiper blade.

NOTICE:

Push the wiper blade into the grooves of the wiper rubber LH so that the wiper rubber LH is completely attached.



85220 8.INSTALL WINDSHIELD WIPER BLADE ASSEMBLY

a.

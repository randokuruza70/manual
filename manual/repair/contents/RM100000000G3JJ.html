WIPER / WASHER  REAR WIPER RUBBER  REPLACEMENT  


PROCEDURE

85220R 1.REMOVE REAR WIPER BLADE ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the claw as shown in the illustration.


b.

Remove the rear wiper blade assembly as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not lower the rear wiper arm with the rear wiper blade removed. The arm tip may damage the back door glass RH surface.



85214R 2.REMOVE REAR WIPER RUBBER

a.

Detach the head part (large side) of the rear wiper rubber from the rear wiper blade.


b.

Remove the rear wiper rubber in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not pull out the wiper rubber forcibly. Doing so will deform the backing plate or damage the blade claw.



85224E 3.REMOVE NO. 1 REAR WIPER RUBBER BACKING PLATE

a.

Remove the No. 1 rear wiper rubber backing plate from the rear wiper rubber.


85224F 4.REMOVE NO. 2 REAR WIPER RUBBER BACKING PLATE

a.

Remove the No. 2 rear wiper rubber backing plate from the rear wiper rubber.

85214R 7.INSTALL REAR WIPER RUBBER

a.

Install the rear wiper rubber to the claw of the rear wiper blade in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration.

HINT:

Install the rear wiper rubber so that the head part (large side) of the rear wiper rubber faces the arm axle side.



b.

Attach the head part (large side) of the rear wiper rubber to the claw on the end of the rear wiper blade.

NOTICE:

Push the rear wiper blade into the grooves of the rear wiper rubber so that the rear wiper rubber is completely attached.



85220R 8.INSTALL REAR WIPER BLADE ASSEMBLY

a.

Attach the claw to install the rear wiper blade assembly.

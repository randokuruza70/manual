WINCH  WINCH FLUID  REPLACEMENT  


PROCEDURE

1.DRAIN WINCH FLUID


a.

Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, remove the drain plug.


b.

Drain the winch fluid.


2.ADD WINCH FLUID


a.

Fill the winch with ATF D-II or equivalent.

Fluid type:

ATF D-II or equivalent


Capacity:

0.9 to 1.0 liters (1.0 to 1.1 US qts, 0.8 to 0.9 Imp. qts)


NOTICE:

Do not overfill the winch.



b.

Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, Install the drain plug.

Torque:
17 N*m (175 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)

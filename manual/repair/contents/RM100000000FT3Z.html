WINDOW / GLASS  BACK DOOR GLASS(for RH Side)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

68131C 1.REMOVE BACK DOOR GLASS RH

a.

When reusing the back door glass weatherstrip RH:

i.

Pry the lip of the back door glass weatherstrip RH outward from the interior part of the body flange.


ii.

Using suction cups and remove the back door glass RH.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the back door glass.




b.

When replacing the back door glass weatherstrip RH:

i.

Using a knife, cut off the back door glass weatherstrip RH as shown in the illustration.



c.

Using suction cups, remove the back door glass RH.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the back door glass.



68291D 2.REMOVE BACK DOOR GLASS WEATHERSTRIP RH

a.

Remove back door glass weatherstrip RH.

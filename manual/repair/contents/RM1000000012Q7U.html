BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM  U0073  Control Module Communication Bus OFF  U0100  Lost Communication with ECM  U0124  Lost Communication with Lateral Acceleration Sensor Module  U0126  Lost Communication with Steering Angle Sensor Module  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) receives the signals from the ECM, steering angle sensor (spiral with sensor cable sub-assembly) and yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag sensor assembly) via the CAN communication system.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
U0073 Control Module Communication Bus OFF Any of the following is detected:
1.

A bus OFF state occurs once or more within 0.1 seconds, 10 times in succession. (Sent signals cannot be received.)

2.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 10 and 17.4 V, a delay in receiving data from the yaw rate sensor and steering angle sensor continues for 1 second or more.

3.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 10 and 17.4 V, the following occurs 10 times in succession within 60 seconds.

·

The condition that a delay in receiving data from the yaw rate sensor and steering angle sensor occurs more than once within 5 seconds.



CAN communication system
U0100 Lost Communication with ECM When the +BS terminal voltage is between 10 and 17.4 V, and the vehicle speed is 15 km/h (9 mph) or more, data cannot be received from the ECM for 2 seconds or more.

1.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 10 and 17.4 V, and the vehicle speed is 7 km/h or more, data from the acceleration sensor cannot be received for 1 second or more.

2.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 10 and 17.4 V, the following occurs 10 times in succession within 60 seconds.

·

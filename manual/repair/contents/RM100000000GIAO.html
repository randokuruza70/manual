BF1B TRANSFER / 4WD / AWD  TRANSFER ASSEMBLY  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

36203 1.INSPECT TRANSFER HIGH SPEED OUTPUT GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE

a.

Using a feeler gauge, measure the thrust clearance of the transfer high speed output gear sub-assembly.

Standard clearance:

0.10 to 0.25 mm (0.0039 to 0.0098 in.)


Maximum clearance:

0.25 mm (0.0098 in.)


If the clearance is more than the maximum, replace the transfer high speed output gear sub-assembly.


36204 2.INSPECT TRANSFER LOW SPEED OUTPUT GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE

a.

Using a feeler gauge, measure the thrust clearance of the transfer low speed output gear sub-assembly.

Standard clearance:

0.10 to 0.25 mm (0.0039 to 0.0098 in.)


Maximum clearance:

0.25 mm (0.0098 in.)


If the clearance is more than the maximum, replace the transfer low speed output gear sub-assembly.


36203 3.INSPECT TRANSFER HIGH SPEED OUTPUT GEAR RADIAL CLEARANCE


a.

Using a dial indicator, measure the radial clearance of the transfer high speed output gear sub-assembly.

Standard clearance:

0.019 to 0.068 mm (0.0007 to 0.0027 in.)


Maximum clearance:

0.068 mm (0.0027 in.)


If the clearance is more than the maximum, replace the transfer high speed output gear sub-assembly, rear transfer output shaft or transfer output shaft taper roller bearing.


36204 4.INSPECT TRANSFER LOW SPEED OUTPUT GEAR RADIAL CLEARANCE

a.

Using a dial indicator, measure the radial clearance of the transfer low speed output gear sub-assembly.

Standard clearance:

0.019 to 0.068 mm (0.0007 to 0.0027 in.)


Maximum clearance:

0.068 mm (0.0027 in.)


If the clearance is more than the maximum, replace the transfer low speed output gear sub-assembly, rear transfer output shaft or transfer output shaft taper roller bearing.

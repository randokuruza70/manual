1HZ STARTING  GLOW PLUG  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

19850 1.INSTALL GLOW PLUG ASSEMBLY

a.

Clean the glow plug assembly installation holes in the cylinder head.


b.

Using a 12 mm deep socket wrench, install the 6 glow plug assemblies.

Torque:
12.7 N*m (130 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)


19871 2.INSTALL NO. 1 GLOW PLUG CONNECTOR

a.

Install the No. 1 glow plug connector and wire harness with the 6 nuts.

Torque:
2.2 N*m (22 kgf*cm, 19 in.*lbf)


b.

Install the 6 glow plug screw grommets.

HINT:

Push the glow plug screw grommet into the threaded portion of the glow plug assembly by hand, and then turn it clockwise.



3.CONNECT CABLE TO NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

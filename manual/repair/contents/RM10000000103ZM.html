CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

When replacing an injector (including interchanging injectors between cylinders) or common rail, replace the corresponding injection pipes with new ones.

·

w/ DPF:

When fuel lines are disconnected, air may enter the fuel lines, leading to engine starting trouble. Therefore, perform forced regeneration and bleed the air from the fuel lines.



PROCEDURE

1. PRECAUTION

NOTICE:

After turning the ignition switch off, waiting time may be required before disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal. Therefore, make sure to read the disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal notice before proceeding with work.

28100 6.REMOVE STARTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the connector from the starter assembly.


b.

Detach the harness clamp and disconnect the wire harness.


c.

Remove the nut and disconnect the starter wire from the starter assembly.


d.

Disconnect the 2 hoses from the starter assembly and starter hose bracket.


e.

Remove the 2 bolts and starter assembly.

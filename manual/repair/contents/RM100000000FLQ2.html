1GR-FE FUEL  FUEL SUB TANK(for Double Tank Type)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

77011 1.INSTALL FUEL TANK FILLER LOWER PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket to the fuel tank filler lower pipe sub-assembly.


b.

Install the fuel tank filler lower pipe sub-assembly with the 6 screws to the fuel sub tank.


77203B 2.INSTALL FUEL SUCTION TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install a new gasket to the fuel suction tube sub-assembly.


b.

Install the fuel suction tube sub-assembly with the 7 screws to the fuel sub tank.


c.

Install the nut.

Torque:
5.5 N*m (56 kgf*cm, 49 in.*lbf)


83320 3.INSTALL FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

4.INSTALL FUEL SUB TANK

a.

Set the fuel sub tank and No. 1 fuel tank protector sub-assembly on an engine lifter.


b.

Raise the engine lifter.


c.

Install the 2 fuel tank bands with the 2 pins.


d.

Connect the 2 fuel tank bands with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
39 N*m (398 kgf*cm, 29 ft.*lbf)


e.

Connect the fuel pump connector and fuel sender gauge connector and attach the wire harness clamp.


77100D 5.CONNECT FUEL BREATHER HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel breather hose to the fuel sub tank and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


77213 6.CONNECT FUEL INLET HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel inlet hose to the fuel tank filler lower pipe sub-assembly and tighten the clamp to secure the hose.


7.CONNECT FUEL RETURN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel return hose to the fuel suction tube sub-assembly and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


8.CONNECT FUEL MAIN HOSE

a.

Connect the fuel main hose to the fuel suction tube sub-assembly.

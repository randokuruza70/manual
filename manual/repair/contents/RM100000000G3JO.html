WIPER / WASHER  WIPER SWITCH  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.


·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

45287 1.REMOVE LOWER STEERING COLUMN COVER

NOTICE:

Removing the lower steering column cover in the incorrect order will cause the parts to break.


a.

While turning the steering wheel to the right and left, remove the 2 screws.


b.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the lower steering column cover.


45286B 2.REMOVE UPPER STEERING COLUMN COVER

a.

Detach the claw and remove the upper steering column cover.


84650 3.REMOVE WINDSHIELD WIPER SWITCH ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect each connector.


b.

Using a screwdriver, detach the claw and remove the windshield wiper switch assembly.

NOTICE:

If the claw is pulled with excessive force, it may break.

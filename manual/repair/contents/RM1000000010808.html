25680 2.REMOVE EGR COOLER SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Slide the clamp and remove the No. 4 water by-pass hose from the EGR cooler sub-assembly.


b.

Slide the clamp and remove the No. 9 water by-pass hose from the No. 2 EGR valve assembly.


c.

Slide the 2 clamps and disconnect the 2 No. 8 water by-pass hoses from the EGR cooler sub-assembly.


d.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 6 water by-pass hose from the No. 6 water by-pass pipe.


e.

Disconnect the No. 2 vacuum transmitting hose assembly from the No. 2 EGR valve assembly.


f.

Remove the 10 bolts and EGR cooler sub-assembly.


1628A 3.REMOVE NO. 6 WATER BY-PASS PIPE WITH NO. 7 WATER BY-PASS HOSE

a.

Slide the clamp and disconnect the No. 7 water by-pass hose from the No. 2 EGR valve assembly and then remove the No. 6 water by-pass pipe with No. 7 water by-pass hose.


25601 4.REMOVE NO. 1 EGR PIPE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts, No. 1 EGR pipe sub-assembly and gasket from the EGR cooler sub-assembly.


25630 5.REMOVE NO. 2 EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 nuts, 2 bolts, No. 3 EGR pipe and gasket.


b.

Remove the No. 2 EGR valve assembly and gasket from the EGR cooler sub-assembly.

10.INSTALL REAR WIPER ARM AND BLADE ASSEMBLY

a.
2.073,2.854 2.563,2.531 true 1.875,0.833 2.365,0.51 true 2.417,0.406 2.729,0.563 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 2.625,2.427 2.938,2.583 0.313,0.156 10 false *b
*a Rear Wiper Arm Serrations
*b Rear Wiper Motor assembly Pivot Serrations


When reinstalling:

i.

Clean the rear wiper arm serrations.

NOTICE:

Do not grind the rear wiper arm serrations.




b.

Clean the rear wiper motor assembly pivot serrations.

NOTICE:

Do not grind the rear wiper motor serrations.



c.

Operate the rear wiper motor assembly and stop at the automatic stop (park) position.


d.
2.698,1.76 2.542,1.99 true 2.385,2.01 2.698,2.167 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 3.104,1.604 3.417,1.76 0.313,0.156 10 false *b
*a Back Door Glass Edge
*b 60 to 80 mm (2.3622 to 3.1496 in.)


Install the rear wiper arm and blade assembly with the nut to the position shown in the illustration.

Torque:
9.0 N*m (92 kgf*cm, 80 in.*lbf)

HINT:

Hold the arm hinge by hand when tightening the nut.



e.

Operate the rear wiper motor assembly while spraying washer fluid on the glass. Make sure that the rear wipers function properly and the wipers do not come into contact with the vehicle body.


f.

Raise the rear wiper arm 2 times and check that the tip of the rear wiper blade is within the range shown in the illustration.

b.

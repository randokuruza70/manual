1VD-FTV EMISSION CONTROL  EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR(for DPF)  INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

89425 1.INSPECT EXHAUST GAS TEMPERATURE SENSOR

a.

Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:
Tester Connection Condition Specified Condition
1 - 2 50 °C (122 °F) 42 to 68 kΩ


HINT:

The temperature indicated in "Condition" is the temperature of the exhaust gas temperature sensor.


If the result is not as specified, replace the exhaust gas temperature sensor.

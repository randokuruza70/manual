BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM  C1337  Diameter of the Tire is not Uniform  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) measures the speed of each wheel by receiving signals from the speed sensors. These signals are used for recognizing whether all 4 wheels are operating properly. Therefore, all wheel signals must indicate the same speed.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1337 Diameter of the Tire is not Uniform The following occurs in 3 consecutive trips:
At a vehicle speed of 20 km/h (12 mph) or more, there is a difference of 20% or more in the average speed between the front wheels and rear wheels for 20 seconds or more.

H150F MANUAL TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE  MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1. CHECK TRANSMISSION OIL

a.

Stop the vehicle on a level surface.


b.

Remove the manual transmission case plug and gasket.


c.
0.708,1.344 1,1.573 0.292,0.229 10 false *a
*a 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.)


Check that the oil level is within 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the manual transmission case plug opening.

If the result is not as specified, add transmission oil.

Manual transmission oil:
Oil grade Oil viscosity Standard capacity
GL-4 SAE 75W-90 2.9 liters (3.1 US qts, 2.6 Imp. qts)


NOTICE:
·

Too much or too little oil will lead to transmission problems.

·

After adjusting the oil level, drive the vehicle and check the oil level again.




d.

Check for oil leakage when the oil level is low.

If leakage is found, repair the area necessary to stop the leak. Replace damaged parts as necessary.


e.

Install a new gasket and the manual transmission case plug.

Torque:
37.24 N*m (380 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)

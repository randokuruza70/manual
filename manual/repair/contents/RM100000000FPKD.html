1HZ STARTING  STARTER(for 3.0 kW Type)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

28120 1.REMOVE STARTER YOKE ASSEMBLY

a.

Open the terminal cap.


b.

Remove the nut and disconnect the lead wire from the terminal C.


c.

Remove the 2 through bolts.


d.

Pull out the starter yoke assembly and starter commutator end frame together with the starter armature assembly.


e.

Remove the 2 O-rings from the starter yoke assembly.

a.

Remove the 3 screws and magnet starter switch assembly from the starter drive housing assembly.

28140 3.REMOVE STARTER BRUSH HOLDER ASSEMBLY

a.

Disconnect the 4 brushes from the starter brush holder assembly.

i.

Using a screwdriver, hold back the brush spring.


ii.

Disconnect the brush from the starter brush holder assembly.



b.

Remove the 4 screws and 4 brushes.


c.

Remove the starter brush holder assembly from the starter yoke assembly.


28160 4.REMOVE STARTER ARMATURE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the starter armature assembly with starter commutator end frame from the starter yoke assembly.


b.

Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap the starter commutator end frame to remove the starter armature assembly from the starter commutator end frame.


c.

Remove the dust protector from the starter commutator end frame.

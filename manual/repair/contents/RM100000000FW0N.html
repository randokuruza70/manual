HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  PRESSURE SWITCH  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

88716B 1.INSPECT COOLER REFRIGERANT LIQUID PIPE B (NO. 1 PRESSURE SWITCH)

a.

Disconnect the connector.


b.
0.042,0.042 0.24,0.25 0.198,0.208 10 false *a
*a Front view of wire harness connector
(Cooler Refrigerant Liquid Pipe B (No. 1 Pressure Switch))


Using a service wire, connect terminals 1 and 2 of the cooler refrigerant liquid pipe B (No. 1 pressure switch) connector on the vehicle wire harness side.


c.

Start the engine.


d.

Turn the A/C switch on and check that the magnet clutch turns on.


e.

Check that the magnet clutch turns off when disconnecting terminals 1 and 2.

OK:
Measurement Condition Specified Condition
Terminals 1 and 2 connected Magnet clutch turns on
Terminals 1 and 2 disconnected Magnet clutch turns off


If the result is not as specified, replace the cooler refrigerant liquid pipe B (No. 1 pressure switch).

BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  DECELERATION SENSOR(w/ ABS)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

89441A 1.INSTALL DECELERATION SENSOR

a.

Connect the deceleration sensor connector.

NOTICE:

Securely connect the connector.



b.
1.156,1.344 1.313,1.542 0.156,0.198 10 true A 2.844,1.615 3,1.813 0.156,0.198 10 false B

Slide the claw of the deceleration sensor into the groove in the floor.


c.

Install the deceleration sensor with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
14.5 N*m (148 kgf*cm, 11 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:
·

Tighten the 2 bolts uniformly in alphabetical order.

·

Do not use any parts which have been dropped or struck.

·

Make sure that there is no foreign matter or carpet between the bracket of the sensor and crossmember.

·

Do not do anything to deform the bracket of the sensor, such as kicking or stepping on it.

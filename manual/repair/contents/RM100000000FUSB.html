48540 2.REMOVE REAR SHOCK ABSORBER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Support the rear axle housing assembly with a jack.


b.

Remove the upper mounting bolt, rear No. 1 shock absorber cushion washer and 2 bushing and disconnect the rear shock absorber assembly LH from the frame.


c.

Remove the lower mounting bolt, rear No. 1 shock absorber cushion washer and 2 bushing and remove the rear shock absorber assembly LH from the spring U bolt seat sub-assembly LH.

SEAT BELT  FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY(for Single Cab)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

73200A 1.INSTALL FRONT SHOULDER BELT ANCHOR ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY (w/ Pretensioner and Force Limiter)

a.

Attach the guide and install the front shoulder belt anchor adjuster assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)


73220 2.INSTALL FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

w/o Pretensioner and Force Limiter:

i.
1.083,1.646 1.74,1.729 true 1.771,0.521 0.958,0.229 true 0.771,0.125 1.083,0.281 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 0.896,1.542 1.208,1.698 0.313,0.156 10 false *b
*a Bolt A
*b Bolt B


Attach the guide and temporarily install the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the bolt (A) and (B).

NOTICE:

When installing the retractor, make sure the guides of the vehicle only contact the installation areas of the retractor.


Torque:
for Bolt A : 5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)
for Bolt B : 42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)


ii.

Connect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the bolt.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)


iii.

Attach the 2 claws to close the front seat outer belt shoulder anchor cover.


iv.

Connect the floor anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the bolt.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)



b.

w/ Pretensioner and Force Limiter:

i.
0.906,0.302 1.615,0.156 true 2.385,0.656 2.76,0.229 true 2.802,2.927 2.406,2.458 true 1.656,0.031 1.969,0.188 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 2.792,0.063 3.104,0.219 0.313,0.156 10 false *b 2.813,2.948 3.125,3.104 0.313,0.156 10 false *c
*a Locking Button
*b Bolt A
*c Bolt B


Attach the 2 guides and temporarily install the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the bolt (A) and (B).

NOTICE:

When installing the retractor, make sure the guides of the vehicle only contact the installation areas of the retractor.


Torque:
for Bolt A : 5.0 N*m (51 kgf*cm, 44 in.*lbf)
for Bolt B : 42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)


ii.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.


iii.

Connect the pretensioner connector and lock the locking button as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Securely lock the locking button.



iv.

Connect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH with the nut.

Torque:
42 N*m (428 kgf*cm, 31 ft.*lbf)



c.

Check that the front seat outer belt is not twisted.


d.

Check that the ELR locks.

NOTICE:

The check should be performed with the front seat outer belt assembly LH installed.


i.

With the belt assembly installed, check that the belt locks when it is pulled out quickly.

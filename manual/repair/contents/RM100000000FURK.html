2.DISCONNECT FRONT SPEED SENSOR LH (w/ ABS)

a.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the front speed sensor LH.


43401 3.REMOVE STEERING KNUCKLE SPINDLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 8 bolts, front axle hub dust seal, knuckle spindle oil retainer gasket and front disc brake dust cover.


b.

Using a brass bar and hammer, tap the knuckle spindle of the steering knuckle.


c.

Remove the knuckle spindle and knuckle spindle gasket.


4.REMOVE FRONT AXLE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

a.

Position one flat part of the outer shaft upward and remove the front axle shaft assembly.

43405 16.REMOVE FRONT AXLE OUTER SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Secure the front axle inner shaft LH in a vise using aluminum plates.


b.

Place a brass bar against the joint inner race and remove the front axle outer shaft assembly.

HINT:

When removing the outer shaft, the snap ring will be broken.

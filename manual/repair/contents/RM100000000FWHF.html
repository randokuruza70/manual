LIGHTING (EXT)  REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY(for Pickup)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

81560 1.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

Remove the 4 screws and rear combination light assembly LH.


b.

Disconnect the connector.


2.REMOVE SCREW GROMMET

a.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the screw grommet.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the screw grommet on the other side.



81620D 3.REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT COVER LH (except Deckless)

a.

Remove the 4 bolts and rear combination light cover LH.


81563 4.REMOVE REAR NO. 1 COMBINATION LIGHT BRACKET LH (for Deckless)

a.

Remove the bolt and rear No. 1 combination light bracket LH.

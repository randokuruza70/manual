11431A 2.REPLACE CYLINDER BLOCK TIGHT PLUG

NOTICE:

If water leaks from the cylinder block tight plug or the plug is corroded, replace the cylinder block tight plug.


a.

Remove the cylinder block tight plugs.


b.

Apply adhesive around new cylinder block tight plugs.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent



c.

Using SST and a hammer, tap in the cylinder block tight plugs to the standard depth.

SST
09950-60010  (09951-00180, 09951-00250, 09951-00350)
09950-70010  (09951-07150)

Standard depth:

1.7 to 2.7 mm (0.0669 to 0.106 in.)

SEAT BELT  FRONT CENTER SEAT INNER BELT(for Bench Seat Type)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.

·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

73150 1.REMOVE FRONT CENTER SEAT LAP TYPE BELT ASSEMBLY

HINT:

When replacing the front seat lap outer belt sub-assembly, replace the front seat lap inner belt sub-assembly together with it as a set.


a.

Remove the bolt and the 2 seat belt floor anchors from the vehicle body.


b.

Disconnect the floor anchor of the front seat lap outer belt sub-assembly from the floor anchor of the front seat inner belt assembly RH.


c.

Pull out the front seat lap outer belt sub-assembly through the hole of the front seat cushion, and remove the front seat lap outer belt sub-assembly.


d.

w/ Seat Belt Warning System:

Detach the connector.


e.

Remove the bolt, front seat lap inner belt sub-assembly and front seat inner belt assembly LH.


f.

w/ Hook:

Remove the hook.

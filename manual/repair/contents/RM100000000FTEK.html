RM25T0E _58 Vehicle Interior _029469 HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING _0137757 FRONT HEATER UNIT A

HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  FRONT HEATER UNIT  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

55990B 1.REMOVE LOWER DEFROSTER NOZZLE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 2 screws and lower defroster nozzle assembly.


87201B 2.REMOVE NO. 2 AIR DUCT SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Heater)


a.

Remove the 2 bolts.


b.

Detach the 2 claws and remove the No. 2 air duct sub-assembly.


3.REMOVE HEATER RADIATOR ASSEMBLY (w/ Heater)


a.

Remove the 2 screws and 2 brackets.


b.

Remove the heater radiator assembly.


87248E 4.REMOVE HEATER WATER INLET PIPE E (w/ Heater)

a.

Remove the screw and clamp.


b.

Remove the heater water inlet pipe E.


87249E 5.REMOVE HEATER UNIT HEATER WATER OUTLET PIPE (w/ Heater)

a.

Remove the screw and clamp.


b.

Remove the heater unit heater water outlet pipe.


55906A 6.REMOVE DEFROSTER DAMPER CONTROL CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the 2 clamps and remove the defroster damper control cable sub-assembly.



55909A 7.REMOVE AIRMIX DAMPER CONTROL CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Heater)

a.

Detach the 2 clamps and remove the airmix damper control cable sub-assembly.



87201D 8.REMOVE AIR DUCT SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 5 screws and air duct sub-assembly.

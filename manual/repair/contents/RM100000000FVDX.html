56111 1.CLEAN WINDSHIELD GLASS

a.

Using a scraper, remove the damaged stoppers, and adhesive sticking to the windshield glass.


b.

Clean the outer circumference of the glass with non-residue solvent.

NOTICE:
·

Do not touch the glass surface after cleaning it.

·

Be careful not to damage the glass.

·

Even if using new glass, clean the glass with non-residue solvent.

NOTICE:
·

·

Throw away any leftover primer.

·

Do not apply too much primer.



HINT:

If primer is applied to an area that is not specified, wipe off the primer with non-residue solvent before it dries.

Install the glass to the vehicle body.

Using suction cups, position the glass so that the matchmarks are aligned. Press it in gently along the rim.

Apply adhesive to any areas where the amount of adhesive is inadequate.

Do not drive the vehicle for the amount of time written in the table below.


Minimum Time:
Temperature Minimum Time Prior to Driving Vehicle

20°C (68°F) 5 hours
5°C (41°F) 24 hours

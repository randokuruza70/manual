DESCRIPTION

The ECM reduces fuel injector operation speed and applied voltage to the fuel injector to reduce the sound it produces during idling. To do this, the ECM outputs applied voltage switching signals to terminal IJEN of the injector driver from terminal IDLO and IDL2 of the ECM.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory

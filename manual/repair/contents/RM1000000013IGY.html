1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECM  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

89661 1.INSTALL ECM

a.

Clean the ECM seal's surface with non-residue solvent.


b.

Attach a new gasket to the ECM.


c.

Install the ECM with the 4 bolts.

Torque:
8.0 N*m (82 kgf*cm, 71 in.*lbf)


d.

Connect the 2 connectors and push each lock lever down to lock the connectors.

84306 1.INSTALL SPIRAL CABLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:
·

Do not replace the spiral cable sub-assembly with the battery connected and the ignition switch on (IG).

·

Do not rotate the spiral cable with the battery connected and the ignition switch on (IG).



a.

Check that the ignition switch is off.


b.

Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) battery terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) battery terminal to disable the SRS system.



c.

Check that the front wheels are facing straight ahead.

AUDIO / VIDEO  QUARTER TRIM SPEAKER(for 5 Door)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

67918A 2.REMOVE REAR DOOR SCUFF PLATE LH


a.

Detach the 2 clips and remove the rear door scuff plate LH.


62552F 3.REMOVE REAR SEAT SIDE GARNISH LH


a.

Detach the 2 clips.


b.

Remove the rear seat side garnish LH in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration.


64707D 4.REMOVE QUARTER TRIM JACK COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY (for LH Side)


a.

Rotate the knob in the direction of the arrow shown in the illustration and remove the quarter trim jack cover sub-assembly.


62512 5.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD LH


a.

Detach the 10 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.


62511 6.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD RH


a.

Detach the 12 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board RH.


86160B 7.REMOVE REAR SPEAKER ASSEMBLY


a.

Disconnect the connector.


b.

Remove the 3 bolts and rear speaker assembly.

NOTICE:

Do not touch the cone part of the speaker.



64370 8.REMOVE REAR SPEAKER GRILLE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 4 nut springs and rear speaker grille assembly.

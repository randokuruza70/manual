1HZ ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  13  Engine Speed Sensor Circuit Malfunction (NE Circuit)  


DESCRIPTION

The pickup sensor in the engine control system contains a signal plate and pickup coil for the NE signal.

The emission control ECU detects the engine speed and the cam lift position of the injection pump. The emission control ECU uses NE signals for injection timing control. The NE signal is also used for injection volume control.

13 Engine Speed Sensor Circuit Malfunction (NE Circuit) No NE signal to emission control ECU for 0.5 seconds or more at 1000 rpm or more

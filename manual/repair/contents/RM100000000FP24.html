SEAT  FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Bench Seat Type)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the front inner seat track assembly RH or front outer seat track assembly RH due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.


HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for RHD and LHD vehicles.

·

The procedure listed below is for LHD vehicles.



PROCEDURE

a.

Remove the front seat headrest assembly.

72137B 4.REMOVE SEAT TRACK COVER RH (w/ Seat Track Cover)

a.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the foremost position.


b.

Remove the clip.


c.

Detach the 2 guides and remove the seat track cover RH.


72157A 5.REMOVE INNER SEAT TRACK BRACKET COVER RH (w/ Seat Track Cover)

HINT:

Use the same procedure described for the seat track cover RH.


6.REMOVE FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 bolts.


b.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the rearmost position.


c.

Remove the 2 bolts.


d.

Operate the seat track adjusting handle to move the seat to the center position and operate the reclining adjusting handle to move the seatback to the upright position.


e.

Remove the front seat assembly from the vehicle.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  ANTI-LOCK BRAKE SYSTEM  DTC CHECK / CLEAR  


DTC CHECK/CLEAR (When Using GTS)


a.

Check for DTCs.

i.

Turn the ignition switch off.


ii.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


iii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iv.

Turn the GTS on.


v.

Enter the following menus: Chassis / ABS/VSC/TRC / Trouble Codes.

Chassis > ABS/VSC/TRC > Trouble Codes
50001 105

vi.

Check the details of the DTCs.



b.

Clear the DTCs.

NOTICE:

Clearing the DTC also clears the freeze frame data.


i.

Turn the ignition switch off.


ii.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


iii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iv.

Turn the GTS on.


v.

Enter the following menus: Chassis / ABS/VSC/TRC / Trouble Codes.

Chassis > ABS/VSC/TRC > Clear DTCs
50002 105

vi.

Clear the DTCs by following the prompts on the GTS.

Check for DTCs.

i.

Using SST, connect terminals 13 (TC) and 4 (CG) of the DLC3.

SST
09843-18040  

NOTICE:

Do not connect the incorrect terminals as this will cause damage.



ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Read the DTCs output from the ABS warning light in the combination meter.

HINT:
·

When the system is normal, the ABS warning light blinks at 0.25 second intervals (0.25 seconds on and 0.25 seconds off).

·

When only 1 DTC is output, the light outputs the same code after an interval of 4 seconds. Example: when DTC 21 is output, the light blinks twice, turns off for 1.5 seconds, blinks once, turns off for 4 seconds, and then repeats this output pattern.

·

When 2 or more DTCs are output, the light outputs the codes with an interval of 2.5 seconds between each different code, and it repeats the output of all codes after an interval of 4 seconds.

Clear the DTCs.

i.

Using SST, connect terminals 13 (TC) and 4 (CG) of the DLC3.

SST
09843-18040  

NOTICE:

Do not connect the incorrect terminals as this will cause damage.



ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Clear the DTCs stored in the ECU by depressing the brake pedal 8 times or more within 5 seconds.


iv.

Check that the ABS warning light blinks at 0.25 second intervals (0.25 seconds on and 0.25 seconds off).


v.

Remove SST from the terminals of the DLC3.




END OF DTC CHECK/CLEAR


a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

Check that the ABS warning light goes off within approximately 3 seconds after turning the ignition switch to ON.

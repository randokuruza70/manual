WINDOW / GLASS  BACK DOOR GLASS(for LH Side)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

68131 1.CLEAN BACK DOOR GLASS

a.

Clean the outer edge of the back door glass with non-residue solvent.

NOTICE:
·

Do not touch the back door glass surface after cleaning it.

·

Be careful not to damage the glass.

·

Even if using a new back door glass, clean the back door glass with non-residue solvent.




68291E 2.INSTALL BACK DOOR GLASS WEATHERSTRIP LH

a.
2.76,1.219 3.135,1.76 3.135,1.76 2.771,1.052 false 3.052,1.813 3.365,2.01 0.313,0.198 10 false *1
*1 Working Cord


Install the back door glass weatherstrip LH to the back door glass LH.


b.

Apply a working cord along the back door glass weatherstrip LH groove, as shown in the illustration.


68131D 3.INSTALL BACK DOOR GLASS LH

a.
1.49,1.385 1.969,1.385 false 0.729,0.927 1.208,0.927 false 2.01,1.313 2.323,1.469 0.313,0.156 10 false *1 0.552,0.854 0.865,1.01 0.313,0.156 10 false *2
*1 Primer M
*2 Adhesive


Apply primer to the back door glass weatherstrip on the surface with body as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:
·

Do not apply too much primer.

·

Let the primer coating dry for 3 minutes or more.




b.

Install the back door glass LH by pulling the cord from the room side, while pushing back door glass windshield LH from the outside with your open hand.


c.

Tap from the outside with your open hand.


62720A 4.CHECK FOR LEAKS AND REPAIR

a.

Conduct a leak test after the adhesive has completely hardened.


b.

Seal any leaks with auto glass sealer.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P011C Charge Air Temperature/Intake Air Temperature Correlation (Bank 1) After the engine is completely warmed up and then allowed to soak for a long time (10 hours or more), the difference in temperature at the intake air temperature sensor and intake air temperature sensor (turbo) after turbocharger after idling for 3 minutes is 25°C (45°F) or higher.
(2 trip detection logic)
·

Open or short in intake air temperature sensor circuit

·

Open or short in intake air temperature sensor (turbo) circuit

·

Intake air temperature sensor (built into mass air flow meter sub-assembly)

·

Intake air temperature sensor (turbo)

·

ECM

·

Engine off timer (P2610)

HINT:

Read freeze frame data using the GTS. Freeze frame data records the engine condition when malfunctions are detected. When troubleshooting, freeze frame data can help determine if the vehicle was moving or stationary, if the engine was warmed up or not, and other data from the time the malfunction occurred.

It is not necessary to allow the engine to soak if the engine is cold.

c.

e.

Read the value.

OK:

The difference between the displayed value and outside air temperature is below 5°C (9°F).


NOTICE:

If the difference between "Coolant Temp" and the outside air temperature is 5°C (9°F) or higher, the heat from the engine affects the intake air temperature sensor, causing an incorrect value to be displayed. In this case, allow the engine to soak again.

1HZ ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER BLOCK  REPAIR  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Bore all 6 cylinders to fit the O/S piston outside diameter.

·

Replace all the piston rings with ones to match the O/S pistons.



PROCEDURE

1.BORE CYLINDER

a.
2.927,1.542 3.208,1.75 0.281,0.208 10 false *a
*a Distance


Prepare 6 new O/S pistons.

Using a micrometer, measure the piston diameter at right angles to the piston pin center line where the distance from the piston head is as specified.

Distance:

58.60 mm (2.3071 in.)


Standard Piston Diameter:
Item Specified Condition
O/S 0.50 94.450 to 94.480 mm (3.7185 to 3.7197 in.)



b.

Calculate the amount each cylinder is to be rebored as follows.

HINT:
·

Size to be rebored = P + C - H

·

P = Piston diameter

·

C = Piston oil clearance: 0.040 to 0.060 mm (0.00157 to 0.00236 in.)

·

H = Allowance for honing: 0.02 mm (0.000787 in.) or less




c.

Bore and hone the cylinders to the calculated dimensions.

Maximum honing:

0.02 mm (0.000787 in.)


NOTICE:

Excess honing will destroy the finished roundness.

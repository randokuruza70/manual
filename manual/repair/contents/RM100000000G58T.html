HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the LH side and RH side.

·

The procedures listed below are for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

71034A 1.INSTALL REAR NO. 1 SIDE SEAT CUSHION LEG SUB-ASSEMBLY LH (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Install the rear No. 1 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH with the 2 pins and 2 E-rings as shown in the illustration.



b.

w/o Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Install the 2 torsion springs, rear No. 1 side seat cushion leg sub-assembly LH with the 2 pins and 2 new E-rings as shown in the illustration.



79929 2.INSTALL FOLD SEAT LEG STOPPER FRAME (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)


a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Temporarily install the fold seat leg stopper frame and 4 plate washers.


ii.

Set a new spring nut, 12 mm socket wrench and extension bar as shown in the illustration.


iii.

Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap the extension bar to insert the spring nut into the groove and install the fold seat leg stopper frame.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the other side.




72660A 3.INSTALL REAR SEAT CUSHION SUPPORT ASSEMBLY (for Rear Side Seat Assembly LH)

a.

w/ Rear No. 2 Seat Inner Belt Assembly:

i.

Install the 2 bolts, 2 spacers and rear seat cushion support assembly.

Torque:
25.5 N*m (260 kgf*cm, 19 ft.*lbf)

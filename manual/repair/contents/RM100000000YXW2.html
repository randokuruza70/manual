LIGHTING (EXT)  REAR FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY(for Pickup)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE REAR FOG LIGHT SOCKET AND WIRE

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Counterclockwise


Turn the bulb socket counterclockwise until the bulb socket stops and remove the rear fog light socket and wire.


2.REMOVE REAR FOG LIGHT BULB

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Push

Counterclockwise


Push the rear fog light bulb in the direction indicated by the black arrow shown in the illustration, turn the bulb counterclockwise, and then remove the bulb from the bulb socket.


3.REMOVE REAR FOG LIGHT LENS

a.

Remove the rear fog light lens.


4.REMOVE REAR FOG LIGHT GASKET

a.

Remove the rear fog light gasket.

NOTICE:

If the rear fog light gasket has been damaged, replace it with a new one.

14.REMOVE TRANSFER SHIFT LEVER ASSEMBLY

HINT:

Put the transfer shift lever assembly in the L4 position before performing work.


a.

Remove the 3 bolts and disconnect the transfer shift lever assembly.


b.

Remove the 4 bolts, transfer control shift lever retainer bracket and transfer control shift lever retainer gasket.


c.

Remove the nut and washer and then disconnect the high and low shift rod sub-assembly.


d.

Remove the transfer shift lever assembly.

HINT:

Lower the transfer shift lever assembly towards the bottom of the vehicle to remove it.

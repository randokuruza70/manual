47201 2.INSTALL BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER SUB-ASSEMBLY (for LHD)

a.

Install a new O-ring to the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


b.

Install a new brake master cylinder gasket to the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


c.

Install the brake master cylinder sub-assembly to the brake booster assembly with the 2 nuts.

Torque:
12.5 N*m (127 kgf*cm, 9 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Make sure foreign matter does not attach to the master cylinder piston. If foreign matter attaches, clean it off with a cloth. Then apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the entire outer circumference contact surface area of the piston.



d.

Connect the brake fluid level warning switch connector to the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.


e.

Connect the clutch reservoir tube to the brake master cylinder sub-assembly.

SEAT  REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY(for Bench Reclining Fold Down Seat Type)  INSTALLATION  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.


NOTICE:

If the seat is set down in another location after removing it from the vehicle, the seat may tilt and damage the outer rear seat lower front track bracket or rear seatback lock cover due to the difference in shape between the left and right seat tracks.


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL REAR NO. 1 SEAT ASSEMBLY

a.

place the rear No. 1 seat assembly in the cabin.

NOTICE:
·

2 or more people are required when carrying the rear No. 1 seat assembly out of the vehicle.

·

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.




b.

Install the rear No. 1 seat assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:
40 N*m (408 kgf*cm, 30 ft.*lbf)


c.

Attach the rear No. 1 seat assembly to the vehicle body with the 2 locks.


d.

Install the 2 bolts.

Torque:
40 N*m (408 kgf*cm, 30 ft.*lbf)


72138A 2.INSTALL REAR SEAT TRACK COVER LH

a.

Pull the lever in the direction indicated by the arrow in the illustration to release 2 locks.


b.

Attach the 3 claws to install the rear seat track bracket cover LH.


72137D 3.INSTALL REAR SEAT TRACK COVER RH

a.

Attach the 4 claws to install the rear seat track bracket cover RH.


72123B 4.INSTALL FRONT OUTER SEAT TRACK BRACKET COVER

a.

Attach the rear No. 1 seat assembly to the vehicle body with the 2 locks.


b.

Attach the 2 guides and claw to install the front outer seat track bracket cover.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to install the front outer seat track bracket cover on the other side.



71940A 5.INSTALL REAR SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY

a.

Raise the seatback.


b.

Install the 2 rear seat headrest assemblies.

STEERING COLUMN  STEERING SYSTEM  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

1. CHECK STEERING WHEEL FREE PLAY

a.

Stop the vehicle and align the tires facing straight ahead.


b.

Turn the steering wheel assembly gently right and left, and check the steering wheel free play.

Maximum free play:

30 mm (1.18 in.)


If the free play is more than the maximum, check the steering system.

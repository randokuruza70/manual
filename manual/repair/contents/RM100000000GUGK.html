FUEL SYSTEM
Fuel pressure Standard fuel pressure 281 to 287 kPa (2.9 to 2.9 kgf/cm2, 41 to 42 psi)
Remains for 5 minutes after engine has stopped 147 kPa (1.5 kgf/cm2, 21 psi) or higher

FUEL INJECTOR
Fuel injector assembly Standard resistance 11.6 to 12.4 Ω at 20°C (68°F)
Standard injection volume 76 to 91 cc (4.6 to 5.6 cu. in.) per 15 seconds
Difference between each fuel injector 15 cc (0.9 cu. in.) or less
Standard fuel drop 1 drop or less per 17 minutes

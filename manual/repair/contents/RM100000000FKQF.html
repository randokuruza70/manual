DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1405 Open or Short in Front Speed Sensor RH Circuit Either condition is met:
1.

An open in the speed sensor signal circuit continues for 0.5 seconds or more.

2.

With the IG1 terminal voltage at 9.5 V or higher, the sensor power supply voltage decreases for 0.5 seconds or more.


·

Front speed sensor RH

·

Skid control sensor wire RH

·

Speed sensor circuit

·

Brake actuator assembly (Skid control ECU)

1.

An open in the speed sensor signal circuit continues for 0.5 seconds or more.

2.

With the IG1 terminal voltage at 9.5 V or higher, the sensor power supply voltage decreases for 0.5 seconds or more.


·

Front speed sensor LH

·

Skid control sensor wire LH

·

Speed sensor circuit

·

Brake actuator assembly (Skid control ECU)



HINT:
·

DTC C1405 is for the front speed sensor RH.

·

DTC C1406 is for the front speed sensor LH.

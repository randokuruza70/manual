1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

89438B 1.INSPECT POWER STEERING OIL PRESSURE SWITCH

a.

Check the power steering oil pressure switch.

i.

Connect the GTS to the DLC3.


ii.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


iii.

Push the GTS ON.


iv.

Enter the following menus: Power train / Engine and ECT / Data List / Power Steering Signal.

Powertrain > Engine and ECT > Data List
Tester Display
Power Steering Signal
50003 61 186

v.

Start the engine.


vi.

Read the value displayed on the GTS.

Result:
Condition Specified Condition
Power steering operation ON
Power steering not operation OFF


If the result is not as specified, check the wire harness and connector. If the wire harness and connector are normal, replace the power steering oil pressure switch.

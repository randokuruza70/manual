a.

13.SET NO. 1 CYLINDER TO 45° BEFORE TDC

a.

Temporarily install the 2 crankshaft pulley set bolts to the crankshaft.


b.

Turn the crankshaft counterclockwise until the No. 1 cylinder is at a position 45° before TDC.

NOTICE:

Do not turn the crankshaft again until after the timing gear is installed.


HINT:

The No. 1 cylinder is at a position 45° before TDC if the crankshaft counterweight is overlapping the cylinder block sub-assembly hole shown in the illustration.



c.

Remove the 2 crankshaft pulley set bolts.


13511 14.INSTALL NO. 1 AND NO. 2 CAMSHAFTS

a.

Add more than 19 cc (1.2 cu.in.) of engine oil into the cylinder head sub-assembly RH side oil holes.


b.

Apply engine oil to the rollers of the No. 1 valve rocker arm sub-assemblies and the camshaft housing of the cylinder head sub-assembly RH.


c.

Install the No. 1 and No. 2 camshafts.

i.

Apply engine oil to the camshaft journals and lobes.


ii.

Align the timing marks (2 dot marks) on the back side of the No. 1 and No. 2 camshaft timing gears as shown in the illustration.


iii.

Place the No. 1 and No. 2 camshafts into the cylinder head sub-assembly RH.

NOTICE:

Before and after setting the No. 1 and No. 2 camshafts, firmly set the No. 1 valve rocker arm sub-assemblies to the No. 1 valve lash adjuster assemblies.




d.

Install the No. 1 camshaft bearing cap.

i.

Align the No. 1 camshaft bearing cap and the ring pins of the No. 2 camshaft bearing cap.


ii.

Temporarily install the 4 bolts by hand.



e.

Install the No. 3 camshaft bearing caps.

i.

Confirm the marks and numbers on the camshaft bearing caps and place them in their proper position and direction.


ii.

Temporarily install the 8 bolts, which are not installed with the camshaft oil feed pipe sub-assemblies, by hand.



f.

Temporarily install the No. 1 and No. 2 camshaft oil feed pipe sub-assemblies with the 8 bolts and 2 union bolts by hand.

NOTICE:

If even one of the camshaft oil feed pipe sub-assembly bolt holes does not match its camshaft bearing cap bolt hole, replace the camshaft oil feed pipe sub-assembly.


HINT:

The pipe is bent on the intake side, and straight on the exhaust side.



g.

Temporarily install the 2 union bolts by hand.

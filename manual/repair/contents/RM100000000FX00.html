PROCEDURE

47319F 1.INSTALL REAR FLEXIBLE HOSE

a.

Attach a new clip to the flexible hose.


b.

Connect the flexible hose to the 3-way.

Torque:
23 N*m (235 kgf*cm, 17 ft.*lbf)

47319F 2.INSTALL REAR FLEXIBLE HOSE LH

NOTICE:

When replacing both the left and right rear flexible hoses, as the left and right hoses each have a different shape, be sure to check the part number to confirm whether the hose is the left or right one before installing it.


a.

Connect the flexible hose with a new gasket and a new union bolt to the disc brake cylinder.

Torque:
30 N*m (306 kgf*cm, 22 ft.*lbf)


b.

Set the flexible hose into place at the connecting point with the brake tube, and then install a new clip.

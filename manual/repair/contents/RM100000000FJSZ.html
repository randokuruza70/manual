1GR-FE INTAKE / EXHAUST  EXHAUST PIPE(for Model Code #J71, #J76 Series)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:
·

Wear protective gloves when removing the exhaust pipe assembly.

·

The exhaust pipe assembly is extremely hot immediately after the engine has stopped.

·

Confirm that the exhaust pipe assembly has cooled down before removing it.



PROCEDURE

17430 1.REMOVE TAILPIPE ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 4 nuts and disconnect the 2 exhaust pipe support cushions.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 nuts and tailpipe assembly.

0.208,0.083 0.448,0.281 0.24,0.198 10 false *A 0.271,2.083 0.552,2.26 0.281,0.177 10 false *B
*A for Model Code #J71 Series *B for Model Code #J76 Series



c.

Remove the gasket from the center exhaust pipe assembly.


d.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the tailpipe assembly.


e.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the No. 2 exhaust pipe support stay.


f.

Remove the 2 nuts, No. 2 exhaust pipe support stay and exhaust pipe clamp from the tailpipe assembly.


17403 2.REMOVE CENTER EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the exhaust pipe support cushion.


b.

Remove the 4 bolts, 4 compression springs and center exhaust pipe assembly.


c.

Remove the 2 gaskets from the front exhaust pipe assembly and No. 2 front exhaust pipe assembly.


d.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the center exhaust pipe assembly.


17450 3.REMOVE NO. 2 FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the exhaust pipe support cushion.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 compression springs and No. 2 front exhaust pipe assembly.


c.

Remove the gasket from the exhaust manifold sub-assembly LH.


d.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the No. 2 front exhaust pipe assembly.


17410 4.REMOVE FRONT EXHAUST PIPE ASSEMBLY


a.

Remove the 2 nuts and disconnect the exhaust pipe support cushion.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts, 2 compression springs and front exhaust pipe assembly.


c.

Remove the gasket from the exhaust manifold sub-assembly RH.


d.

Remove the 2 nuts and exhaust pipe support cushion from the front exhaust pipe assembly.


e.

Remove the 2 nuts, 2 bolts and exhaust pipe damper from the front exhaust pipe assembly.

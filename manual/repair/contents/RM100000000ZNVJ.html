DESCRIPTION

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C1422 Master Cylinder Pressure Sensor Zero Point High Malfunction When vehicle speed is 3 km/h (2 mph) or more and stop light switch assembly is off, master cylinder pressure continuously exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi) for 5 seconds or more.

Harness or connector

·

·

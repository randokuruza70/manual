RM25T0E _58 Vehicle Interior _029924 DOOR LOCK _0138408 KEY REMINDER WARNING SYSTEM D

DOOR LOCK  KEY REMINDER WARNING SYSTEM  OPERATION CHECK  


CHECK KEY REMINDER WARNING SYSTEM


a.

Check that the key reminder warning buzzer sounds.

i.

With the driver side door closed, insert the key into the ignition key cylinder and leave the ignition switch off, or turn the ignition switch to ACC.


ii.

Check that the key reminder warning buzzer sounds intermittently if the driver side door is opened.



b.

Check that the key reminder warning buzzer stops.

i.

Check that the buzzer stops sounding when any of the following operations is performed while the key reminder warning buzzer is sounding:

·

Closing the driver side door (front courtesy light switch LH turns off).

·

Turning the ignition switch to ON.

·

Removing the key from the ignition key cylinder.

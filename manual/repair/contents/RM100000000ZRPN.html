DESCRIPTION

The curtain shield squib RH circuit consists of the airbag sensor assembly and curtain shield airbag assembly RH.

The circuit instructs the SRS to deploy when deployment conditions are met.

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction is detected in the curtain shield squib RH circuit.

1HZ ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  TC and CG Terminal Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

Terminal TC is located in the DLC3.

The DLC3 is located in the vehicle interior. When terminal TC is connected with terminal CG, DTCs can be read from the glow indicator light in the combination meter assembly.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Even though terminal TC is not connected with terminal CG, the glow indicator light may blink.

·

For the above phenomenon, the likely cause is an open or short in the wire harness, or malfunction inside the emission control ECU.

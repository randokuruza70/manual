1HZ STARTING  STARTER(for 3.0 kW Type)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

28100 1.INSTALL STARTER ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the starter assembly and ground cable with the bolt and nut.

Torque:
68 N*m (693 kgf*cm, 50 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the starter connector.


c.

Connect the starter wire with the nut.

Torque:
21 N*m (214 kgf*cm, 15 ft.*lbf)


d.

Close the terminal cap.


2.CONNECT CABLE TO NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  VACUUM PUMP(for 1HZ)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE VACUUM HOSE UNION

a.

Remove the union bolt, union and 2 gaskets.


2.REMOVE CHECK VALVE SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the check valve and gasket.


3.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP END COVER

a.

Remove the 3 bolts.


b.

Using a 5 mm pin punch and hammer, tap out the 2 straight pins.


c.

Remove the end cover and O-ring.


4.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP ROTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the vacuum pump rotor sub-assembly.


5.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP BLADE

a.

Remove the 5 vacuum pump blades from the vacuum pump rotor.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to drop the blades.



6.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP GEAR

a.

Remove the lock nut.


b.

Remove the vacuum pump gear and woodruff key.


7.REMOVE HOLE SNAP RING

a.

Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring.


8.REMOVE VACUUM PUMP SHAFT

a.

Using a plastic-faced hammer, tap out the vacuum pump shaft.


9.REMOVE BEARING

a.

Using a 17 mm socket wrench and press, press out the bearing from the vacuum pump housing.

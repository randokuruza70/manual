AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  REAR DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY(w/ Differential Lock)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.LOCK REAR DIFFERENTIAL

a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

Shift the transfer shift lever to the H4 or L4 position.


c.

Turn the differential lock control switch to the RR position and lock the rear differential.

HINT:

Rotate the rear wheels to check they are in the differential lock condition.



d.

Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) terminal of the battery.

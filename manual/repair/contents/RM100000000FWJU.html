BRAKE SYSTEM (OTHER)  BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER(w/o ABS)  REASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Make sure foreign matter does not attach to the master cylinder's piston. If foreign matter attaches, clean it off with a cloth. Then apply lithium soap base glycol grease to the entire outer circumference contact surface area of the piston.


PROCEDURE

1.INSTALL NO. 2 MASTER CYLINDER PISTON SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.
0.365,0.885 0.458,1 false

Lithium soap base glycol grease


Apply a light coat of lithium soap base glycol grease to the rubber parts indicated by arrows.


b.

Insert the spring and piston.

NOTICE:

Insert the spring and piston straight in. Do not insert them at an angle.



2.INSTALL NO. 1 MASTER CYLINDER PISTON SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Apply a light coat of lithium soap base glycol grease to the rubber parts indicated by arrows.


b.

Insert the spring and piston.

NOTICE:

Insert the spring and piston straight in. Do not insert them at an angle.



3.INSTALL HOLE SNAP RING

a.

Push in the piston with a screwdriver and install the snap ring with snap ring pliers.


47210T 4.INSTALL PISTON STOPPER BOLT

a.

Push the pistons in all the way and install the piston stopper bolt over a new gasket.

Torque:
10 N*m (102 kgf*cm, 7 ft.*lbf)


47255 5.INSTALL MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR GROMMET

a.
0,0 0,0 false

Lithium soap base glycol grease


Apply a light coat of lithium soap base glycol grease to 2 new master cylinder reservoir grommets, and then install them to the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


47202 6.INSTALL BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the brake master cylinder reservoir to the brake master cylinder body.


b.

Install the reservoir with the screw.

Torque:
1.8 N*m (18 kgf*cm, 16 in.*lbf)


47299 7.INSTALL BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR STRAINER

a.

Install the brake master cylinder reservoir strainer to the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.


47230 8.INSTALL BRAKE MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR FILLER CAP ASSEMBLY

a.

Install the brake master cylinder reservoir filler cap assembly to the brake master cylinder reservoir assembly.

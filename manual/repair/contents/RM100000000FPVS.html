POWER ASSIST SYSTEMS  VANE PUMP(except 1GR-FE)  DISASSEMBLY  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

When using a vise, place aluminum plates between the part and vise.

·

When using a vise, do not overtighten it.



PROCEDURE

44337 1.REMOVE POWER STEERING SUCTION PORT UNION

a.

Remove the bolt and power steering suction port union from the vane pump assembly.


b.

Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the power steering suction port union.


2.REMOVE FLOW CONTROL VALVE

a.

Remove the pressure port union.


b.

Remove the O-ring from the pressure port union.


c.

Remove the flow control valve and flow control valve compression spring.


44316 3.REMOVE VANE PUMP GEAR

a.
0.656,0.385 0.917,0.385 0.917,0.385 1.135,0.542 false 0.406,0.313 0.771,0.479 0.365,0.167 10 false SST

Using soft jaws on a vise, clamp the vane pump assembly.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vane pump assembly.



b.

Using SST, remove the vane pump gear.

SST
09950-30012  (09951-03010, 09953-03010, 09954-03010, 09955-03011)


4.REMOVE VANE PUMP REAR HOUSING

a.

Remove the 4 bolts and vane pump rear housing from the vane pump front housing.


b.

*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the vane pump rear housing.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



5.REMOVE VANE PUMP ROTOR, 10 VANE PUMP PLATES AND VANE PUMP CAM RING

a.

Remove the 10 vane pump plates.

NOTICE:

Take care not to drop the vane pump plates.



b.

Remove the vane pump rotor.


c.

Remove the vane pump cam ring from the vane pump front housing.


6.REMOVE VANE PUMP FRONT SIDE PLATE

a.

Remove the vane pump front side plate from the vane pump front housing.


b.

*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, remove the O-ring from the vane pump front side plate.

HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



c.

Remove the O-ring from the vane pump front housing.


7.REMOVE VANE PUMP SHAFT WITH VANE PUMP SHAFT BEARING

a.

*a Protective Tape


Using a screwdriver, remove the vane pump housing snap ring.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vane pump front housing.


HINT:

Tape the screwdriver tip before use.



b.
2.458,0.177 2.135,0.177 2.135,0.177 1.729,0.854 true 2.521,0.115 3.177,0.458 0.656,0.344 10 false *a
*a Protective Tape


Tape the serrated part of the vane pump shaft.


c.

Using a press, press out the vane pump shaft with bearing.


8.REMOVE VANE PUMP SHAFT BEARING

a.

Using 2 screwdrivers, remove the vane pump shaft snap ring from the vane pump shaft.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vane pump shaft.



b.

Using a press, press out the vane pump shaft bearing from the vane pump shaft.

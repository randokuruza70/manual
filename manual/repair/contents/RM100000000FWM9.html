DOOR LOCK  WIRELESS DOOR LOCK CONTROL SYSTEM  DIAGNOSIS SYSTEM  


SELF-DIAGNOSTIC MODE


a.

The vehicle should be in the following conditions.

i.

Driver side door is locked.


ii.

Ignition switch is off.



b.

Switch to self-diagnostic mode.

i.

Unlock the driver side door using the power window regulator master switch assembly*1 or door control switch assembly*2. (End in unlocked)


ii.

Within 5 seconds after the above operation, lock and unlock the driver side door using the power window regulator master switch assembly*1 or door control switch assembly*2. (End in unlocked)


iii.

Within 30 seconds after the above operation, perform the following 9 times: Turn the ignition switch to ON then off. (End in off)

HINT:

Turning the ignition switch to ON after the procedure above has been completed will end self-diagnostic mode.


NOTICE:

If the system fails to enter self-diagnostic mode, the system will return to normal mode.



iv.

Check the diagnostic outputs when the door control transmitter switch is held. The diagnostic outputs can be checked by the flashes of the hazard warning lights.

HINT:

While the self-diagnostic mode is performed, the wireless door lock control functions cannot be operated.

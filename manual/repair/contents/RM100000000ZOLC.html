SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS  AIRBAG SYSTEM  SYSTEM DESCRIPTION  


GENERAL


a.

Deceleration sensors used for the airbag system are installed on various parts of the vehicle and calculate the deceleration rate of each part during a collision.


b.

Depending on the situation, the airbag sensor assembly sends a deployment signal to each airbag based on the information from each sensor.

1HZ ENGINE MECHANICAL  CYLINDER HEAD GASKET  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

1.CHECK PISTON PROTRUSION AND SELECT CYLINDER HEAD GASKET

a.

Check piston protrusions each cylinder.

i.

Clean the cylinder block solvent.


ii.

Set the piston of the cylinder to be measured to slightly before TDC.


iii.

Place a dial indicator on the cylinder block, and set the dial indicator at 0 mm (0 in.).

HINT:
·

Use a dial indicator measuring tip as shown in the illustration.

·

Make sure that the measuring tip is square to the cylinder block gasket surface and piston head when taking the measurements.




iv.

Find where the piston head protrudes most by slowly turning the crankshaft clockwise and counterclockwise.

2.SET NO. 1 CYLINDER TO BDC


a.

Turn the crankshaft pulley, and align the BDC mark of the timing gear cover with the No. 2 camshaft timing pulley flange groove.


11115 3.INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD GASKET

a.

Place a new cylinder head gasket in position on the cylinder block.

NOTICE:
·

Be careful of the installation direction.

·

Gently place the cylinder head in order not to damage the gasket with the bottom part of the head.




11101 4.INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Place the cylinder head sub-assembly in position on the cylinder head gasket.

HINT:
·

The cylinder head set bolts are tightened in 3 progressive steps.

·

If any cylinder head set bolts is broken or deformed, replace it.




b.

Apply a light coat of engine oil on the threads and under the heads of the cylinder head set bolts.

LIGHTING (INT)  REAR DOOR COURTESY SWITCH(for Double Cab)  REMOVAL  


CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

62512 1.REMOVE QUARTER INSIDE TRIM BOARD LH

a.

Remove the 3 clips.


b.

Detach the 2 clips and remove the quarter inside trim board LH.


84234 2.REMOVE REAR DOOR COURTESY LIGHT SWITCH ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the bolt.


b.

Disconnect the connector and remove the rear door courtesy light switch assembly.

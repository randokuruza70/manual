RM25T0E _51 Engine / Hybrid System _029893 1HZ FUEL _0136500 FUEL TANK SELECT VALVE A

1HZ FUEL  FUEL TANK SELECT VALVE  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

77406 1.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID VALVE WITH BRACKET

a.

Slide the 3 clamps and disconnect the 3 fuel hoses from the fuel tank solenoid return valve assembly.


b.

Slide the 3 clamps and disconnect the 3 fuel hoses from the fuel tank solenoid main valve assembly.


c.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


d.

Disconnect the fuel tank solenoid main valve connector and fuel tank solenoid return valve connector.


e.

Remove the 2 bolts and fuel tank solenoid valve with bracket.


77710B 2.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID MAIN VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


b.

Remove the bolt and fuel tank solenoid main valve assembly from the fuel solenoid valve main bracket.


77720E 3.REMOVE FUEL TANK SOLENOID RETURN VALVE ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


b.

Remove the bolt and fuel tank solenoid return valve assembly from the fuel solenoid valve main bracket.

33364 1.INSTALL NO. 2 TRANSMISSION HUB SLEEVE

a.

Install the 3 No. 2 synchromesh shifting key springs and 3 No. 2 synchromesh shifting keys to the No. 2 transmission clutch hub.

NOTICE:
0,0 0,0 false

Front Side


Be careful of the direction of the No. 2 transmission hub sleeve and that of the No. 2 transmission clutch hub.



b.

33333A 2.INSTALL 3RD GEAR NEEDLE ROLLER BEARING

a.

Apply gear oil to the 3rd gear needle roller bearing, and install it to the output shaft.

33368 4.INSTALL NO. 2 SYNCHRONIZER RING (FOR 3RD GEAR) (FOR 3RD GEAR)

a.

a.

HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING  CONDENSER(for 1HZ RHD)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

88474 1.REMOVE COOLER DRYER

a.

Using a 14 mm socket hexagon wrench, remove the cap from the modulator.


b.

Remove the O-ring from the cap.


c.

Using pliers, remove the cooler dryer.


88717A 2.REMOVE COOLER REFRIGERANT SUCTION PIPE A


a.

Remove the cooler refrigerant suction pipe A from the cooler condenser assembly.

NOTICE:

Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.



88716A 3.REMOVE COOLER REFRIGERANT LIQUID PIPE A


a.

Remove the bolt and cooler refrigerant liquid pipe A from the cooler condenser assembly.

NOTICE:
·

When removing the bolt, do not allow any tools to contact the pipe.

·

When removing the bolt, hold a part of the pipe near the connector.




b.

Remove the O-ring from the cooler refrigerant liquid pipe A.

NOTICE:

Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent moisture and foreign matter from entering them.



88467B 4.REMOVE UPPER COOLER BRACKET LH


a.

Remove the bolt and upper cooler bracket LH.


88467B 5.REMOVE UPPER COOLER BRACKET RH


a.

Remove the bolt and upper cooler bracket RH.


88467B 6.REMOVE LOWER COOLER BRACKET LH


a.

Remove the bolt and lower cooler bracket LH.


88467B 7.REMOVE LOWER COOLER BRACKET RH


a.

Remove the bolt and lower cooler bracket RH.


88467 8.REMOVE PIPING BRACKET LH


a.

Remove the 2 bolts and piping bracket LH.


88467 9.REMOVE PIPING BRACKET RH


a.

Remove the 2 bolts and piping bracket RH.

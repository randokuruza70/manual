BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  BRAKE ACTUATOR(w/o VSC)  DISASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

44510 1.REMOVE BRAKE ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 3 bolts and brake actuator assembly from the brake actuator bracket.


2.REMOVE BRAKE ACTUATOR CASE COLLAR

a.

Remove the 3 brake actuator case collars from the 3 brake actuator bracket cushions.


44547B 3.REMOVE BRAKE ACTUATOR BRACKET CUSHION

a.

Remove the 3 brake actuator bracket cushions from the brake actuator bracket.

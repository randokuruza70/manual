HINT:
·

Use the same procedure for the RH and LH sides.

·

The procedure listed below is for the LH side.



PROCEDURE

81144 1.REMOVE NO. 1 HEADLIGHT COVER


a.

Remove the No. 1 headlight cover from the headlight unit assembly LH.


81110B 2.REMOVE NO. 1 HEADLIGHT BULB

a.

Push and pull up the set spring to release it.


b.

Remove the No. 1 headlight bulb from the headlight unit assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Do not touch the glass part of the No. 1 headlight bulb.

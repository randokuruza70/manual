81272 2.INSTALL LICENSE PLATE LIGHT LENS GASKET

HINT:

Use the same procedure to install the license plate light lens gasket on the other side.


a.

for Back Door:

81271 3.INSTALL LICENSE PLATE LIGHT LENS

a.

for Back Door:

b.

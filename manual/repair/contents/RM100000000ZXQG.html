AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  FRONT DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY(w/ Differential Lock)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1. LOCK FRONT DIFFERENTIAL

a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

Shift the transfer shift lever to the H4 or L4 position.


c.

Turn the differential lock control switch to the FR.RR position, and lock the front differential.

HINT:

Rotate the wheels to check they are in the differential lock condition.



d.

Disconnect the cable from the negative (-) terminal of the battery.

4.REMOVE FRONT BRAKE TUBE (w/o ABS)

a.

for 1HZ:


i.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.


ii.

Remove the clip.


iii.

Remove the 4 bolts and 3-way joint with the brake hose and tube.



b.

for 1GR-FE:


i.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.


ii.

Remove the clip.


iii.

Remove the 4 bolts and 3-way joint with the brake hose and tube.



5.REMOVE FRONT BRAKE TUBE (w/ ABS,VSC)

a.

for 1HZ, 1VD-FTV:


i.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.


ii.

Remove the 2 clips.


iii.

Remove the 5 bolts and hose bracket joint with the brake hose and tube.



b.

for 1GR-FE:


i.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the front flexible hose with a wrench.


ii.

Remove the 3 bolts and brake tube.

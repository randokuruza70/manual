DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0101 Mass Air Flow Circuit Range / Performance Problem After the engine is completely warmed up, while the accelerator pedal is released for 7 seconds or more and the vehicle is decelerating, the intake air volume is higher or lower than the threshold* (EGR valve fully closed and throttle valve is fully open).
(2 trip detection logic)
*: There is a threshold for a low air flow rate malfunction and high air flow rate malfunction. The threshold is different depending on the values of "Engine Speed", "MAP" and "Intake Air Temp (Turbo)".
·

Mass air flow meter sub-assembly

·

Intake air temperature sensor (turbo)

·

Manifold absolute pressure sensor

·

Electric EGR control valve assembly

·

ECM

·

Open or short in mass air flow meter sub-assembly circuit


Comes on DTC stored

HINT:
·

Low air flow rate malfunction: For example when vehicle deceleration if "MAF" is 50 gm/sec or less, low air flow malfunction is confirmed (at "Engine Speed" is 1600 rpm, "MAP" is 110 kPa and "Intake Air Temp (Turbo)" is 35째C).

·

High air flow rate malfunction: For example when vehicle deceleration if "MAF" is 94 gm/sec or more, high air flow malfunction is confirmed (at "Engine Speed" is 1600 rpm, "MAP" is 110 kPa and "Intake Air Temp (Turbo)" is 35째C).

HINT:

If DTC P0101 is stored, either of the following symptoms may appear:

1.

As the ECU mistakenly determines that there is less air than the actual intake air amount, EGR is decreased to match the target EGR.

·

Combustion noise worsens


2.

As the ECU mistakenly determines that there is more air than the actual intake air amount, EGR is increased to match the target EGR:

·

Misfire

·

White smoke

·

Black smoke

1VD-FTV ENGINE MECHANICAL  DRIVE BELT  ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION  


PROCEDURE

16361A 1.INSPECT FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT

a.
1.688,2.833 2,2.99 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 2.365,3.979 2.677,4.135 0.313,0.156 10 false *b 0.896,3.969 1.208,4.125 0.313,0.156 10 false *b
*a CORRECT
*b INCORRECT


Check the fan and generator V belt for wear, cracks or other signs of damage.

If any of the following defects is found, replace the fan and generator V belt.

·

The fan and generator V belt is cracked.

·

The fan and generator V belt is worn out to the extent that the cords are exposed.

·

The fan and generator V belt has chunks missing from the ribs.



b.

Check by hand that the fan and generator V belt fits properly in the ribbed grooves and has not slipped out of the grooves on the bottom of the crankshaft pulley. Then run the engine for 5 minutes or more and recheck the tension.


16620D 2.INSPECT V-RIBBED BELT TENSIONER ASSEMBLY

a.

Turn the V-ribbed belt tensioner assembly clockwise and counterclockwise. Check that the V-ribbed belt tensioner assembly moves smoothly.

If a malfunction exists, replace the V-ribbed belt tensioner assembly.

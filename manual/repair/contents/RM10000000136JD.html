1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM(w/o EGR System)  P0724  Brake Switch "B" Circuit High  


DESCRIPTION

This DTC indicates that the stop light switch remains on. When the stop light switch remains on during GO and STOP driving, the ECM interprets this as a fault in the stop light switch. Then the MIL illuminates and the ECM stores the DTC.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area MIL Memory
P0724 Brake Switch "B" Circuit High Stop light switch remains on even when vehicle is driven in GO (30 km/h (19 mph) or more) and STOP (less than 3 km/h (1.8 mph)) pattern 5 times.

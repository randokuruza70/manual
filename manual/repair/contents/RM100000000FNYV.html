CLUTCH  CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER(for RHD)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

31440P 1.REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR FILLER CAP ASSEMBLY

31426 2.REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER RESERVOIR FLOAT

3.DRAIN CLUTCH FLUID

31410 4.REMOVE CLUTCH MASTER CYLINDER ASSEMBLY

a.

Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the clutch master cylinder to flexible hose tube.

HINT:

Use a container to catch the fluid.



b.

Remove the clip and clutch master cylinder push rod clevis pin.


c.

Remove the 2 nuts and clutch master cylinder assembly.


d.

Remove the gasket.

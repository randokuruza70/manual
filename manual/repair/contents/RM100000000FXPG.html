SLIDING ROOF / CONVERTIBLE  SOFT TOP(for Model Code #J79 Series)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

65910A 1.REMOVE TARPAULIN ASSEMBLY

a.

Detach the 26 ropes and remove the tarpaulin assembly.



65837 2.REMOVE TOP BOW PLATE

a.

Remove the 4 bolts, 4 nuts and top bow plate.


65811B 3.REMOVE NO. 1 TOP BOW

a.

Loosen the 6 bolts.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for all bolts.



b.

Remove the 3 No. 1 top bows.


65871 4.REMOVE TOP BOW HOLDER

a.

Remove the bolt, nut and top bow holder.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for all top bow holders.



65802A 5.REMOVE NO. 2 TOP BOW SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 4 bolts and No. 2 top bow sub-assembly.

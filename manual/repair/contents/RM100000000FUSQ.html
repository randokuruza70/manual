48610A 2.TEMPORARILY INSTALL FRONT LEADING ARM ASSEMBLY

a.

Temporarily install the front leading arm assembly to the front axle housing assembly with 2 new bolts, 2 washers and 2 nuts.


b.

Temporarily install the front leading arm assembly to the frame with a new bolt, washers and nut.

1HZ ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  Timing Control Valve Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

The Timing Control Valve (TCV) inside the injection pump is turned ON/OFF by the emission control ECU.

When the ignition switch is ON, battery voltage is continuously applied to the +B terminal of the TCV. The circuit of the TCV2 terminal is open or closed on the emission control ECU side. The TCV will switch the fuel injection timing to the advance or retarded side. (When the TCV is connected, it is on the advance side.)

HINT:

If the TCV has a failure and is stuck either on the advance side or the retarded side, the following conditions will occur.

·

Retarded side: Black smoke occurs. When starting, misfiring occurs.

·

Advance side: Noise (diesel knock) will become greater.

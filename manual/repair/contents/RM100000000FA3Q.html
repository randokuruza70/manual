78104 8.REMOVE NO. 2 ACCELERATOR CONNECTING ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

78103 9.REMOVE NO. 1 ACCELERATOR CONNECTING ROD SUB-ASSEMBLY

a.

w/ EGR System:

Disconnect the connector and detach the wire harness clamp from No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts, nut and the No. 1 accelerator connecting rod sub-assembly from the intake manifold.


25860A 10.REMOVE NO. 1 VACUUM SWITCHING VALVE

a.

Disconnect the 2 vacuum hoses from the No. 1 vacuum switching valve and connector.


b.

Remove the bolt and No. 1 vacuum switching valve from the intake manifold.


11.DISCONNECT HEATER HOSE BRACKET

a.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the heater hose bracket.


b.

Remove the bolt and disconnect the heater hose valve bracket.


17111 12.REMOVE INTAKE MANIFOLD

a.

w/ EGR System:

Remove the 2 bolts and 2 wire harness clamp brackets from the intake manifold.


b.

Remove the 12 nuts and 12 seal washers from the intake manifold.


c.

Remove the intake manifold, intake manifold gasket and 5 No. 2 intake manifold gaskets from the cylinder head sub-assembly.

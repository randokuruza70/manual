SEAT BELT WARNING SYSTEM DESCRIPTION


a.

Front seat belt warning light operation for driver side seat belt:

The front seat belt warning light on the combination meter assembly blinks or turns off in accordance with the driver side seat belt state or vehicle speed.


b.

Seat belt warning buzzer (except 1VD-FTV):

The seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds 5 times, when the ignition switch is turned to ON while the driver side seat belt is not fastened.

The seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 9.6 seconds, and then the buzzer sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 20 seconds when the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more while the driver side seat belt is unfastened.


c.

Seat belt warning buzzer (for 1VD-FTV):

The seat belt warning buzzer in the combination meter assembly sounds at an interval of 1.2 seconds for 30 seconds, and then the buzzer sounds at an interval of 0.4 seconds for 90 seconds when the vehicle is driven at approximately 20 km/h (12 mph) or more while the driver side seat belt is unfastened.

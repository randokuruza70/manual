1GR-FE FUEL  FUEL PUMP  REASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

23217D 1.INSTALL FUEL PUMP FILTER

a.

Install the fuel pump filter with a new clip.


23221 2.INSTALL FUEL PUMP

a.

Connect the fuel hose to the fuel suction tube sub-assembly and slide the clamp to secure the hose.


b.

Install the rubber cushion to the fuel pump.


c.

Install the fuel pump by pushing the lower side of the fuel pump.


d.

Connect the lead wire connector to the fuel pump.

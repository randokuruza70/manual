69350 9.REMOVE BACK DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY

a.

w/ Power Door Lock:

i.

Detach the clamp.


ii.

Disconnect the 2 connectors.


iii.

Detach the 2 snap and disconnect the 2 control links as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

The control link can be easily removed by pushing the end of the control link with the tip side of a screwdriver.



iv.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and back door lock assembly.



b.

w/o Power Door Lock:

i.

Detach the 2 snap and disconnect the 2 control links as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

The control link can be easily removed by pushing the end of the control link with the tip side of a screwdriver.



ii.

Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 3 screws and back door lock assembly.



84235 10.REMOVE BACK DOOR COURTESY LIGHT SWITCH ASSEMBLY

a.

Remove the 2 screws and back door courtesy light switch assembly.

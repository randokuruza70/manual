AXLE AND DIFFERENTIAL  REAR DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY(w/ Differential Lock)  INSTALLATION  


PROCEDURE

41110 1.INSTALL REAR DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY

a.

Clean the contact surfaces of the rear differential carrier sub-assembly and axle housing.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the installation surface.



b.

Install the rear differential carrier sub-assembly, and a new gasket and the 10 washers with the 10 nuts.

Torque:
72 N*m (734 kgf*cm, 53 ft.*lbf)

NOTICE:

Be careful to install the gasket without tearing it.



2.CONNECT WIRE HARNESS

a.

Connect the wire harness with the nut.

Torque:
36 N*m (367 kgf*cm, 27 ft.*lbf)


b.

Connect the connector clamp, 2 connectors and breather hose.


41467 3.INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL LOCK CYLINDER PROTECTOR

a.
1.323,0.063 1.479,0.26 0.156,0.198 10 false A 0.25,1.104 0.406,1.302 0.156,0.198 10 false B

Install the differential lock cylinder protector with the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

Torque:
Bolt A : 19.5 N*m (199 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)
Bolt B : 17 N*m (173 kgf*cm, 13 ft.*lbf)
Nut : 35 N*m (357 kgf*cm, 26 ft.*lbf)

LIGHTING (EXT)  LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY(for Pickup)  REMOVAL  


PROCEDURE

1.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE BRACKET WITH LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY (except Step Type)

a.

Disconnect the 2 connectors and detach the wire harness clamp.


b.

Remove the 2 bolts and license plate bracket with license plate light assembly.


81270 2.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY (except Step Type)

a.

Cut off the wire harness clamp.


b.

Remove the 2 nuts and license plate light assembly from the license plate bracket.

HINT:

Use the same procedure to remove the license plate light assembly on the other side.



81270 3.REMOVE LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY (for Step Type)

a.
2,0.344 2.313,0.5 0.313,0.156 10 true *A 1.344,1.531 1.656,1.688 0.313,0.156 10 true *B
*A for LH Side
*B for RH Side


Disconnect the connector.


b.

Cut off the cable tie.


c.

for LH Side:

i.

Detach the wire harness clamp.


ii.

Remove the 2 screws.


iii.

Pull the license plate light assembly to remove it.



d.

for RH Side:

i.

Detach the 2 wire harness clamps.


ii.

Remove the 2 screws.


iii.

Pull the license plate light assembly to remove it.

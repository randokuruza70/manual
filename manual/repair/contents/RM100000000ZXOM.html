16.INSPECT DIFFERENTIAL DRIVE PINION PRELOAD

a.

Using a torque wrench, measure the preload.

Preload (Starting Torque):
Item Specified Condition
New bearing 1.01 to 1.60 N*m (11 to 16 kgf*cm, 9 to 14 in.*lbf)
Reused bearing 0.52 to 0.81 N*m (6 to 8 kgf*cm, 5 to 7 in.*lbf)


If the result is not as specified, adjust the preload.

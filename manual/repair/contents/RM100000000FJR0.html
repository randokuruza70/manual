32.REMOVE FUEL INJECTOR RH

a.

Remove the union bolt, 4 hollow screws, 5 gaskets and nozzle leakage pipe.

NOTICE:
·

When removing the nozzle leakage pipe, place a cushion under the nozzle leakage pipe.

·

Be careful not to deform or scratch the union seal surface.

·

After removing the fuel pipe, put it in a plastic bag to prevent foreign matter from contaminating its injector inlet.




b.

Remove the 4 bolts, 4 washers, 4 nozzle holder clamps and 4 fuel injector RH.

HINT:

Arrange the fuel injector RH, nozzle holder clamps, washers and bolts in the correct order.



c.

Remove the O-ring from each fuel injector RH.


d.

Remove the 4 injection nozzle seats from the cylinder head sub-assembly RH.


33.REMOVE FUEL INJECTOR LH

a.

Remove the union bolt, 4 hollow screws, 5 gaskets and No. 2 nozzle leakage pipe.

NOTICE:
·

When removing the No. 2 nozzle leakage pipe, place a cushion under the No. 2 nozzle leakage pipe.

·

Be careful not to deform or scratch the union seal surface.

·

After removing the fuel pipe, put it in a plastic bag to prevent foreign matter from contaminating its injector inlet.




b.

Remove the 4 bolts, 4 washers, 4 nozzle holder clamps and 4 fuel injector LH.

HINT:

Arrange the fuel injector LH, nozzle holder clamps, washers and bolts in the correct order.



c.

Remove the O-ring from each fuel injector LH.


d.

Remove the 4 injection nozzle seats from the cylinder head sub-assembly LH.

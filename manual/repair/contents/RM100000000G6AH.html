WINDOW / GLASS  WINDOW DEFOGGER SYSTEM  OPERATION CHECK  


CHECK WINDOW DEFOGGER OPERATION


a.

Turn the ignition switch to ON.


b.

Check that pressing the rear window defogger switch assembly illuminates the ON/OFF indicator and warms the rear window surface.


c.

Check that after approximately 15 minutes, the ON/OFF indicator light turns off and the window defogger deactivates.

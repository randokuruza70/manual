1GR-FE LUBRICATION  OIL PUMP  REASSEMBLY  


PROCEDURE

15131 1.INSTALL OIL PUMP RELIEF VALVE

a.

Coat the oil pump relief valve with engine oil and insert the oil pump relief valve and relief valve spring into the valve hole.


b.

Install the relief valve plug.

Torque:
49 N*m (500 kgf*cm, 36 ft.*lbf)


15115 2.INSTALL OIL PUMP COVER

a.
1.635,0.688 0.917,0.167 0.917,0.167 0.75,0.167 true 1.677,0.146 2.083,0.25 2.083,0.25 2.25,0.25 true 2.271,0.177 2.583,0.333 0.313,0.156 10 false *a 0.594,0.083 0.906,0.24 0.313,0.156 10 false *a
*a Matchmark


Apply engine oil to the drive rotor and driven rotor.


b.

Place the drive rotor and driven rotor into the timing chain cover sub-assembly with the matchmarks facing upward.


c.

Install the oil pump cover with the 7 bolts.

Torque:
9.1 N*m (93 kgf*cm, 81 in.*lbf)


15413 3.INSTALL OIL PUMP OUTLET PIPE

a.

Install a new O-ring to the oil pump cover.


b.

Install a new O-ring to the oil pump outlet pipe.


c.

Install the oil pump outlet pipe with the 3 bolts.

Torque:
9.1 N*m (93 kgf*cm, 81 in.*lbf)

73220 8.REMOVE FRONT SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY LH

a.

w/o Pretensioner and Force Limiter:

i.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the floor anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.


ii.

Using moulding remover D, detach the 2 claws and open the front seat outer belt shoulder anchor cover.


iii.

Loosen the bolt and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.


iv.

Remove the 2 bolts.


v.

Detach the guide and remove the front seat outer belt assembly LH.



b.

w/ Pretensioner and Force Limiter:

i.

Remove the nut and disconnect the shoulder anchor of the front seat outer belt assembly LH.

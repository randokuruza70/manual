SCOPE OF THE REPAIR WORK EXPLANATION


a.

This text explains the welding panel replacement instructions from the vehicle's body in white condition. Please refer to the electronic technical manual (repair manual) for explanations of the removal and reinstallation of the equipment parts up to the body in white condition and of the installation, inspection, adjustment and final inspection of equipment parts after replacing the welding panel.

HINT:
·

Illustrations of similar vehicle models are sometimes used. In these cases, minor details may be different from the actual vehicle.

·

About a symmetrical part, only one side is described.




b.

When repairing, do not cut and join areas that are not shown in this manual.

NOTICE:

Only work on the specified contents to maintain body strength.



c.

The following procedures are omitted from this manual. However, these procedures must be performed.

i.

Use a jack or lift to perform operations.


ii.

Clean all removed parts.


iii.

Perform a visual check before and after performing any work.
